# Central Park Watcher's Digest: Fri Jul 3 – Thu Jul 9

Hi park watchers,

A holiday week defined by weather extremes on both ends: a dangerous heat wave crests Friday and the Fourth, then a wet, stormy cool-down that runs most of the following week. The calendar itself is quiet — but the operational picture is busy, and the heat is now visibly reshaping programming.

## Weather this week

100° today and 96° on the Fourth constitute the summer's second serious heat event, before a sharp break Sunday into a cooler, thunderstorm-heavy stretch (upper 70s–low 80s) through Wednesday. The pattern to watch: this is the first time this season heat has forced a cancellation inside the park.

## The heat is cancelling programming

The headline operational note: **SummerStage cancelled Friday's Odd Mob show at Rumsey Playfield outright, rescheduling it to 2027, citing the extreme-heat threat.** That's the first weather-driven cancellation of the season and a marker worth logging — City Parks Foundation pulling a booked show, not just advising caution. Watch whether the Wednesday (Spoon) and Thursday (Shaggy) free shows hold as the pattern cools.

## Closures & maintenance across the park

- **Bethesda Terrace & Fountain — closed all week** (daily, through the window). A marquee landmark offline; worth tracking how long it runs.
- **East Meadow — closed Sat Jul 4 & Sun Jul 5**, then a separate maintenance closure Thu Jul 9.
- **The Pond lawn — maintenance, Mon Jul 6.**
- **Bowling Greens — maintenance, Tue Jul 7.**

## FIFA World Cup footprint at the SW corner

**FIFA World Cup bus operations stage at Columbus Circle from 6 AM on Sunday Jul 5**, congesting Merchants' Gate and the 59th Street entrance — part of the tournament's summer-long footprint on the park's southwest edge. The Final hits the region July 19; expect this corner's activation to intensify.

## New this season: Gottesman Pool free swim

The **Gottesman Pool at the Davis Center (Harlem Meer)** is now running free public swim — lap swim mornings, plus parent-and-tot and learn-to-swim sessions appearing Thursday. A notable new recurring fixture on the north-end calendar as the Davis Center comes fully online.

## Quiet on the annual-tradition front

**Naumburg's summer series continues Tuesday Jul 7 (Delirium Musicum, 7:30 PM, free)** — the season is mid-run, no openers or closers this week. No galas, parades, or festivals; the SummerStage free run (Wed/Thu) is the week's main draw.

## Quick recap

**First heat-driven cancellation of the season** — Odd Mob (Fri) pulled to 2027.

**Closures:** Bethesda Terrace (all week), East Meadow (Sat/Sun + Thu), The Pond (Mon), Bowling Greens (Tue).

**FIFA buses at Columbus Circle, Sun from 6 AM** — the SW-corner footprint continues toward the Jul 19 Final.

**New fixture:** Gottesman Pool free swim at the Davis Center, Harlem Meer.

Watching the week,
