# Central Park Sports This Week: Fri Jul 3 – Thu Jul 9

Hey sports fans,

Holiday-week league play is thinner than usual and the heat will thin it further, but the diamonds are busy once the weather turns. A few field closures to know about, plus a new one worth flagging: **free swim is open at the Gottesman Pool** at the Davis Center.

## Weather this week

100° today and 96° on the Fourth make midday play a real heat risk — expect softball forfeits and short benches Friday–Saturday — before it cools into a wet, storm-interrupted stretch Monday through Wednesday. Morning and early-evening slots are your friend all week; rain, not heat, is what shortens games after Sunday.

## Softball & youth ball — the daily roll-up

The permit diamonds at Heckscher, North Meadow, and the Great Lawn edges carry the usual summer softball and t-ball load every day this week — a few dozen games across the weekend, plus scattered soccer and kickball. Nothing tournament-scale on the calendar; the heat Friday–Saturday and storms Monday–Tuesday will do more to the schedule than any permit conflict. The **East 102nd Street Clay Oval** has a July session opening Thursday (Jul 9).

## Field closures & maintenance

- **East Meadow — closed Sat Jul 4 & Sun Jul 5**, then a maintenance closure again Thu Jul 9. Plan pickup elsewhere.
- **The Pond lawn — maintenance, Mon Jul 6.**
- **Bowling Greens — maintenance, Tue Jul 7** (the lawn-bowling greens offline for the day).

## Model Yacht Racing — Sat Jul 4, 9 AM, Conservatory Water

The model-yacht fleet races at **Kerbs Boathouse on Conservatory Water at 9 AM on the Fourth** — a classic, low-key holiday-morning spectator sport before the heat peaks.

## New: free swim at Gottesman Pool (Davis Center)

The **Gottesman Pool at the new Davis Center (Harlem Meer) is running free lap swim at 7 AM** most mornings this week, with parent-and-tot and learn-to-swim sessions added Thursday. In a 100° week, the best "sport" in the park might be an early lap swim — first come, first served.

## Quick recap

**Daily — summer softball/t-ball** at Heckscher, North Meadow, Great Lawn; heat and storms thin the schedule more than permits do.

**Sat Jul 4, 9 AM — Model Yacht Racing** at Conservatory Water.

**Field closures:** East Meadow (Sat/Sun + Thu), The Pond lawn (Mon), Bowling Greens (Tue).

**New — free lap swim at Gottesman Pool**, 7 AM, Davis Center at Harlem Meer.

Play it early,
