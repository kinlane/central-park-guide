# Central Park Stage: a quiet theater week, and where to find live performance

Hi theater fans,

A quiet one on the drama front: no Shakespeare in the Park dates, no Delacorte, and no outdoor-theater runs on the calendar this week — the holiday and a dangerous heat wave have kept the boards dark. But there's live performance to be had if you widen the lens, and it lands in the cooler back half of the week.

## Weather this week

100° today and 96° on the Fourth are simply too hot for outdoor staging — which is part of why the week is dark — before a cooler, showery stretch settles in from Sunday (upper 70s–low 80s). The performances worth your evening are Tuesday and Wednesday, when it's cool enough to sit out comfortably; keep an eye on the storm chance but neither venue scrubs easily.

## Tue Jul 7 — Delirium Musicum at Naumburg Bandshell, 7:30 PM (free)

For the performance-minded, the **Naumburg Orchestral Concerts series** brings Delirium Musicum to the Bandshell on the Mall at 7:30 PM, free. It's a concert, not a play, but it's the closest thing to staged evening spectacle in the park this week — the historic Bandshell setting, a program-driven ensemble, and an audience on blankets down the Literary Walk. A fine substitute for a dark theater night.

## Wed Jul 8 & Thu Jul 9 — SummerStage returns to Rumsey (free)

If you want a crowd and a stage, the free **SummerStage** nights are back: Spoon and company Wednesday at 6 PM, Shaggy's big dancehall revue Thursday at 7 PM, both at Rumsey Playfield. Music rather than theater — but staged, produced, and free, and a good outlet in a week with no scripted work.

## Looking ahead

Shakespeare in the Park's 2026 season isn't reflected in our calendar yet — when the Public Theater posts Delacorte dates we'll lead with them. For now, the marionette shows at the Swedish Cottage remain the reliable year-round stage bet; nothing new surfaced in the data this week, but it's worth a direct check if you're planning a kids' matinee.

## Quick recap

**No outdoor theater or Shakespeare this week** — the stage is dark for the holiday and the heat.

**Tue 7:30 PM — Delirium Musicum at Naumburg Bandshell (free):** the week's best staged-evening substitute.

**Wed 6 PM & Thu 7 PM — free SummerStage at Rumsey:** produced, live, and free.

Back to the boards soon,
The Central Park Guide
