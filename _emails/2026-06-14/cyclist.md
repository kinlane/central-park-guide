# Central Park Cycling Heads-Up — Mon Jun 15 – Sun Jun 21

Hey cyclists,

Good news, and it's the rare kind: **the loop is yours all week.** No drive closures, no CRCA crit, no charity ride, no parade, nothing staging on East or West Drive that touches your lap. After last week's pile-up — CRCA crit, the Central Park 5K on the north cross drive, the Puerto Rican Day Parade swallowing the east side — this is the first genuinely wide-open week in a while. Clean drives every single day, Monday through Sunday.

So the only thing shaping your week is the weather, and the weather hands you a gift early. Monday through Wednesday is cool, dry, and low-humidity — the best riding of the week by a wide margin. Bank your fast laps and long rides there. Thursday turns hot and stormy, Friday warms up for Juneteenth, and the solstice weekend goes hot and sunny with a gusty west wind. More on all of it below.

## Weather this week

Comfortable upper-70s and low humidity Monday through Wednesday, then heat and thunderstorms arrive Thursday before a warm Juneteenth Friday and a hot, sunny solstice weekend. Bank your fast laps and long efforts Monday–Wednesday while it's cool and still, and on the hot, windy weekend ride early and hydrate.

## Mon 6/15 — Clean loop, 78°F, AM showers then sunny

The week opens with the loop fully open and no conflicts anywhere on the drives. A few AM showers clear to sunny at 78°F with low humidity — once the roads dry, this is prime riding. Get out mid-morning onward and put in the miles; nothing's in your way today.

## Tue 6/16 — Clean loop, 79°F, sunny and nearly still

The standout day. 79°F, sunny, and a light, nearly still wind — that's a PR setup if you've been chasing a fast lap. No closures, no events on the drives, low humidity. If you want one clean, quick session this week, this is the morning to take it.

## Wed 6/17 — Clean loop, 79°F, PM t-storm chance

Last of the cool, low-humidity stretch at 79°F, with a thunderstorm chance building in the afternoon. Ride the morning or midday to stay ahead of the cells — that's your last comfortable long-ride window before the heat moves in. Drives stay open and clear all day.

## Thu 6/18 — Clean loop, 87°F, thunderstorms likely

The turn. 87°F with thunderstorms likely and a south wind 7–16 mph. The loop is still open, but the weather isn't — get a dawn ride in before the cells build, or take the rest day. Roads will be slick if you do head out.

One thing to flag at the SW corner: the **Columbus Circle Food Market** runs 9:30 AM at Merchants' Gate. It's not a closure, but expect walkers spilling toward the merge at Columbus Circle and the southwest entry. Stay heads-up through that pinch on your approach.

## Fri 6/19 — Clean loop, 84°F, Juneteenth foot traffic on West Drive

Juneteenth, 84°F, chance of rain, west wind 10–15 mph. The loop is open — but **Juneteenth in Seneca Village** gathers at 1 PM on West Drive between W 81st and W 86th. It's not a closure, just midday foot traffic in that stretch. Ride the morning to skip it entirely, or ease through and give the other side of the road room as you pass.

## Sat 6/20 — Clean loop, 85°F, sunny and breezy

Solstice weekend kicks off hot and sunny at 85°F with a west wind 10–15 mph — that's a headwind on the west-side climbs and a push down the east, plus gusts on the back stretch. The loop is clean and open. Ride early to beat the heat, and watch the **Columbus Circle Food Market** again at 9:30 AM (Merchants' Gate) — walkers spilling near the SW merge.

## Sun 6/21 — Clean loop, 86°F, Father's Day and the solstice

The longest day of the year, sunny and hot at 86°F, gusty west wind 10–15 on the back stretch. Loop's wide open — no closures, no events touching the drives. Get your laps in early before the heat peaks, hydrate hard, and enjoy a rare clean Sunday on a quiet loop.

## Quick recap

- **All week: the loop is yours** — no closures, no crit, no charity ride, no parade. First clean week in a while; drives open Mon–Sun.
- **Mon–Wed: the riding window** — upper-70s, low humidity, no conflicts. Tuesday's nearly still wind is your fast-lap day.
- **Thu 6/18: 87°F, thunderstorms likely** — dawn ride or skip. Columbus Circle Food Market (9:30 AM, Merchants' Gate) crowds the SW merge.
- **Fri 6/19: Juneteenth foot traffic** on West Drive W 81st–86th from 1 PM — not a closure; ride the morning or pass on the far side.
- **Sat–Sun: hot and breezy** — 85–86°F, gusty W wind 10–15 on the back stretch. Ride early, hydrate. Saturday's food market crowds the SW corner at 9:30 AM.

Tailwinds,
— Central Park Guide
