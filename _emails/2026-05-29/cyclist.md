# Central Park Guide for Cyclists — Fri May 29 – Thu Jun 4

Hey cyclists — this is a CRCA-Saturday plus clean-loop week, and the only friction on your radar is Friday's Corporate Challenge teardown lingering on West Drive through midday.

## Weather this week

Cool, breezy weekend with a pristine sunny Sunday at 77°F, then mild and unsettled Mon–Tue with PM thunderstorm risk both days, warming to a sunny 83°F Thursday with a heat wave waiting on the other side of the weekend. Sunday is the unambiguous long-ride day of the week — bank the miles before the Monday and Tuesday afternoon storms force you into morning-only windows.

## Fri May 29 — Corporate Challenge teardown on West Drive

The race itself ran Wed/Thu nights, but the J.P. Morgan Corporate Challenge footprint at South Dead Road B and Bandshell Plaza is still coming down this morning. Expect equipment movement — trucks, fencing, barricades — on West Drive roughly from 66th to 72nd through midday. The loop is rideable, but don't treat it as a clean spin: ride defensively through that stretch, or push your ride to after lunch when crews should be clear. Evening is wide open, 56°F and breezy from the west.

## Sat May 30 — CRCA Central Park Racing Series, 4:00–8:30 AM on East Drive

This is the week's defining cyclist event and it owns East Drive for the morning. Central Park Racing Association crit format means racers-only on East Drive during the racing window — if you're not pinned on, your three options are: (1) register and join the series, (2) ride the loop before 4 AM, or (3) wait until ~9 AM when the field has packed up and East Drive is fully released. There's no useful middle ground here; rolling onto East Drive at 6 AM "to see how it feels" puts you in a peloton at race pace. West Drive is unaffected, but the natural counter-clockwise loop is broken until the racers clear. Day itself is gorgeous: 66°F, mostly sunny, breezy out of the north 12–15 — a beautiful day to race or to spectate from the Tavern bend.

Also worth knowing: the YAI Central Park Challenge stages at Bandshell Plaza 8 AM–noon. Not a road race, but spectator drift onto West Drive between 72nd and Bethesda in that window — give the south end an extra beat of patience if you're rolling through after the crit wraps.

## Sun May 31 — The long-ride day

No Affects Loop events, no closures, no permits worth dodging — and 77°F, sunny, light wind. This is your day. Bank the long miles, run repeats, do whatever you've been saving the legs for. The folkdancers are out at King Jagiełło Plaza (east side near Turtle Pond) and the Skate Circle is rolling at Dead Road North 2–7 PM, but neither touches the loop. If you've only got one ride this week, make it Sunday morning.

## Mon Jun 1 — Clean loop, thunderstorm risk after lunch

No loop-affecting events. 76°F and mostly sunny early, but PM chance of showers and thunderstorms — ride before noon if you can. The CP Sports Summer Program kicks off at South Dead Road A and B (8:30 AM–noon and 12:30–4 PM) and will run weekday mornings and afternoons through the week; it's a small youth-sports footprint, not a loop closure, but you'll see more foot traffic crossing the south end of West Drive around the program transitions.

## Tue Jun 2 — Clean loop, PM rain risk again

Same pattern as Monday: 74°F, mostly sunny morning, PM chance of rain. No permits affecting the drives. Morning ride window is the play. If you missed Sunday, this is the next-best long-ride option provided you're rolling by 11.

## Wed Jun 3 — Clean loop

75°F, slight chance of rain — the cleanest midweek loop day. The Wild West Playground Lawn (west side mid-park) is closed for maintenance until 2 PM, but it's off the loop entirely; mentioning it only because you'll see crews on the path. No other footprint. Ride anytime.

## Thu Jun 4 — Hot but clean

83°F and sunny — the warmest day of the week and a preview of the heat wave that hits Friday (88°F forecast). No events on the loop. Early morning is the smart window before the heat builds and humidity follows; afternoon will feel like the season has turned. The East 106th St South Mount is closed for maintenance until 2 PM but that's far north-east and not in your path.

## Quick recap

- **Fri 5/29:** **Corporate Challenge teardown** on West Drive ~66th–72nd through midday. Loop usable but expect equipment movement; ride after lunch for a clean spin.
- **Sat 5/30:** **CRCA crit on East Drive 4:00–8:30 AM.** Racers only — join the race, ride before 4 AM, or wait until ~9. West Drive open throughout.
- **Sun 5/31:** **Clean loop, 77°F sunny.** The long-ride day of the week. No permits anywhere.
- **Mon 6/1:** Clean loop; ride before noon to beat PM thunderstorms.
- **Tue 6/2:** Clean loop; same morning-window play as Monday.
- **Wed 6/3:** Clean loop, slight rain chance — ride anytime.
- **Thu 6/4:** Clean loop, 83°F. Early morning before the heat builds; heat wave starts Friday.

Tailwinds,
— Central Park Guide
