# Central Park Family Plans — Mon Jun 22 – Sun Jun 28

*Your Sunday week-ahead look, covering Mon Jun 22 through Sun Jun 28.*

Hi families — the big kid-friendly stuff lands on the weekend, and it's a good one: model sailboats on Conservatory Water, storytelling at the Hans Christian Andersen statue, and a chess lecture with a real grandmaster. There's also **free World Cup soccer for kids at Tavern on the Green most days** while the tournament's on. A wet start makes Mon–Tue better for indoor plans; the weather's lovely by midweek.

## Weather this week

Storms Monday (76°) and showers Tuesday (79°) clear to sunny, dry days Wednesday (83°) and Thursday (84°), then it gets hot — 82° Friday and a humid 86° Saturday, both with afternoon storm chances. Save outdoor outings for the Wed–Thu sweet spot or Saturday morning, and pack water and shade for the weekend heat.

## Mon 6/22 – Tue 6/23 — Rainy days, plan indoors

Showers and storms Monday (76°), more showers Tuesday (79°). Not much scheduled for kids, and the lawns will be soggy — good days for the carousel, the Swedish Cottage, or saving energy for the weekend. The **FIFA Arena free soccer at Tavern on the Green** runs daily if there's a dry window.

## Wed 6/24 — Sunshine returns + a kids' event at Pilgrim Hill

83° and sunny — back outside. **Rothy's hosts a kids' event at Pilgrim Hill in the late afternoon (around 4:30–7 PM)**, near the Conservatory Water side — easy to pair with the model-boat pond and the Alice in Wonderland statue.

## Thu 6/25 — Fair weather, snacks at Columbus Circle

Mostly sunny, 84°. The **Columbus Circle Food Market opens at Merchants' Gate (9:30 AM–9 PM)** — easy snacks if you enter at the southwest corner, though it's busy there with crowds and buses. A fine playground-and-picnic day, plus free FIFA Arena soccer at Tavern on the Green.

## Sat 6/27 — The big family day

Hot at 86° with an afternoon storm chance, so go early. The classics are all out: **model yacht racing on Conservatory Water at Kerbs Boathouse (9 AM–3 PM)**, **storytelling at the Hans Christian Andersen statue (10 AM–1 PM)** for younger kids, and a **chess lecture and simul with Grandmaster Irina Krush at the Chess & Checkers House (11 AM)** — free, ages 6 and up, great for older kids (register ahead; a few walk-up spots). Note there's a morning footrace on the park drives (~7:30–10 AM), so plan around the loop early on.

## Sun 6/28 — Folk dancing and skating; heads-up on the big concert

Summer warmth, possible afternoon storm. **International folk dancers at King Jagiello Plaza (from 11 AM)** by Turtle Pond are fun to watch, and the **Skate Circle spins up at Dead Road in the afternoon.** One note for parents: **Dreamland: Pride at SummerStage / Rumsey (from 3 PM)** is a big ticketed evening dance party — great energy, but an after-dark grown-ups' show, so keep younger-kid time to the morning and the north end.

## Quick recap

- **Sat 6/27 is the day:** model yachts (9 AM–3 PM), Andersen-statue storytelling (10 AM–1 PM), and **chess with GM Irina Krush (11 AM, register ahead).** Go early — it hits 86°.
- **Daily:** free FIFA Arena World Cup soccer for kids at Tavern on the Green.
- **Wed 6/24, ~4:30 PM:** Rothy's kids' event at Pilgrim Hill, near the boat pond.
- **Sun 6/28, 11 AM:** folk dancing at King Jagiello Plaza + afternoon Skate Circle at Dead Road.
- **Mon–Tue rainy; heat + storms Sat–Sun afternoons** — plan mornings, pack water and shade.

See you in the park,
— Central Park Guide
