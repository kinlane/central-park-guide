# Central Park Sports — Mon Jun 22 – Sun Jun 28

*Your Sunday week-ahead look, covering Mon Jun 22 through Sun Jun 28.*

Hey sports fans — a heavy league week on the diamonds, two NYRR races back-to-back on the weekend, and free World Cup soccer most days. **The Front Runners NY Pride Run 4M is Saturday (8 AM) and the Achilles Hope & Possibility 4M is Sunday (8:15 AM)** — both full fields on the park drives. The rain Mon–Tue will scramble early-week games; the weekend is the full slate.

## Weather this week

Storms Monday (76°) and showers Tuesday (79°) — expect field closures and rainouts early — then a dry, sunny Wednesday (83°) and Thursday (84°), and a hot, unsettled weekend (82° Friday, 86° Saturday with afternoon storm chances). Grass fields at Heckscher, North Meadow, and the Great Lawn stay soft into midweek; weekend heat is the main factor for the races and Saturday's outdoor meet.

## Mon 6/22 – Tue 6/23 — Wet fields, expect rainouts

Softball, baseball, kickball, and soccer-training permits fill **North Meadow, Heckscher, and the Great Lawn**, but storms Monday and showers Tuesday will close soft fields and push games. Check before heading out. The **FIFA Arena free soccer at Tavern on the Green** runs daily through the World Cup if you want a kickabout in a dry window.

## Wed 6/24 — Fields dry out, full league slate

83° and sunny. The diamonds come back — a normal busy weekday of **adult softball and youth baseball at North Meadow and Heckscher.** A 7 PM "Media Challenge" runs on the Lower Loop in the evening if you're around the south end.

## Thu 6/25 — Clear, normal weeknight games

Mostly sunny, 84°. Standard league play across the fields — softball, kickball, youth training. Good conditions front to back, plus free FIFA Arena soccer at Tavern on the Green.

## Fri 6/26 — Warm, unsettled, watch the cells

82° with storm chances. Games are on, but watch the radar — passing thunderstorms could pause or pull matches off soft fields late.

## Sat 6/27, 8 AM — Front Runners Pride Run 4M, plus a loaded field day

Hot at 86° with an afternoon storm chance — hydrate. **The Front Runners NY LGBT Pride Run 4M starts at 8 AM** (Race-Day Central at the Naumburg Bandshell), putting a full NYRR field on the drives ~7:30–10 AM. Then the slate is full: the **MSA Outdoor Meet by the tennis courts (9 AM–2 PM)**, **model yacht racing on Conservatory Water (9 AM–3 PM)**, the **Skate Circle at Dead Road North (2–7 PM)**, and heavy adult/youth softball, baseball, and kickball across North Meadow and Heckscher. **East Meadow is closed all weekend.**

## Sun 6/28, 8:15 AM — Achilles Hope & Possibility 4M, plus the diamonds

Summer warmth, possible afternoon storm. **The Achilles Hope & Possibility 4M starts at 8:15 AM** — one of the largest inclusive races in the country, with handcyclists, wounded veterans, and athletes of all abilities on the drives ~7:30–10:30 AM. Then a full slate of **adult softball, baseball, and kickball** across the fields, plus the **Skate Circle (2–7 PM).** East Meadow stays closed; Dreamland Pride fills Rumsey in the afternoon.

## Quick recap

- **Sat 6/27, 8 AM:** Front Runners NY Pride Run 4M on the drives (field ~7:30–10 AM).
- **Sun 6/28, 8:15 AM:** Achilles Hope & Possibility 4M — inclusive race, handcycles, field ~7:30–10:30 AM.
- **Mon–Tue: rainouts likely; Wed–Thu: full, dry league slate** at North Meadow, Heckscher, Great Lawn.
- **Sat also:** MSA Outdoor Meet (9 AM–2 PM), model yacht racing (9 AM–3 PM), Skate Circle (2–7 PM); **Sun:** diamonds + Skate Circle.
- **Daily:** free FIFA Arena World Cup soccer at Tavern on the Green. **East Meadow closed all weekend.**
