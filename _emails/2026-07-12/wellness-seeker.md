Hi wellness seekers — a hot, quiet week is an invitation to slow down and go early. With no crowds-drawing races and the heat pushing everyone else to the margins of the day, the dawn hours in the park's contemplative corners are yours. Lap swim at the new Gottesman Pool and a Friday prayer service by the water are the week's anchored moments.

## Weather this week
A heat wave peaks at 98°F on Wed 7/15 before easing into the low 90s and breaking with Saturday's showers. Dawn is the practice window all week — cool air, soft light, and near-empty paths before 7:30 — and on Wednesday's peak, keep everything to first light or the shade; the Ramble, North Woods, Hallett, and Summit Rock hold the coolest, quietest air.

## Mon–Wed — Dawn practice, shaded stillness
No scheduled classes, so let the park be the practice. Sunrise at the Reservoir or on the Mall, then retreat into shade as the heat climbs — Hallett Nature Sanctuary (near the Pond), the Ramble, and Summit Rock are the park's quietest, coolest refuges. On Wednesday's 98°F, do your morning practice and let the rest of the day go.

## All week — Gottesman Pool lap swim, 7 AM
The new **Gottesman Pool at the Davis Center** (Harlem Meer) offers lap swim daily from 7 AM — the single best way to move and cool down in a heat wave. Early lanes are calmest.

## Thu Jul 16 — Solitude on the west side
At 91°F, the Great Lawn fills for an evening concert (7 PM), which means the **west side and north woods stay quiet** — head for the Ramble, the Pool, or the Conservatory Garden (opens 8 AM) for a contemplative morning while the crowd energy gathers elsewhere later.

## Fri Jul 17 — Shoresh Prayer Service at the Pool, 5:30 PM
A rare spiritual gathering on the calendar: a **prayer service at the Pool** (the park's serene northwest water garden) at 5:30 PM. Even if it isn't your tradition, the setting — willows, still water, cooling evening — makes it a gentle place to end the week's practice.

## Sat–Sun — Cooler air, the garden
As the heat breaks Saturday, the **Conservatory Garden** (formal, quiet, 8 AM open) is at its best in cooler air — a slow morning walk through the three gardens before possible afternoon showers. Sunday stays cool and unsettled, ideal for a soft, unhurried ramble through the damp, green North Woods.

## Finding solitude this week
With league play concentrated on the east and mid-park fields (North Meadow, Great Lawn, Heckscher), the **western woodlands and the far north end stay quietest**. When in doubt this week, go northwest and go early.

## Quick recap
- **Dawn is the week** — practice before 7:30; shade (Ramble, Hallett, Summit Rock, North Woods) by day.
- **Gottesman Pool lap swim** daily from 7 AM — your heat-wave reset.
- **Shoresh Prayer Service** at the Pool, Fri 5:30 PM — the week's spiritual anchor.
- **Wed 7/15 peaks at 98°F** — first light or shade only; quiet returns with Saturday's cooler air.
