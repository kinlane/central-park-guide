# Central Park Running Heads-Up: Week of May 10–16

Hey runners,

After last week's Bike Tour craziness, this one's much calmer. Two cycling events take over the 102nd Street area on different days — plan around those and you're fine. Here's the week.

## Today (Sun May 10) — Race of Hope + Skate Circle

**Race of Hope NYC** runs on the **102nd Street Cross Drive and Transverse, 8:30 AM to noon**. This is a small race compared to last week, but it'll close the 102nd Street connector — runners using the full outer loop will need to take the 96th Street Transverse south or detour through the bridle path.

**Skate Circle** returns at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM. West Drive near 72nd gets congested with skaters and crowds in the afternoon. Run mid-morning or stick to the upper park after lunch.

Also: it's Mother's Day, so expect the south end (Bethesda, Bow Bridge, Cop Cot, the Mall) to be packed with families and ceremonies from late morning on. Early-AM runs win today.

## Wed May 13 — two things to know

- **Pedals of Honor 2026** on the **102nd Street Cross Drive, 9 AM–3 PM.** A cycling event closing the same connector again. Same workaround as Sunday: use the 96th Street Transverse.
- An event setup at **North Reservoir Landscape A, 2–5 PM.** Same as last week — won't close anything, but expect foot traffic on the Reservoir's north end.

## Sat May 16 morning — CRCA racing on East Drive

**CRCA Central Park Racing Series, 4 AM–8:30 AM, East Drive.** This is the Saturday-morning cycling race series. Early morning runners on the east side will share the road with race traffic. Options:
- Run **before 4 AM** (good luck)
- Run **after 8:30 AM**
- Use the **Reservoir loop** or **bridle path** — both unaffected

## Quick recap

**Sun 5/10, 8:30 AM–noon — Race of Hope on 102nd**
Use 96th St Transverse to cross instead.

**Sun 5/10, 2–7 PM — Skate Circle at 72nd**
Stick to upper park or run mornings.

**Wed 5/13, 9 AM–3 PM — Pedals of Honor on 102nd**
Same workaround as Sunday.

**Wed 5/13, 2–5 PM — Reservoir north side**
Brief foot traffic, no closures.

**Sat 5/16, 4–8:30 AM — CRCA race on East Drive**
Run the Reservoir or bridle path, or wait til 8:30.

Best windows this week: **weekday mornings (esp. Tue and Thu)**, and **late mornings Sun/Sat** after the events clear. The Reservoir loop stays clean almost all week.

See you on the loop,
The Central Park Guide
