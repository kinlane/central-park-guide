# Central Park Walking Plans: Week of May 10–16

Hi walkers,

Easier week than last week. A few specific things to dodge, but lots of open paths and the park is full of late-spring color. Here's the week.

## Today (Sun May 10) — Mother's Day, head north

It's the park's biggest day for family celebrations after Easter. The lower park around Bethesda, Bow Bridge, Cherry Hill, Wagner Cove, Cop Cot, and Ladies' Pavilion is **wall-to-wall ceremonies all day**. Beautiful to walk through, but slow.

**For a quieter Mother's Day walk:**
- **Conservatory Garden** (105th & 5th) — formal, walled, calm even today
- **The Ramble + North Woods** — quiet, late warbler migration
- **Reservoir loop** at sunset

Also: **Race of Hope NYC** runs on the 102nd Street Cross Drive 8:30 AM–noon, and **Skate Circle** activates Dead Road North 2–7 PM. Neither is a big walking obstacle, but the area around 72nd West gets busy in the afternoon.

## Mon–Tue (May 11–12) — calm

Two of the quietest days of the week. Pick anywhere. **Bowling Green Lawn is closed Tue 5/12 all day** if that's a regular spot for you.

## Wed May 13 — small things to know

- **Pedals of Honor 2026** charity ride on the 102nd Street Cross Drive, 9 AM–3 PM. Affects the connector, not the main loop. Easy to walk around.
- **Bill Popp and the Tapes** at Bandshell, 5–7 PM — outdoor live music, lovely walk-by.
- Small event setup at North Reservoir Landscape A, 2–5 PM — won't block paths, but expect setup activity if you walk the Reservoir north side.

## Sat May 16 — busy morning at the Reservoir, busy afternoon at Levin

- **CRCA Central Park Racing Series**, East Drive, 4–8:30 AM. Early-morning walkers on East Drive should expect cyclist race traffic.
- **Model Yacht Racing** at Kerbs Boathouse, 9 AM–3 PM — fun to walk past, kids love it.
- **Sacred Sites Open House** at Mineral Springs, 10 AM–3 PM — guided history of park structures.
- **PS 77 Spring Festival** at Lawn Outside Levin Playground, 2–5 PM — crowded in that pocket of the park.
- **East Meadow closed** all day.

For Saturday, plan your walk **mid-morning** (after CRCA, before Levin festival ramps up).

## Best walks this week

- **Conservatory Garden → Harlem Meer → North Woods**: the upper park loop, 90 minutes, peaceful most days
- **Mall → Bethesda → Bow Bridge → Bethesda**: best done **Tue or Thu midday** this week, when ceremonies thin out
- **Reservoir at sunset**: ~1.6 miles, water + skyline, no events any evening this week

## Quick recap

**Today — head north**
Mother's Day saturates the south end.

**Mon–Tue — calmest days for any walk**

**Wed 5/13 afternoon — Bandshell music + Pedals of Honor wrapping up**

**Sat 5/16 — best window is mid-morning**
After cyclists (8:30 AM), before Levin festival (2 PM).

Enjoy the late-spring stretch,
The Central Park Guide
