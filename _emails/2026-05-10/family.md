# Central Park Family Plans: Week of May 10–16

Hi families,

Happy Mother's Day. The park's a great place to be today, and the week ahead has a couple of standout family events. Here's what to plan around.

## Today (Sun May 10) — Mother's Day in the park

It's a busy day for celebrations. A few low-key picks for moms and kids:

- **Central Park International Folkdancers** at King Jagiallo Plaza (75th & CPE), 11 AM–7 PM. Costumes, live music, kid-friendly.
- **Skate Circle** at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM — fun to watch, music playing, energetic.
- **Bow Bridge, Bethesda Terrace, Cherry Hill** all have small ceremonies and celebrations today — lovely to walk past, but expect crowds.

Best Mother's Day move with kids: **early walk to Conservatory Garden** (105th & 5th, much calmer than the south end), or **early picnic on the Great Lawn**.

## Closures to know

- **East Meadow** is closed today (5/10) and again Sat 5/16 for events
- **Bowling Green Lawn** closed all day Tue 5/12, and morning of Fri 5/15
- **East 72nd Street Playground Lawns** — was closed Mon–Wed last week, should be open this week

## Best family day of the week — Saturday May 16

Three family-friendly things in one day:

- **Model Yacht Racing** at Kerbs Boathouse, 9 AM–3 PM. Free, drop-in, remote-controlled sailboats on Conservatory Water — kids love this.
- **Sacred Sites Open House Day 1** at Mineral Springs, 10 AM–3 PM. NYC-wide program; the park location features tours of historic park structures. Older kids get something out of this.
- **PS 77 PTA Spring Festival** at Lawn Outside Levin Playground (Section A), 2–5 PM. Public school spring festival — typically open to the community, games and food.

## Theater note

**Julius Caesar** is running daily at The Pool (100th–103rd, west side), 10 AM–9 PM all week. Check the company's actual show times before heading over — kids 10+ may enjoy it, especially the outdoor staging.

## Quick recap

**Sun 5/10 — Mother's Day, head north for calm**
Conservatory Garden, Great Lawn, North Woods.

**Tue 5/12 — Bowling Green Lawn closed**

**Sat 5/16 — best family day**
Model boats (9–3), Sacred Sites tours (10–3), PS 77 festival (2–5).

Have a good week with the kids,
The Central Park Guide
