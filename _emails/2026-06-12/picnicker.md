# Central Park Picnic Intel — Sat Jun 13 – Fri Jun 19

Picnickers — East Meadow is closed Saturday and Sunday, Cherry Hill is closed through Sunday, and Tuesday sees Cedar Hill and Pilgrim Hill maintenance closures. Monday is the best open-lawn day of the week at 82°F. Friday's Juneteenth commemoration at Seneca Village makes the west-side lawns worth a thoughtful plan.

## Weather this week

88°F Saturday, 84°F Sunday, 82°F Monday, 85°F Tuesday, 89°F Wednesday, 91°F Thursday, 87°F Friday. Blankets down before 11 AM on any day above 88°F; shade is non-negotiable by early afternoon.

## Sat 6/13 — East Meadow closed, cherry tree lawn closed; work with Sheep Meadow or the Great Lawn

East Meadow and Cherry Hill are both closed. **Sheep Meadow** (west side, 66th–69th Street) is wide open; arrive by 10 AM to secure a shaded edge spot. The **Great Lawn** is active with Little League games across the diamonds — the lawn itself is accessible but the east-side diamonds will have sports traffic. Bowling Green has the Primavera Pairs event (9 AM–3 PM). Columbus Circle Food Market is operating for provisions.

The Martinez Brothers SummerStage show (Rumsey Playfield, 6–10 PM, ticketed) draws crowds to the east 72nd Street area from 4 PM — avoid that stretch for your blanket setup.

## Sun 6/14 — East Meadow still closed, Cherry Hill still closed

Same situation as Saturday. Sheep Meadow is the clean choice. The Great Lawn south-west lawn is clear most of the day — BBBC Book Exchange is there from 5–7 PM, which is more ambient cultural noise than a conflict. International Folk Dancers at King Jagiello Plaza (11 AM–9 PM) don't affect the lawns.

## Mon 6/15 — Best day (82°F): Cherry Hill reopens, East Meadow back open

Both Cherry Hill and East Meadow reopen today. This is the week's best picnic day — 82°F, both key destinations accessible, no major event conflicts before evening. **Cherry Hill fountain lawn** is one of the park's nicest picnic spots and it will be fresh after maintenance. Bring everything; stake a spot by 10 AM.

## Tue 6/16 — Cedar Hill and Pilgrim Hill closed; Mineral Springs open

**Cedar Hill** (east side, 79th Street) is closed for maintenance, as is **Pilgrim Hill** (72nd Street area). **Mineral Springs Pavilion** (67th Street, west side) is unaffected — the north end of Sheep Meadow with a food kiosk and good shade. The 40-in-Focus Opening Reception at the Arsenal (6–8 PM) is nearby if you want a post-picnic indoor destination.

## Wed 6/17 — 89°F, morning picnic only

Wellness at the Park runs 9 AM–1 PM at the Great Lawn north side — low-key ambient backdrop. Sheep Meadow's shade is useful if you're out by 9 AM and pack up by noon. Julius Caesar at The Pool Lawns (5–10 PM) means foot traffic on northern paths from 4 PM — avoidable.

## Thu 6/18 — 91°F, Columbus Circle Food Market

91°F — not a picnic afternoon unless you're in deep shade. Columbus Circle Food Market is operating for provisions; pick up and carry to Sheep Meadow or Mineral Springs before 10 AM, then retreat.

## Fri 6/19 — Juneteenth in Seneca Village area (West Drive, 1–3 PM)

The area near West Drive between 82nd and 86th Street is the Juneteenth commemoration zone from 1–3 PM. Respectful distance if you're picnicking nearby; the nearby lawns between West Drive and the Reservoir in that corridor are open but will have foot traffic. Good alternative: **Dene Lawn** (east side, 68th Street area) is quiet and underused. Julius Caesar final night at The Pool (5–10 PM) makes the northern pool area lively in the evening.

## Quick recap

- **East Meadow and Cherry Hill:** Closed Sat–Sun, both reopen Mon 6/15.
- **Cedar Hill and Pilgrim Hill:** Closed Tue 6/16.
- **Mon 6/15 is the week** — 82°F, both key destinations open, clean calendar.
- **Sheep Meadow:** Available all week; best backup option when Cherry Hill is closed.
- **Mineral Springs Pavilion:** Good Tuesday fallback; food kiosk on-site.
