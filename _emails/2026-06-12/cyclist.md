# Central Park Cycling Heads-Up — Sat Jun 13 – Fri Jun 19

Hey cyclists — no race closures and no drive shutdowns this week. The heat is the only obstacle: 88–91°F from Wednesday through Thursday. Monday at 82°F is your open window; every other quality ride requires an early start.

## Weather this week

88°F Saturday, 84°F Sunday, 82°F Monday, 85°F Tuesday, 89°F Wednesday, 91°F Thursday, 87°F Friday. Manageable if you front-load; punishing if you don't.

## Sat 6/13 — Clean loop, 88°F, SummerStage setup from midday

No race, no drive closures. The Martinez Brothers SummerStage show at Rumsey Playfield runs 6–10 PM — setup occupies the Bandshell Plaza / Rumsey area from roughly midday, and the post-show pedestrian surge hits East Drive near 72nd Street by 10:30 PM. Ride early (before 10 AM) or after the show clears. East Meadow closed all day; Bowling Green Lawn running the Primavera Pairs event 9 AM–3 PM — no drive impact.

## Sun 6/14 — 84°F, clean loop

Second day of East Meadow closure; no drive events. International Folk Dancers at King Jagiello Plaza (11 AM–9 PM) are off the drives. BBBC Book Exchange at Great Lawn SW Lawn (5–7 PM) — no impact. Ride the full loop without obstacles any time before 4 PM.

## Mon 6/15 — Best day (82°F, clean loop)

82°F, sunny, nothing on the drives. This is the week. Long ride or intervals — do it Monday. The 40-in-Focus exhibit opens at the Arsenal at 9 AM; foot traffic at the Fifth Avenue entrance picks up by 10 AM but stays off the drives.

## Tue 6/16 — 85°F, drives clear

Clean loop. Bowling Greens, Cedar Hill, and Pilgrim Hill all closed for maintenance — zero drive impact. Julius Caesar at The Pool Lawns starts 5 PM; foot traffic increases near the 100th Street West Drive area after 4:30 PM. Evening opening reception for 40-in-Focus at the Arsenal (6–8 PM) draws foot traffic near the Fifth Avenue entrance — drives stay clear.

## Wed 6/17 — 89°F, drives open

SummerStage Festival operating at Rumsey Playfield. Wellness at the Park runs 9 AM–1 PM at the Great Lawn north side — no drive impact. Julius Caesar (5–10 PM) again at The Pool. Early ride only in 89°F heat.

## Thu 6/18 — 91°F, drives open

SummerStage at Rumsey Playfield again. Private Starlight Dinner at Summit Rock (6–9 PM) — trail permit, not on the loop. Pre-dawn riding only at 91°F.

## Fri 6/19 — 87°F, Juneteenth on West Drive 1–3 PM

**Juneteenth in Seneca Village** places a permit on West Drive near 82nd–86th Street from 1–3 PM. That stretch of West Drive has pedestrian presence and possible staging during the event — plan a morning ride and be off West Drive before noon. SummerStage Festival and Julius Caesar both operating in the evening.

## Quick recap

- **No drive closures or races all week.**
- **Mon 6/15 is the week** — 82°F, full loop, no conflicts.
- **Sat 6/13:** SummerStage crowd near 72nd St from 4 PM; ride before 10 AM.
- **Fri 6/19:** Juneteenth on West Drive 1–3 PM — morning ride only.
- **Thu 6/18 peaks at 91°F** — pre-dawn or skip.
