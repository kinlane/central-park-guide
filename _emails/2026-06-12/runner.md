No loop events during running hours. The Martinez Brothers SummerStage show at Rumsey Playfield starts at 6 PM — crowds arrive from 4 PM onward, filling the paths around the 72nd Street Cross Drive and near Bandshell Plaza. Drives stay clear. Run in the morning. East Meadow is closed all day.
