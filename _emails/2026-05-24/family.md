# Central Park Guide for Families — Mon May 25 – Sun May 31

Hi families,

**Memorial Day washes out**, then opens into a beautiful week with a packed free-Saturday lineup. **Cast for Kids at The Pond, Model Yacht Racing at Kerbs Boathouse, *Griffin's Tale* at Pilgrim Hill, *That Guitar Man* at Hernshead, and the Skate Circle** all hit on Saturday May 30 — pick two and you've got a great day. **Julius Caesar runs daily at The Pool 10 AM** for the older kids; younger ones get the Skate Circle on weekends.

## Weather this week

Stormy Memorial Day clearing into **a beautiful, dry Tue–Sun**, with Wednesday peaking at 83°F (the heat day) and the rest of the week comfortably in the upper 70s. **Bring water bottles and the sun hat starting Tuesday**; if you're picnicking, Friday afternoon and Saturday late-morning are the sweet spots.

## Mon May 25 (Memorial Day) — indoor day

Thunderstorms, 74°F. **The playground plans are off** — Levin, Heckscher, and Diana Ross are going to be wet and unsupervised. If you want an indoor pivot inside the park, **the Arsenal Gallery is showing *Sarah Yuster: Outside Voices*** (free, 9 AM, third floor of the Arsenal building near 64th & 5th). Otherwise it's a museum/zoo-indoor day.

## Tue May 26 — playgrounds reopen, Skate Circle in the afternoon

80°F partly sunny — **the playgrounds dry out and reopen** (Heckscher, Levin, and Diana Ross are your closest to where you live). *Esther Farkas* performs at **Dene Lawn 11 AM** — gentle, picnic-blanket-friendly. **Skate Circle at Dead Road North 2 PM** — a perennial favorite; the kids can watch the regulars or join if they have skates.

## Wed May 27 — heat day, mornings only, Corporate Challenge night 1 of 2

83°F mostly sunny, **the warmest day of the week — plan park time before 11 AM or after 5 PM, not midday**. **Heads-up: Corporate Challenge race night 1 of 2 takes East Drive Wed evening, 6:45 PM start** — south end (60s–70s) gets crowded with race logistics from late afternoon. Stick to the north loop or West Drive playgrounds (Levin, Adventure) in the late afternoon.

## Thu May 28 — Corporate Challenge night 2 + Barefoot Shakespeare

80°F mostly sunny. **Corporate Challenge race night 2 of 2, 6:45 PM start** — same south-end advisory as Wednesday. **Conservancy Women's Committee Game Event at the Chess and Checkers House 8 AM**. **Barefoot Shakespeare debuts their June show at Summit Rock 3 PM** — free outdoor theater, great for kids 8+. **Sexual Health Mobile Medical Unit at Grand Army Plaza 10 AM** (FYI if you're walking past the southeast corner with kids).

## Fri May 29 — food market + early-weekend energy

78°F mostly sunny. **Columbus Circle Food Market at Merchants' Gate 9:30 AM** — easy stroller-friendly stop for snacks before heading deeper into the park. **Barefoot Shakespeare at Summit Rock 3 PM** (second performance). **Maria Benvenuto at Cop Cot 6 PM** — small acoustic in a beautiful gazebo, a sweet pre-dinner stop for the family. **Julius Caesar at The Pool 10 AM** continues.

## Sat May 30 — the big free-event day

77°F mostly sunny. **This is the day.** Highlights for families:

- **Cast for Kids at The Pond, 9 AM** — kids try fishing, gear provided, free
- **Model Yacht Racing at Kerbs Boathouse, 9 AM** — Conservatory Water classic
- **Skate Circle at Dead Road North, 2 PM**
- **SummerStage Festival at Rumsey Playfield, 8 AM** (more for older family members; check the lineup)
- ***Griffin's Tale* at Pilgrim Hill, 12 PM** — family-friendly storytelling
- ***That Guitar Man from Central Park* at Hernshead, 12 PM** — singalong-style busking
- **Montefiore Adaptive Sports Festival at the Tennis Courts, 11 AM** — inclusive sports day, worth a visit even as a spectator
- **Barefoot Shakespeare at Summit Rock, 3 PM** for the older kids

**East Meadow is closed** all weekend — keep that off your picnic list.

## Sun May 31 — Folkdancers + Skate Circle wrap

74°F partly sunny. **Central Park International Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza 11 AM** — gorgeous to watch with kids, very approachable, often welcoming new dancers. **Skate Circle at Dead Road North 2 PM**. *Julius Caesar* at The Pool 10 AM is the closing performance. **East Meadow still closed**.

## Quick recap

- **Mon** — washout, museum/indoor day
- **Tue** — playgrounds reopen; Skate Circle 2 PM
- **Wed** — heat day, morning park only; race night 1 (6:45 PM) south-end logistics
- **Thu** — race night 2 (6:45 PM); Conservancy event; **Barefoot Shakespeare debut**
- **Fri** — Food market 9:30 AM; Maria Benvenuto at Cop Cot 6 PM
- **Sat** — **the big day**: Cast for Kids, Model Yachting, Skate Circle, Adaptive Sports Festival
- **Sun** — Folkdancers + Skate Circle, gentler pace

Have a beautiful week in the park,
The Central Park Guide
