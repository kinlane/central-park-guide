# Central Park Guide for Picnickers — Mon May 25 – Sun May 31

Hey picnickers,

**Memorial Day is a washout** — skip it, the weather has other plans. But **Tue through Saturday is a picnic dream**: dry, breezy, low humidity, partly to mostly sunny, **Wednesday peaking at 83°F**. **Saturday is the year's first real packed-lawn day** with SummerStage at Rumsey, Skate Circle, and a free-event lineup pulling crowds. **East Meadow is closed Sat–Sun**; route around it. **Columbus Circle Food Market sets up at Merchants' Gate Thu/Fri/Sat 9:30 AM** — easy stop for picnic supplies.

## Weather this week

Stormy Memorial Day clears into **a beautiful, dry Tue–Sun** — perfect picnic weather. **Wednesday's 83°F is the only hot day**; Tue–Thu and Fri–Sun are upper 70s. **Breeze stays light all week**, no umbrella-flip days. **Friday afternoon and Saturday late-morning are sweet spots** for a picnic spread.

## Mon May 25 (Memorial Day) — picnic cancelled, indoor day

Thunderstorms, 74°F, 84% precip. **Memorial Day picnic plans are washed.** Save the spread for Tuesday. The Arsenal Gallery (free, 9 AM) is an indoor pivot.

## Tue May 26 — first picnic day, **Sheep Meadow or Cherry Hill**

80°F partly sunny. **Sheep Meadow opens for picnicking** (lawn-closure schedule allowing — check the gate sign). **Cherry Hill is the prettiest spot at midday** with the Lake and Bow Bridge in view. Lawns will be damp from Monday's rain — bring an extra blanket. *Esther Farkas* at Dene Lawn 11 AM is a small acoustic — if you picnic at Dene Lawn you get the show for free.

## Wed May 27 — heat day, **shade picnic at Mineral Springs** (race night 1 of 2)

83°F mostly sunny — **the warmest day of the week**. **Pick a shade spot: Mineral Springs is your best bet** (the elm canopy stays cool all afternoon). Heckscher Picnic Area is also shaded. **Avoid open lawn picnics midday** — Sheep Meadow and Great Lawn will bake. **Heads-up: Corporate Challenge race night 1 of 2 takes East Drive Wed evening, 6:45 PM start** — south end is crowded with race logistics from 4 PM; pack up your picnic before that.

## Thu May 28 — **picnic + Food Market** + race night 2 + Barefoot Shakespeare

80°F mostly sunny. **Columbus Circle Food Market opens at Merchants' Gate 9:30 AM** — your easy picnic-shopping stop before heading deeper into the park. **Cherry Hill** or **Dene Lawn** are good Thursday spots. Slight late-shower risk after 4 PM — finish your picnic by 4. **Corporate Challenge race night 2 of 2, 6:45 PM start** — same south-end advisory as Wednesday, plan picnic exits before 4 PM if you're south of 72nd. **Barefoot Shakespeare debuts at Summit Rock 3 PM** if you want a picnic-and-show plan (Summit Rock has limited spread space but the lawn beneath has room).

## Fri May 29 — **the perfect Friday picnic**

78°F mostly sunny. **The picnic day of the week.** Food market continues at Merchants' Gate 9:30 AM. **Sheep Meadow, Cherry Hill, Heckscher Picnic Area, Mineral Springs, Cedar Hill, Ladies' Pavilion, Cop Cot** — pick any. **Maria Benvenuto plays Cop Cot 6 PM** — picnic at Cop Cot 5 PM, stay for the music, walk out at sunset.

## Sat May 30 — **busy lawns, plan the spread**

77°F mostly sunny. **The year's first packed lawn day** — SummerStage Festival at Rumsey draws crowds, NYRR Open Run takes the Great Lawn 8 AM, Adaptive Sports Festival uses the Tennis Courts, Skate Circle is at Dead Road North. **East Meadow is closed** — cross it off the list. **Best Saturday picnic spots: Mineral Springs, Cedar Hill, Sheep Meadow (if not blocked), Cherry Hill** — go early (before 11) to claim a spot. Food market repeats at Merchants' Gate. **Long picnic + slow walk past *Griffin's Tale* at Pilgrim Hill 12 PM + *That Guitar Man* at Hernshead 12 PM is a beautiful day.**

## Sun May 31 — **slower Sunday picnic**

74°F partly sunny. **Sunday lawns settle back down.** **Sheep Meadow, Cherry Hill, Cedar Hill, Heckscher Picnic Area, Ladies' Pavilion** — all open and quieter than Saturday. **Folkdancers at King Jagiello 11 AM** is a lovely walk-through. *Julius Caesar* closes at The Pool 10 AM. **East Meadow still closed**.

## Quick recap

- **Mon** — washout, no picnic
- **Tue** — first picnic day, **Cherry Hill or Sheep Meadow**
- **Wed** — heat day, **shade picnic at Mineral Springs**; race night 1 (6:45 PM)
- **Thu** — picnic + food market opens at Merchants'; race night 2 (6:45 PM)
- **Fri** — **the perfect Friday picnic**, Maria at Cop Cot 6 PM
- **Sat** — **packed lawns**, plan early; East Meadow closed
- **Sun** — slower Sunday picnic, Folkdancers walk-through

Lay it out,
The Central Park Guide
