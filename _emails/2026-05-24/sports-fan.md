# Central Park Guide for Sports Fans — Mon May 25 – Sun May 31

Hey sports fans,

**The week is built around three races and a full youth league weekend.** **J.P. Morgan Corporate Challenge takes East Drive Wednesday AND Thursday evenings** in 2026 — the race has expanded to a two-night format this year, 6:45 PM start each evening. **CRCA's Central Park Racing Series crit** goes off Saturday 4–7 AM, and Saturday brings **NYRR's Open Run at the Great Lawn** plus the **Montefiore Adaptive Sports Festival** at the Tennis Courts. **Adult and youth softball/baseball/tennis fields are mostly active Tue–Sun** after Monday's washout.

## Weather this week

Stormy Memorial Day (74°F, 84% precip) washes out all field play. **Tue–Sun is beautiful** — dry, **Wednesday peaking at 83°F**, rest 77–80°F. **Field conditions on Tuesday will be heavy from Monday's rain** — heads-up if you're playing early.

## Mon May 25 (Memorial Day) — washout, fields closed

Thunderstorms all day, 74°F. **All field play likely cancelled** — softball, tennis, t-ball, kickball. Corporate Challenge staging trucks arrive at South Dead Road and Bandshell Plaza despite the rain. Indoor day.

## Tue May 26 — fields drying, light league play

80°F partly sunny. **Heckscher, North Meadow, and Great Lawn** are likely playable by afternoon but morning fields will be heavy. Tennis Courts dry fast — adult tennis tournaments and bookings should be on. **CP Sports mini-camp programming begins** at South Dead Road Wednesday (heads-up for the south end).

## Wed May 27 — **Corporate Challenge race night 1 of 2, 83°F**

83°F mostly sunny. **Corporate Challenge race night 1 of 2, 6:45 PM start, East Drive** — 3.5-mile course, the corporate-team flagship NYC race of the year, **first year as a two-night event** (Central Park Conservancy is the 2026 beneficiary). **Watch from Bandshell Plaza** for the start/finish area; **East 72nd Street and Cherry Hill** are good mid-race vantages. Heat will be a story — **83°F is the warmest start the Corporate Challenge has seen in recent years**. CP Sports Mini-camp at South Dead Road 9:30 AM.

## Thu May 28 — **Corporate Challenge race night 2 of 2** + fields full

80°F mostly sunny. **Corporate Challenge race night 2 of 2, 6:45 PM start** — same East Drive course. **Full adult and youth field play** Tue–Thu — Heckscher (softball, soccer training), North Meadow (softball, t-ball), Tennis Courts (tournament play heating up), and the East 102nd Street Clay Oval (Little League). **Conservancy Women's Committee at Chess and Checkers House 8 AM** — chess and game-day event, two consecutive days. **Columbus Circle Food Market at Merchants' Gate 9:30 AM**.

## Fri May 29 — clean Friday, league play continues

78°F mostly sunny. Field schedules continue across Heckscher, North Meadow, Great Lawn, the Clay Oval, and Tennis Courts. **Maria Benvenuto at Cop Cot 6 PM** (small acoustic, not sports but in the south-end mix). Otherwise a normal Friday in spring.

## Sat May 30 — **the racing day**: CRCA crit + NYRR Open Run + Adaptive Sports Festival

77°F mostly sunny. The lineup:

- **CRCA Central Park Racing Series, East Drive 4–7 AM** — open crit racing on the loop, A-B-C fields, the first big CRCA Saturday of the stretch
- **NYRR Open Run at the Great Lawn, 8 AM** — free 5K, no-reg, the casual-runner counterpart to CRCA's morning
- **Montefiore Adaptive Sports Festival, Tennis Courts, 11 AM** — inclusive sports day, worth checking out as a spectator
- **Model Yacht Racing at Kerbs Boathouse, 9 AM** — Conservatory Water, the small-craft tradition
- **Cast for Kids at The Pond, 9 AM** — fishing, gear provided
- **Skate Circle at Dead Road North, 2 PM**

**Full field play across the park** behind those events. **East Meadow is closed** all weekend.

## Sun May 31 — recovery day + Folkdancers

74°F partly sunny. **Lighter field schedule** Sunday — youth softball and t-ball morning slots, adult tennis bookings in the afternoon. **Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza 11 AM** (cultural, not sports). **Skate Circle at Dead Road North 2 PM**. East Meadow still closed.

## Quick recap

- **Mon** — washout, all fields likely cancelled
- **Tue** — fields drying, league play returns by afternoon
- **Wed** — **Corporate Challenge race night 1 of 2** (6:45 PM), 83°F heat
- **Thu** — **Corporate Challenge race night 2 of 2** (6:45 PM); full league play; Conservancy event at Chess House
- **Fri** — clean league day
- **Sat** — **CRCA crit + NYRR Open Run + Adaptive Sports Festival**
- **Sun** — lighter day, Folkdancers + Skate Circle

Play well,
The Central Park Guide
