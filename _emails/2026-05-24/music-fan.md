# Central Park Guide for Music Fans — Mon May 25 – Sun May 31

Hey music fans,

**The big news this week:** SummerStage's first festival weekend opens **Saturday May 30 at Rumsey Playfield** — the first big free outdoor day of the season. *Maria Benvenuto* at Cop Cot Friday evening and the **Central Park International Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza Sunday** round out a strong week. And: **Naumburg's season opens June 9** — two weeks out, mark the calendar.

## Weather this week

Memorial Day storms wash out Monday, then **Tue–Sun is dry and beautiful** — 77–83°F, low humidity, perfect outdoor-music weather. **Saturday at Rumsey Playfield is going to be a glorious day** for a festival; bring sunscreen and water.

## Mon May 25 (Memorial Day) — no music, indoor pivot

Storms all day. No outdoor programming. **The Arsenal Gallery is hosting *Sarah Yuster: Outside Voices*** (free, 9 AM) if you want an arts-adjacent indoor stop.

## Tue May 26 — Esther Farkas at Dene Lawn

80°F partly sunny. **Esther Farkas performs at Dene Lawn, 11 AM** — small, acoustic, the kind of pop-up south-end performance that's easy to miss. Beautiful first-clean-day setting.

## Wed May 27 — quiet music day, Corporate Challenge night 1 of 2

83°F mostly sunny. **No major music programming today** — the Bandshell and Rumsey are quiet. The big park energy is the **Corporate Challenge race night 1 of 2 in the evening (6:45 PM start)**; not a music event, but it pulls crowds to the south end if you're walking past.

## Thu May 28 — Barefoot Shakespeare debut + Corporate Challenge night 2

80°F mostly sunny. **Barefoot Shakespeare's June 2026 show debuts at Summit Rock 3 PM** — Shakespeare productions usually carry a music ensemble; tagged Concerts & Performances + Theater. Worth checking out for the musical scoring side. **Corporate Challenge race night 2 of 2, 6:45 PM start** (south-end crowd heads-up, not a music event).

## Fri May 29 — Maria Benvenuto at Cop Cot, golden hour

78°F mostly sunny. **Maria Benvenuto at Cop Cot, 6 PM** — small acoustic, intimate setting, the kind of pre-sunset show that defines park summers. Cop Cot is the wooden gazebo just inside the southeast corner — bring a blanket and arrive a few minutes early. *Barefoot Shakespeare* at Summit Rock 3 PM also has the ensemble music angle.

## Sat May 30 — **SummerStage Festival opens, Rumsey Playfield, 8 AM**

77°F mostly sunny. **The first festival day of the SummerStage season at Rumsey Playfield, 8 AM onward** — this is the headline of the music week and the unofficial start of summer concert season at Central Park. Check cityparksfoundation.org for the lineup. **Free, all-day.** Also: ***That Guitar Man from Central Park* at Hernshead, 12 PM** — open busking, the classic park guitar tradition; **Barefoot Shakespeare** at Summit Rock 3 PM continues.

## Sun May 31 — Folkdancers + Folkdancers Closing

74°F partly sunny. **Central Park International Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza, 11 AM** — circle dancing, multi-cultural music, the sound of a Sunday morning in the south end. Tagged Concerts & Performances + Folk + Music.

## Looking ahead: Naumburg opens June 9

The **Naumburg Orchestral Concerts** season opens **Tuesday June 9** at Bandshell Plaza — *Baroklyn, Simone Dinnerstein, & Concora choir*. The full season runs Tuesdays through August 4. Five concerts. **Mark the date** — free, historic, the oldest free outdoor classical series in the country.

## Quick recap

- **Mon** — washout, no music
- **Tue** — Esther Farkas at Dene Lawn 11 AM
- **Wed** — quiet music day
- **Thu** — Barefoot Shakespeare debut (music in production)
- **Fri** — **Maria Benvenuto at Cop Cot 6 PM**
- **Sat** — **SummerStage Festival opens at Rumsey 8 AM**
- **Sun** — Folkdancers at King Jagiello 11 AM
- **Next week:** Naumburg opens Tuesday June 9

Listen well,
The Central Park Guide
