# Central Park Guide for Cyclists — Mon May 25 – Sun May 31

Hey cyclists,

**The J.P. Morgan Corporate Challenge owns Wed AND Thu evenings — 2026 is a two-night event**, 6:45 PM start each evening, 3.5-mile course, with staging trucks at South Dead Road and Bandshell Plaza Mon → Fri. Memorial Day washes out; **CRCA crit kicks off Saturday at 4 AM**. The clean lap days this week are Tuesday morning and Friday — Thursday gets the second race night.

## Weather this week

Stormy Memorial Day (74°F, 84% precip) clearing into **a beautiful Tue–Sun stretch**, **Wednesday peaking at 83°F mostly sunny**, the rest of the week 77–80°F partly-to-mostly sunny with low precip. Skip Monday's wet roads, **ride Tuesday morning hard**, and on Wed AND Thu get your laps in before 6 AM or write the evening off after 5 PM (both race nights).

## Mon May 25 (Memorial Day) — wet, staging begins

Showers and thunderstorms all day, 74°F. **Corporate Challenge staging trucks arrive at South Dead Road and Bandshell Plaza** — lane impact on West Drive in the 70s, though wet pavement is the bigger reason to stay off the loop today. Indoor trainer or a coffee ride.

## Tue May 26 — your cleanest lap day

**80°F partly sunny, dry, no events on the loop.** First clean ride day post-storms. Staging footprint at South Dead Road continues but doesn't take the road. **This is the long ride or the hard intervals day** — go early before the work-from-home walker volume picks up around 9 AM.

## Wed May 27 — race night 1 of 2, loop closed 5–10 PM

83°F mostly sunny, **the warmest day of the week**. **Corporate Challenge race night 1, 6:45 PM start**: East Drive in the 60s–70s is closed to all traffic 5–10 PM, with staging trucks parked at South Dead Road and Bandshell Plaza since Monday. **Pre-dawn (before 6 AM) is the clean window — heat is mild and the road is yours.** Anything after work is a no-go until 10 PM.

## Thu May 28 — race night 2 of 2, same closure window

80°F mostly sunny, slight chance of late-day showers. **Corporate Challenge race night 2, 6:45 PM start** — East Drive in the 60s–70s closed 5–10 PM, same as Wednesday. **Pre-dawn or post-10 PM only on the loop.** Morning is clean; watch the radar for any late-day cells too.

## Fri May 29 — clean, food market at Merchants' Gate

78°F mostly sunny. **Columbus Circle Food Market at Merchants' Gate from 9:30 AM** — adds pedestrian volume to the Merchants' Gate corner, slow your line there. Loop is otherwise yours.

## Sat May 30 — CRCA Central Park Racing Series, your morning is the race

77°F mostly sunny. **CRCA Central Park Racing Series crit on East Drive, 4–7 AM** — race traffic, lap-traffic peloton, the works. If you're in it, you know. **If you're not racing, push your ride to 8 AM or later** — by then it's clear and the weather is perfect for a long Saturday loop. Also that morning: **NYRR Open Run at the Great Lawn 8 AM** (5K runners, off-loop but adds Great Lawn foot traffic). East Meadow is closed all weekend.

## Sun May 31 — open road, gentle Folkdancer crowd at the south end

74°F partly sunny. **Central Park International Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza 11 AM** — south-end pedestrian volume, off-loop. East Meadow remains closed. This is your recovery-long-ride day; the loop is open.

## Quick recap

- **Mon** — wet + staging begins, off-day
- **Tue** — clean lap day, ride hard
- **Wed** — race night 1 (6:45 PM); ride pre-dawn or post-10 PM
- **Thu** — **race night 2 (6:45 PM)**; same closure window
- **Fri** — open road, food market at Merchants'
- **Sat** — CRCA crit 4–7 AM, ride after 8
- **Sun** — clean, gentle south-end foot traffic

Tailwinds,
The Central Park Guide
