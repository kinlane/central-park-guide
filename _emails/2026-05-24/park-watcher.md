# Central Park Guide for Park Watchers — Mon May 25 – Sun May 31

Hi park watchers,

**Two signal events anchor the week**: the J.P. Morgan Corporate Challenge takes the loop **Wednesday AND Thursday evenings** in 2026 — the race has expanded to a two-night format this year, 6:45 PM start each evening, Mon–Fri staging at South Dead Road and Bandshell Plaza. **SummerStage's festival season opens Saturday May 30 at Rumsey Playfield** — the unofficial start of summer programming. **Naumburg's season opens June 9**, two weeks out, the oldest free outdoor classical series in the country since 1905. Memorial Day is a washout that cancels nothing notable; the rest of the week is dry.

## Weather this week

Stormy Memorial Day clears into **a beautiful Tue–Sun**, **Wednesday peaking at 83°F mostly sunny**, the rest 77–80°F. **This is the first heat day of the season** (83°F Wed) — first real test of the Corporate Challenge's late-spring date, last spring it ran in milder conditions.

## Mon May 25 (Memorial Day) — washout, staging begins

Showers and thunderstorms, 74°F, 84% precip. **Corporate Challenge staging begins at South Dead Road and Bandshell Plaza** — first appearance of the staging trucks this year. No Memorial Day ceremony entries in the data this year; *Sarah Yuster: Outside Voices* at the Arsenal Gallery is the only programming holding up against the rain.

## Tue May 26 — first clean weekday, modest calendar

80°F partly sunny. **Esther Farkas at Dene Lawn 11 AM** is the only programmed event outside the Corporate Challenge footprint. *Julius Caesar* (NY Classical Theatre) launches a seven-day run at The Pool 10 AM — daily through Sunday May 31. **First Skate Circle of the week, 2 PM at Dead Road North**.

## Wed May 27 — **Corporate Challenge race day** + first heat day

83°F mostly sunny. **Corporate Challenge race night 1 of 2, 6:45 PM start** (3.5 miles, East Drive 60s–70s closed 5–10 PM). **2026 is the first year the race uses a two-night format** — Central Park Conservancy is the named beneficiary. **First 83°F day of the season** — worth watching how the heat affects participant times and post-race medical staging on Bandshell Plaza. **CP Sports Mini-camp** at South Dead Road 9:30 AM is the new education listing this week.

## Thu May 28 — **Corporate Challenge race night 2 of 2** + Barefoot Shakespeare debut

80°F mostly sunny. **Corporate Challenge race night 2 of 2, 6:45 PM start** — same closure window as Wednesday. **Barefoot Shakespeare's June 2026 show opens at Summit Rock 3 PM** — first appearance of the company in the calendar this year; runs Thursday/Friday/Saturday this week. **Conservancy Women's Committee Game Event at Chess and Checkers House 8 AM** (small invitational, two consecutive days). **Columbus Circle Food Market at Merchants' Gate 9:30 AM** — first appearance, runs Thu/Fri/Sat. **Sexual Health Mobile Medical Unit at Grand Army Plaza 10 AM** — new entry in the calendar this year.

## Fri May 29 — quiet weekday, Maria Benvenuto at Cop Cot

78°F mostly sunny. Food market repeats at Merchants'; Barefoot Shakespeare repeats at Summit Rock; **Maria Benvenuto at Cop Cot 6 PM** is the only new entry. Conservancy Women's Committee continues at Chess and Checkers.

## Sat May 30 — **biggest event day of the week**: SummerStage + CRCA + NYRR Open Run

77°F mostly sunny. The lineup:

- **CRCA Central Park Racing Series, East Drive 4–7 AM** — first CRCA crit appearance in this stretch of the calendar
- **SummerStage Festival opens at Rumsey Playfield 8 AM** — **the unofficial start of summer programming season at the park**
- **NYRR Open Run at the Great Lawn 8 AM** — free 5K, no-reg
- **Model Yacht Racing at Kerbs Boathouse 9 AM**
- **Cast for Kids at The Pond 9 AM**
- **Montefiore Adaptive Sports Festival at Tennis Courts 11 AM**
- ***Griffin's Tale* at Pilgrim Hill 12 PM**
- ***That Guitar Man from Central Park* at Hernshead 12 PM**
- **Skate Circle 2 PM, Barefoot Shakespeare 3 PM**

**East Meadow Closure begins Saturday and runs through Sunday** — first lawn closure of the season.

## Sun May 31 — Folkdancers + Julius Caesar finale

74°F partly sunny. **Central Park International Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza 11 AM** — first appearance of the year. *Julius Caesar* closes its seven-day run at The Pool 10 AM. East Meadow closure continues. **Skate Circle 2 PM** wraps the weekend.
