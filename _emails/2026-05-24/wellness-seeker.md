# Central Park Guide for Wellness Seekers — Mon May 25 – Sun May 31

Hi wellness seekers,

**Post-storm clarity is the gift of this week.** Memorial Day's thunderstorms scrub the air, and Tuesday opens into the cleanest five-day stretch we've had this spring — quiet trails, soft morning light, low pollen. **The Ramble, North Woods, Hallett Nature Sanctuary, and Conservatory Garden** are all at peak. Dawn windows are gorgeous Tue–Fri before the warmer afternoons land.

## Weather this week

Stormy Memorial Day (74°F, 84% precip) washes the air clean, then **Tue–Sun is beautiful**: dry, **Wednesday peaking at 83°F** (the only real heat day), rest 77–80°F with cool nights in the low 60s. **Dawn temperatures Tue–Fri are around 60°F** — ideal for slow movement, breathwork, sun salutations on a quiet lawn.

## Mon May 25 (Memorial Day) — rest day

Thunderstorms all day. **Take it as a rest day.** If you need movement, indoor yoga or a slow walk under the colonnade at Bethesda Terrace between cells. The Arsenal Gallery is showing *Sarah Yuster: Outside Voices* (free, 9 AM) for an indoor cultural stop.

## Tue May 26 — dawn at the Reservoir, slow movement at Cherry Hill

80°F partly sunny. **The most beautiful morning of the week.** Dawn around 5:30 AM, low 60s, post-storm light on the Reservoir water. **Cherry Hill is a good open lawn for slow movement** if you want to stretch outdoors. The Bethesda Terrace upper plaza is quiet 7–9 AM. Avoid the south end after 9 AM as the workday foot traffic builds.

## Wed May 27 — heat day, **early or wait until evening** (race night 1 of 2)

83°F mostly sunny. **Get out by 7 AM** — by 10 AM the heat starts and the park's south end is in **Corporate Challenge race mode** all evening (logistics 4 PM onward, **race 6:45 PM**, first of two race nights). **The Loch and North Woods stay coolest** through the day. Evening is a no for outdoor practice in the south end; consider a North Woods dusk walk after 7:30 PM once the sun lowers.

## Thu May 28 — Hallett Sanctuary morning + race night 2 of 2 in the evening

80°F mostly sunny, slight late shower chance. **Hallett Nature Sanctuary** opens at 8 AM (closes 5 PM) — a small, fenced sanctuary just inside the Pond, almost no visitors midweek. **Conservatory Garden** is at peak — South Garden's perennial border is one of the most contemplative spaces in the park. **Corporate Challenge race night 2 of 2, 6:45 PM start** — same south-end advisory as Wednesday (avoid outdoor practice south of 72nd 4–10 PM). **Sexual Health Mobile Medical Unit at Grand Army Plaza 10 AM** — useful resource to know about if it's relevant to you (free, drop-in).

## Fri May 29 — Conservatory + Cop Cot golden hour

78°F mostly sunny. **Conservatory Garden** in the morning, **Cop Cot at golden hour** in the evening — Cop Cot is the wooden gazebo just inside the southeast corner, often empty, with a view to the Pond and the city beyond. Maria Benvenuto plays an acoustic set at Cop Cot 6 PM if you want gentle ambient music alongside.

## Sat May 30 — head north, the south is busy

77°F mostly sunny. **The south end is packed all day** (SummerStage Festival, NYRR Open Run, Skate Circle, Adaptive Sports Festival, CRCA crit at dawn). **Take Saturday north**: dawn walk through the Ramble or North Woods, slow movement at the Pinetum, lunch by the Loch. **The North Meadow stays quiet on Saturdays** despite the south-end chaos. East Meadow is closed.

## Sun May 31 — Folkdancers as ambient sound, slow Sunday

74°F partly sunny. **Sunday's natural quiet is back** — east of the Reservoir is gorgeous mid-morning; the Conservatory Garden is best 9–11 AM before the casual visitors arrive. **Folkdancers at King Jagiello 11 AM** add a warm cultural ambient sound to the south end if you want to end your morning there.

## Quick recap

- **Mon** — rest day, indoor pivot
- **Tue** — **dawn at the Reservoir**, slow movement at Cherry Hill
- **Wed** — heat day, before 7 AM only; race night 1 (6:45 PM); North Woods cool refuge
- **Thu** — **Hallett Sanctuary morning** + Conservatory Garden; race night 2 (6:45 PM)
- **Fri** — Conservatory Garden AM, Cop Cot golden hour
- **Sat** — **head north**, south is packed
- **Sun** — slow Sunday, Folkdancers as ambient sound

Move gently,
The Central Park Guide
