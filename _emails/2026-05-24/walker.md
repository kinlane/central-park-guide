# Central Park Guide for Walkers — Mon May 25 – Sun May 31

Hi walkers,

**Memorial Day is a washout**, but every day from Tuesday on is genuinely beautiful — partly to mostly sunny, 74–83°F, low humidity by late-May standards. **The Pool gets daily 10 AM Julius Caesar** from NY Classical Theatre (free), **Barefoot Shakespeare debuts at Summit Rock Thursday**, and Saturday brings a packed calendar of free programming you can string into a walking loop. Plan your big walks for Tue, Sat, and Sun.

## Weather this week

Wet, stormy Memorial Day clearing into **a stunning Tue–Sun**: dry, comfortable, **Wednesday peaking at 83°F mostly sunny**, the rest of the week 77–80°F. After Monday's storms pass, **the air is going to be clean** — this is the best walking week we've had this month, and the long-daylight Friday and Saturday evenings are worth a sunset loop.

## Mon May 25 (Memorial Day) — indoor day

Showers and thunderstorms, 74°F, 84% precip. **The Arsenal Gallery is open** if you want to duck inside — *Sarah Yuster: Outside Voices* is the show, free, 9 AM. Otherwise an indoor day; **save your Memorial Day walking for next year.**

## Tue May 26 — Julius Caesar at The Pool, your first clean walk day

80°F partly sunny. **NY Classical Theatre's *Julius Caesar* at The Pool, 10 AM (free, daily this week)** — pair it with a walk through the North Woods on the way in or out, and you get one of the park's best quiet stretches before the show. *Esther Farkas* performs at Dene Lawn 11 AM (south end, gentle audience). **Skate Circle at Dead Road North 2 PM** — fun to walk past, even better to sit and watch.

## Wed May 27 — heat peaks, Corporate Challenge night 1 of 2

83°F mostly sunny — **the warmest day of the week, go early.** *Julius Caesar* at The Pool 10 AM still on. **Heads-up: J.P. Morgan Corporate Challenge race night 1 of 2 takes East Drive Wednesday evening, 6:45 PM start** — if you walk the East Drive after 4 PM you'll be inside a staging zone. **South end (60s–70s) is best avoided 4–10 PM**; walk the north loop or the Bridle Path instead.

## Thu May 28 — Corporate Challenge night 2 + Barefoot Shakespeare debut

80°F mostly sunny, slight late-day shower chance. **Corporate Challenge race night 2 of 2, 6:45 PM start** — same East Drive south-end advisory as Wednesday (avoid 60s–70s 4–10 PM). **Barefoot Shakespeare's June 2026 show debuts at Summit Rock, 3 PM** — free, outdoor, the kind of pop-up theater that defines park summers. **Columbus Circle Food Market at Merchants' Gate 9:30 AM** for a lunch detour. **Conservancy Women's Committee event at Chess and Checkers House 8 AM**.

## Fri May 29 — busiest weekday, Friday calm sets in

78°F mostly sunny. **Barefoot Shakespeare at Summit Rock 3 PM** (second performance of the run). Food market repeats at Merchants' Gate 9:30 AM. *Julius Caesar* at The Pool 10 AM. **Maria Benvenuto at Cop Cot 6 PM** — small acoustic, beautiful spot for a sunset walk. The park settles into Friday-evening calm after work hours.

## Sat May 30 — the biggest day, string it into a walk

77°F mostly sunny — **plan a long walk and turn it into a free-event hop**. The big list: **SummerStage Festival at Rumsey Playfield 8 AM, NYRR Open Run at the Great Lawn 8 AM, Model Yacht Racing at Kerbs Boathouse 9 AM, Cast for Kids at The Pond 9 AM, *Griffin's Tale* at Pilgrim Hill 12 PM, *That Guitar Man* at Hernshead 12 PM, Skate Circle at Dead Road North 2 PM, Barefoot Shakespeare at Summit Rock 3 PM.** That's a complete south-to-north walking story if you start at The Pond and finish at the Pool. **East Meadow is closed all weekend** (route around it).

## Sun May 31 — Folkdancers, slower pace

74°F partly sunny. **Central Park International Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza 11 AM** — circle dancing at the south end, free, a lovely thing to wander past. *Julius Caesar* at The Pool 10 AM is the closing performance of this run. **Skate Circle 2 PM**. East Meadow still closed; the rest of the park is yours.

## Quick recap

- **Mon** — washout, indoor day
- **Tue** — *Julius Caesar* + North Woods, first clean walk
- **Wed** — early walks only; avoid south end after 4 PM (race night 1 of 2, 6:45 PM)
- **Thu** — race night 2 (6:45 PM); **Barefoot Shakespeare debut** at Summit Rock 3 PM
- **Fri** — busy day-of-events; Maria Benvenuto at Cop Cot 6 PM
- **Sat** — biggest event day, plan a long walk
- **Sun** — Folkdancers + Folkdancers Closing *Julius Caesar*

Enjoy the late spring,
The Central Park Guide
