# Final week of Julius Caesar + Bandshell season opens: Mon May 18 – Sun May 24

Hey theater fans,

Sunday-evening edition, looking at the week starting tomorrow. **Julius Caesar enters its final stretch at The Pool. The Bandshell wakes up Saturday with the Salsa Dura Social — the outdoor performance season formally opens.** Two big anchor moments in one week.

## Weather this week

Hot and sunny Mon–Tue with Tuesday at 93°F, Wednesday-evening thunderstorms, then cool and damp Thursday on with **rain likely Saturday**. Bring water to any outdoor performance Mon–Tue — The Pool has limited shade. Saturday's Bandshell opener is at meaningful rain risk; watch for postponement notices Friday/Saturday.

## Julius Caesar — final stretch at The Pool (closes Sat May 30)

**Final performances at The Pool Lawns** before the production closes. This week's schedule:

- **Mon 5/18** — performing (86°F — bring water)
- **Tue 5/19** — DARK (smart call given 93°F)
- **Wed 5/20** — performing, storms after 4 PM; afternoon shows at risk
- **Thu 5/21** — performing in much cooler air (70°F)
- **Fri 5/22** — DARK
- **Sat 5/23** — performing; outdoor staging affected by likely rain
- **Sun 5/24** — performing

**Best calm-night performance this week: Thursday May 21.** Cool, dry, end-of-week air, no rain. If you've been putting off seeing the production, Thursday is the pick.

## Sat May 23, 5–7:30 PM — Salsa Dura Social at Bandshell

**The 2026 Bandshell season formally opens** with Salsa Dura. The original heavy-hitting NYC salsa style — dance floor + DJ + live elements. Free, plaza dancing in front of the shell.

The shell covers performers but the plaza is open to the sky. **Saturday's rain forecast means possible postponement to the following weekend.** Watch the Bandshell's social channels Friday evening for updates.

## Sat May 23 — also worth catching (rain permitting)

- **That Guitar Man at Hernshead, noon–6 PM** — solo guitar performance, very much theater-of-place. Cancels in steady rain.
- **Julius Caesar matinee + evening at The Pool** — last Saturday in the run

## Sun May 24, 11 AM–7 PM — Folkdancers at King Jagiallo

**Central Park International Folkdancers** at King Jagiallo Plaza. Costumed traditional dance with live music — closer to dance theater than concert. Drop in any afternoon hour.

## Looking ahead

- **May 28–30** — Barefoot Shakespeare opens at the Bandshell (immediately after Salsa Dura)
- **Jun 2–10** — "Julius Caesar 2 of 2" begins at The Pool (a second production claims the space immediately)
- The Public Theater's Delacorte Shakespeare in the Park season usually starts early June
