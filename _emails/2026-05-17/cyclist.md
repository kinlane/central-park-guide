# Central Park Cycling Heads-Up: Mon May 18 – Sun May 24

Hey cyclists,

Sunday-evening edition, looking at the week starting tomorrow. **No organized races or rides on the calendar all week — including no CRCA crit racing Memorial Day Saturday.** The road is yours. The catches: a Tuesday 93°F heat peak, a Wednesday storm front, and a wet Memorial Day Saturday.

## Weather this week

Hot and sunny through Tuesday with **Tuesday peaking at 93°F**, Wednesday-evening thunderstorms, then cool damp Thursday onward with **rain showers likely Saturday** and chance of rain Sunday. Dawn lap days Mon–Tue (no loop shade), Wednesday's bisected by the front, Thursday–Friday are the makeup window, Saturday is likely a rest day.

## Mon May 18 — dawn laps recommended

86°F. **Best ride window: 5:30–7:30 AM** before the heat builds. **Bethesda Fountain Terrace party 6–9 PM** — minor pedestrian spill onto 72nd Cross Drive at the Bethesda underpass, easy to ride around.

## Tue May 19 — 93°F, no events, treat it like August

Hottest day of the year so far. **Ride before 6:30 AM or after 7:30 PM.** Two bottles. Lower park has the most shade on West Drive between 72nd and 96th.

## Wed May 20 — morning ride, storms by evening

Hot morning (high 80s), thunderstorms after 4 PM. **Morning is the ride window.** Watch the radar.

## Thu May 21 – Fri May 22 — best lap days of the week

70°F then 66°F. Light winds. **No events all week. This is the makeup window** for any rides you bailed on Mon/Tue. Long ride, hill repeats, whatever you've got.

**Columbus Circle Food Market** opens Thu 5/21 and runs through Sun 5/24 (Memorial Day extension; usually Fri/Sat/Sun only) at Merchants' Gate, 9:30 AM–9 PM. Slow your line through Columbus Circle on those days.

## Sat May 23 — no CRCA, rain likely, heavy south-end foot traffic

**No CRCA crit racing this Saturday** (Memorial Day skip — first absence of the series this season). Rain showers likely all day. Most riders will bail; if you go, it's a slow social ride.

The south end is crowded with pedestrians despite rain:
- **Skate Circle** at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM (cancels in heavy rain, but setup spillover regardless)
- **That Guitar Man at Hernshead**, noon–6 PM — audience on West Drive 75th–78th
- **Salsa Dura Social** at Bandshell, 5–7:30 PM — Bandshell season opener; audience plaza is on the loop
- **Columbus Circle Food Market**, 9:30 AM–9 PM
- **Mineral Springs party**, 3–6 PM
- **Model Yacht Racing** at Kerbs, 9 AM–3 PM (rain-dependent)
- East Meadow closed

**Slow your line everywhere south of 86th** on the afternoon laps.

## Sun May 24 — Memorial Day Sunday

71°F, chance of rain showers.

- **Folkdancers** at King Jagiallo, 11 AM–7 PM (Turtle Pond east — not a loop issue)
- **Skate Circle** at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM
- **Mineral Springs picnic**, noon–5 PM
- **Food Market** at Merchants' Gate, 9:30 AM–9 PM
- East Meadow closed

Morning ride beats afternoon crowd.
