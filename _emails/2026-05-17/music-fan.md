# Central Park Music & Performance: Mon May 18 – Sun May 24

Hey music fans,

Sunday-evening edition, looking at the week starting tomorrow. **The Bandshell wakes up Saturday with the Salsa Dura Social** — the first proper outdoor concert of the 2026 season. Catch: Saturday's looking wet, so watch for postponement updates Friday.

## Weather this week

Hot and sunny Mon–Tue (Tuesday hits 93°F), Wednesday-evening thunderstorms, then cool damp Thursday onward with **rain showers likely Saturday** and chance of rain Sunday. Buskers along the Mall thin during the heat peak; **Saturday's Bandshell season opener is at meaningful rain risk** — check the Bandshell's social channels Friday evening for updates.

## Mon May 18 — Bandshell dark, buskers thinned by heat

No scheduled performances. 86°F all day, but cooler in the evening. **Best busker odds:** Mall and Bethesda Terrace after 7 PM.

## Tue May 19 — Bandshell dark, 93°F keeps buskers off the Mall

Almost no street music on a 93°F day. If you crave live park music tonight, **Julius Caesar at The Pool is DARK** — production rest day. Save your evening for Thursday.

## Wed May 20 — front day, storms in evening

No scheduled music. Julius Caesar at The Pool performs in the heat then risk-of-rain — afternoon shows possibly affected. Buskers thin until the front passes.

## Thu May 21 — Mall buskers return, Julius Caesar in cool weather

70°F. The front has passed, the air is cool, summer light is back. **Thursday evening on the Mall is the sweet spot** for buskers all week. Also: **Julius Caesar at The Pool** in 70°F air is the calm-night performance pick of the season's run.

Small: minor event at Mineral Springs 5–7 PM.

## Fri May 22 — Bandshell dark, Julius Caesar dark, Mall is yours

No formal music programming. **Mall buskers continue.** 66°F evening — really pleasant Friday night strolling weather.

## **Sat May 23, 5–7:30 PM — Salsa Dura Social at Bandshell** (weather pending)

The 2026 outdoor season opener at Bandshell Plaza. Salsa dura — the original heavy-hitting NYC style: dance floor + DJ + live elements. Free, plaza dancing in front of the shell.

**Rain risk:** Saturday forecast shows rain showers likely. The shell covers performers but the audience plaza is open. The show may proceed under reduced format or postpone. If it goes, this is the show of the season so far.

## Sat May 23 — also worth catching (rain permitting)

- **That Guitar Man at Hernshead, noon–6 PM** — solo guitar by the Lake. Extended set (six hours, vs. usual four). Cancels in heavy rain.
- **Skate Circle** at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM — DJ'd music, skate community. Cancels in rain.

## Sun May 24, 11 AM–7 PM — Folkdancers at King Jagiallo

**Central Park International Folkdancers** at King Jagiallo Plaza (east end of Turtle Pond, 75th & CPE). Live musicians, costumed dancers, traditions from around the world. Free.

## Continuing all week — Julius Caesar at The Pool

Live musical staging within the production. **Daily 10 AM–9 PM except Tue 5/19 and Fri 5/22** (dark days). **Final week before May 30 close.** Thursday May 21 in the cool 70°F air is the pick.

Watch the weather Friday — Salsa Dura may shift,
