# Central Park Weekly Digest: Fri Jun 26 – Thu Jul 2

Hi park watchers,

A Pride-weekend-and-World-Cup convergence, SummerStage in full swing, and the season's first real heat wave. Here's the week from above.

## Weather this week

The headline pattern: the **first sustained heat wave of the summer** arrives. Showery and mild through the weekend (mid-70s to mid-80s), then a steady climb — **89° Tue, 91° Wed, 94° Thu** — with afternoon thunderstorm chances Tue–Thu. Expect the park to shift to a morning-and-evening rhythm midweek.

## Pride weekend, twice over

Pride lands on the park from two directions this weekend. On the loop: the **Front Runners NY LGBT Pride Run 4M** (Sat ~8 AM, staging Bandshell Plaza) — a community fixture, `affects-loop`. On the stage: **Dreamland: Pride in Central Park** takes over **Rumsey Playfield Sun 3–10 PM** (SG Lewis, Kungs, Dani Brasil) — one of the bigger SummerStage draws of the season. The **Conservancy's Queer Central Park Tour** (Sat 10 AM, Bethesda) rounds out the theme.

## SummerStage is the week's engine

Rumsey Playfield carries the calendar: **Laurie Anderson's *Republic of Love*** (Fri, free) — a notable booking for a free city stage — a **World Cup watch party** (Sat), **Dreamland Pride** (Sun), and a stacked free **SHABAKA / Kokoroko / Omar / Lovie** bill (Wed). The breadth (avant-garde → dance → spiritual jazz) is the story.

## FIFA World Cup activations ramping

Worth flagging as a pattern: World Cup infrastructure is now visibly in the park. **FIFA Arena free soccer at Tavern on the Green** (daily), **FIFA World Cup bus operations at Columbus Circle** (early mornings Sat & Tue onward), and the SummerStage watch party. Expect this footprint to grow through July.

## Two charity 4-milers, one weekend

Beyond the Pride Run, **Achilles Hope & Possibility 4M** (Sun ~8:15 AM, Bandshell Plaza) brings a large adaptive-athlete field — both `affects-loop`. NYRR-adjacent community races that the permit feed underreports; both were hand-confirmed this week.

## Operational notes

- **Bethesda Terrace and Fountain — closed all week.** The park's centerpiece is offline; worth watching for when it reopens.
- **40 in Focus: SummerStage Through the Lens** photo exhibit running daily at the **Arsenal** (9–5, free) — a quiet season marker.
- East Meadow closures (Sat–Sun) and East Green/72nd Playground maintenance (Mon).

## Quick recap

**Pride weekend:** Front Runners Pride Run 4M (Sat, loop) + Dreamland Pride at SummerStage (Sun) + Queer Central Park Tour.

**SummerStage:** Laurie Anderson (Fri), World Cup party (Sat), Dreamland (Sun), SHABAKA et al. (Wed) — all free.

**FIFA footprint growing:** Tavern soccer, Columbus Circle bus ops, watch party.

**Two `affects-loop` 4-milers:** Pride Run (Sat) + Achilles (Sun), both at Bandshell Plaza.

**Bethesda closed all week; first heat wave (up to 94° Thu).**
