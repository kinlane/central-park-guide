# Central Park Wellness: Fri Jun 26 – Thu Jul 2

Hi wellness seekers,

Yoga on the lawn, a sunrise walk, and a new Afro Vinyasa session this week — plus a heat wave that makes the dawn hours sacred and tells you exactly which side of the park to find quiet on. Here's your week.

## Weather this week

Showery and mild this weekend (mid-70s to mid-80s), then a heat wave Tuesday onward, topping near 94°F Thursday with afternoon storm chances. The lesson is simple: **practice at dawn**. Early mornings stay cool and calm all week; by midday from Tuesday, seek deep shade and water.

## Fri Jun 26 — Yoga on Sheep Meadow

**Yoga Classes** are running on **Sheep Meadow** (part of the park's warm-season yoga programming) — an open, grassy flow with the skyline behind you. Mornings are best before the day warms. For stillness afterward, the **Conservatory Garden** (105th & 5th) is the park's quietest formal space.

## Sat Jun 27 — Sunrise Wellness Walk

A **Sunrise Wellness Walk** sets out near the **Sherman statue / Grand Army Plaza area** in the early morning — a gentle, restorative way to start the weekend before the park fills. With Pride events drawing crowds to the south end this weekend, plan your calm around the **north end**: the **Ramble, North Woods, and the Loch** (the waterfalls run well after the weekend rain).

## Sun Jun 28 — find solitude up north

The south end is given over to the SummerStage Pride celebration (Rumsey, afternoon). Let that decide your route: head to **Hallett Nature Sanctuary, Turtle Pond below Belvedere, or the Conservatory Garden** for quiet. A slow morning in the **Ramble** before the heat is the move.

## Wed Jul 1, 6 PM — Central Park Afro Vinyasa Yoga (Sheep Meadow)

New this week: a **Central Park Afro Vinyasa Yoga** session on **Sheep Meadow at 6 PM** — flow practice set to Afrobeat rhythms, in the cooler evening light. A lovely midweek reset, and the timing dodges the worst of the day's heat. Bring water — it's a 91° day.

## Tue Jul 1 – Thu Jul 2 — dawn and shade

Otherwise a quiet week, ruled by the thermometer (up to **94° Thursday**). Best windows: **before 8 AM** for any practice, and the deep-shade contemplative corners (**North Woods, the Loch, Summit Rock**) by midday. Hydrate, slow down, and let the heat set the pace.

## Quick recap

**Fri 6/26 — Yoga on Sheep Meadow:** morning flow; Conservatory Garden for stillness.

**Sat 6/27 — Sunrise Wellness Walk (Grand Army Plaza area):** restorative early start; quiet up north.

**Sun 6/28 — solitude up north** while Pride fills the south (Ramble, Hallett, Conservatory Garden).

**Wed 7/1, 6 PM — Afro Vinyasa Yoga (Sheep Meadow):** evening flow, cooler light; bring water.

**Tue–Thu — heat (up to 94°):** practice at dawn, deep shade by midday.

Breathe easy,
The Central Park Guide
