# Central Park Music: Fri Jun 26 – Thu Jul 2

Hey music fans,

A genuinely great SummerStage week at Rumsey Playfield — avant-garde tonight, a huge Pride dance party Sunday, and a stacked jazz-and-soul bill Wednesday. All free. Here's the lineup.

## Weather this week

Showery and mild this weekend (mid-70s to mid-80s), turning hot midweek — up to 94°F Thursday with scattered evening thunderstorm chances Tue–Thu. The outdoor shows this week are evening/afternoon slots, so heat is manageable; keep an eye on the radar for Wednesday's show.

## Fri Jun 26, 8 PM — Laurie Anderson: Republic of Love (Rumsey, free)

The headline: **Laurie Anderson** brings *Republic of Love* to **Rumsey Playfield** — a free SummerStage evening of the avant-garde icon's storytelling, electronics, and violin. If you only go to one show this week, this is it. Doors fill early for the marquee names; get there ahead of 8.

## Sat Jun 27, 5 PM — World Cup Watch Party (Rumsey)

SummerStage turns Rumsey into a **World Cup watch party (Panama v. England, 5 PM)** — big screen, DJ sets, festival energy. More vibe than concert, but a fun free hang if football's your thing. (For something acoustic, there's **tango at the Shakespeare statue, 6 PM**.)

## Sun Jun 28, 3–10 PM — Dreamland: Pride in Central Park (Rumsey)

The week's big one: **Dreamland: Pride in Central Park** at **Rumsey Playfield, 3–10 PM** — a full SummerStage Pride celebration headlined by **SG Lewis** and **Kungs**, with **Bob's Dance Shop, Dani Brasil, and MkX**. House and dance all afternoon into the night. Free; expect it to be packed.

## Wed Jul 1, 5 PM — SHABAKA / Kokoroko / Omar / Lovie (Rumsey, free)

A spectacular free bill: **SHABAKA** (the London spiritual-jazz force), Afrobeat collective **Kokoroko**, neo-soul legend **Omar**, and **Lovie** — **5 PM at Rumsey Playfield**. Arrive early; this is one of the best free jazz/soul lineups of the season. Watch Wednesday's storm chance.

## Looking ahead

The **Naumburg Orchestral Concerts** return to the Bandshell **Tuesday Jul 7** (Delirium Musicum) — your classical fix is a week and a half out.

## Quick recap

**Fri 6/26, 8 PM — Laurie Anderson: Republic of Love (Rumsey, free):** the week's must-see.

**Sat 6/27, 5 PM — World Cup Watch Party (Rumsey):** festival hang, DJ sets.

**Sun 6/28, 3–10 PM — Dreamland Pride (Rumsey, free):** SG Lewis + Kungs, all-day dance.

**Wed 7/1, 5 PM — SHABAKA / Kokoroko / Omar / Lovie (Rumsey, free):** stacked jazz/soul; watch the radar.

**Tue 7/7 — Naumburg returns (Bandshell):** classical, looking ahead.
