# Central Park Theater: Fri Jun 26 – Thu Jul 2

Hi theater fans,

Free Shakespeare in the Park is running *Romeo & Juliet* at the Delacorte, a performance-art legend takes Rumsey, and the marionettes are back at the Swedish Cottage. Here's your week onstage.

## Weather this week

Showery this weekend (mid-70s Sat, mid-80s Sun) then a heat wave midweek, up to 94°F Thursday with afternoon/evening thunderstorm chances Tue–Thu. For the open-air Delacorte and Rumsey shows, the weekend evenings are the comfortable ones — and keep an eye on the radar for any midweek outdoor plans.

## Fri Jun 26 – Sun Jun 28, 8 PM — Free Shakespeare in the Park: Romeo & Juliet

The headline: **Free Shakespeare in the Park** is staging ***Romeo & Juliet* at the Delacorte Theater**, 8 PM nightly this weekend. It's the quintessential Central Park theater experience under the stars at Turtle Pond. Free tickets go fast — line up early, enter the lottery, or watch for standby. If you've never done a Delacorte night, this is the one.

## Fri Jun 26, 8 PM — Laurie Anderson: Republic of Love (Rumsey)

Not a play, but unmissable for anyone who loves performance: **Laurie Anderson** brings ***Republic of Love*** to **SummerStage at Rumsey Playfield** — storytelling, electronics, and violin from one of the great avant-garde theatrical artists. Free. (Same 8 PM slot as the Delacorte — you'll have to choose.)

## Sat Jun 27 — Marionettes + tango

Family-and-folk theater notes: **'Little Red's Hood' at the Swedish Cottage Marionette Theatre** (the classic hand-built puppet stage — reserve ahead), and an evening of **tango at the Shakespeare statue (6 PM)** on the Mall, if you like your performance al fresco and participatory.

## Mon Jun 29 – Thu Jul 2 — between shows

A quieter midweek for the stage, with the Delacorte running its own schedule. The **40 in Focus** photo exhibit at the Arsenal (free, daily) is a worthwhile companion if you want park-arts context, and SummerStage's music slate continues midweek.

## Quick recap

**Fri–Sun 6/26–28, 8 PM — Romeo & Juliet at the Delacorte (Free Shakespeare):** the week's headline; line up early.

**Fri 6/26, 8 PM — Laurie Anderson: Republic of Love (Rumsey):** avant-garde performance, free.

**Sat 6/27 — 'Little Red's Hood' marionettes (Swedish Cottage) + tango at the Shakespeare statue (6 PM).**

**Mon–Thu — quieter; heat builds to 94°.**

Break a leg (and bring water),
The Central Park Guide
