# Central Park Family Plans: Fri Jun 26 – Thu Jul 2

Hi families,

Zoo feedings, the carousel, marionettes, model boats, and free World Cup soccer — a packed kid week, with a heat wave to plan around by Thursday. Here's the plan.

## Weather this week

Showery but mild this weekend (mid-70s to mid-80s), then it heats up fast — 89°F Tue, 91°F Wed, 94°F Thu with afternoon storm chances. Do the open-air stuff this weekend and weekday mornings; from Tuesday, hit the shaded and indoor options (zoo, marionette theater) by midday and bring water bottles.

## Fri Jun 26 — Zoo day + the carousel

The **Central Park Zoo** has **sea lion feedings** and **penguin feedings** today (check the daily board for times), plus **Zoo Quests** for older kids. The **Central Park Carousel** (near 65th St) spins **10 AM–6 PM**. And **FIFA Arena: Free Soccer in Central Park** is on at **Tavern on the Green (10 AM)** — free drop-in soccer for kids during the World Cup.

## Sat Jun 27 — Model boats + marionettes

Two classics: the **Central Park Model Yacht Club races** at **Conservatory Water (10 AM)** — kids love watching the little sailboats — and **'Little Red's Hood' at the Swedish Cottage Marionette Theatre** (a retelling of Little Red Riding Hood; reserve ahead). The **Farm Stand** is open at 10 AM, and the zoo feedings repeat. **Fishing at the Harlem Meer** (catch-and-release, poles provided) runs from the Dana Discovery Center.

## Sun Jun 28 — Davis Center + brunch music

**Events on The Green at the Davis Center** (the new Harlem-Meer pool/rink complex) has family programming, and there's **live music at Tavern on the Green's brunch (noon)**. Note: the south end is busy with the **Dreamland Pride** celebration at Rumsey Playfield in the afternoon — fun but crowded; the **north end and the zoo** stay easier with little ones.

## Mon Jun 29 – Thu Jul 2 — heat plan

Quieter on the calendar, hotter on the thermometer. Best family bets midweek: **morning zoo visits**, the **carousel** before noon, and the **Swedish Cottage** marionette shows (indoors, cool). The **40 in Focus photo exhibit** at the Arsenal (9 AM–5 PM, free) is an easy air-conditioned stop. Thursday hits **94°** — keep it short and shady.

## Quick recap

**Fri 6/26 — Zoo feedings + carousel + FIFA free soccer (Tavern):** classic kid day.

**Sat 6/27, 10 AM — Model yacht races (Conservatory Water) + marionettes (Swedish Cottage):** reserve the puppet show.

**Sun 6/28 — Davis Center programming + Tavern brunch music:** north end easier; Pride crowds the south.

**Mon–Thu — heat (up to 94°):** mornings, shade, and indoor stops (zoo, marionettes, Arsenal exhibit).

Have a fun (and hydrated) week,
The Central Park Guide
