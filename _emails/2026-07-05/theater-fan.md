# Central Park Stage Notes: Delirium Musicum at Naumburg — Mon Jul 6 – Sun Jul 12

Hi theater fans,

A quiet week for the park's stages — no Shakespeare on the Delacorte calendar yet — so the live performance to catch is **Delirium Musicum at the Naumburg Bandshell Tuesday**, a string collective that plays with real theatrical drama, plus a **tango at the Shakespeare statue Saturday evening**.

## Weather this week

Thunderstorms Monday and Tuesday in the mid-70s, clearing Wednesday, then hot into the low 90s Thursday and Friday and storm-flecked upper-80s over the weekend. Tuesday's Bandshell concert is the only outdoor show with a rain question — these performances rarely scrub and storms tend to clear by curtain, but glance at the radar before you go.

## Tue Jul 7, 7:30 PM — Delirium Musicum at the Naumburg Bandshell (free)

The week's marquee performance: **Delirium Musicum** takes the Bandshell on the Mall at 7:30 PM as part of the free **Naumburg Orchestral Concerts** series. This is an LA string collective known for programmatic, dramatically staged sets — closer to a theatrical event than a straight recital, and exactly the kind of programming this series does best. Bring a blanket and arrive early.

## Sat Jul 11, 6 PM — Tango at the Shakespeare statue

A small, lovely bit of park performance: **social tango at the Shakespeare statue on Literary Walk, 6–7 PM** — dancers taking over the Mall's south end beneath the Bard himself as the evening cools. Worth a stop if you're near Literary Walk; it's 87°, so the shaded elm allée is the place to be.

## Sat Jul 11, noon–6 PM — That Guitar Man From Central Park at Hernshead

For a performance-in-the-landscape afternoon, **"That Guitar Man From Central Park"** plays his long-running set at **Hernshead on the Lake, noon–6 PM** — a scenic, informal bit of live music theater on the water.

## On the horizon

**No Shakespeare in the Park run is posted for the Delacorte** in the current calendar — when the Public Theater announces its dates, this brief will lead with them. Until then, the Bandshell and the park's informal performers carry the stage.

## Quick recap

**Tue 7:30 PM — Delirium Musicum, Naumburg Bandshell (free):** the week's must-see performance.

**Sat 6 PM — Tango at the Shakespeare statue, Literary Walk.**

**Sat noon–6 PM — That Guitar Man at Hernshead.**

**Delacorte / Shakespeare in the Park — no dates posted yet;** we'll lead with them the moment they land.
