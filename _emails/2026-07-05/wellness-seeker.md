# Central Park Wellness Week: Mon Jul 6 – Sun Jul 12

Hi wellness seekers,

A week to move your practice to first light. The heat builds toward the weekend, so the calm hours are early — and there's a lovely, gentle bit of programming to know about: **Color and Chill at Harlem Meer on Saturday**, plus free dawn lap swim all week. Where you'll find solitude shifts midweek, when the concert crowds gather mid-park.

## Weather this week

Showers and thunderstorms Monday and Tuesday in the mid-70s, clearing Wednesday, then heat climbing to the low 90s Thursday and Friday and humid upper-80s over the weekend. Claim the **dawn hours Wednesday through Sunday** for anything outdoors — sunrise is a little after 5:30 and the light on the Reservoir and the Mall is at its best before the heat and humidity settle in around mid-morning.

## All week, 7–8:30 AM — free lap swim at Gottesman Pool

A quiet, meditative way to start a hot day: the **Gottesman Pool at Harlem Meer** opens for **free lap swim, 7–8:30 AM daily**. Early laps before the day-camp sessions arrive are about as close to solitude as moving water gets in July.

## Mon–Tue — the rain resets the park

Two soaking days aren't a loss for a slow practice — a walk through a rain-quiet **Ramble** or **North Woods** between cells is deeply restorative, and the crowds vanish. If you'd rather stay dry, this is a good week for indoor sitting and gentle movement, saving the outdoor practice for the clear midweek.

## Wed–Thu — dawn is yours; evenings belong to the crowds

The clear, cooler mornings are the week's contemplative peak — take them at **Summit Rock, the Conservatory Garden, or Hallett Nature Sanctuary**, the park's stillest corners. But note: **free SummerStage concerts fill Rumsey Playfield (mid-park at 72nd) both Wednesday and Thursday evenings**, so the center of the park gets loud and busy after 6 PM. For an evening wind-down, head **north of the 79th Street Transverse** — the Reservoir's north end, the Pool, and the North Woods stay calm.

## Sat Jul 11, 11 AM–4 PM — Color and Chill at Harlem Meer

The week's gentlest offering: **Color and Chill at Harlem Meer, 11 AM–4 PM** — an unhurried, mindful coloring-and-relaxation gathering by the water at the park's tranquil north end. Bring water; it's 87° and humid, so the shaded Meer edge is the spot.

## Sun Jul 12 — a slow, shaded morning

Close the week early and in the shade before the afternoon storm chance: the **Conservatory Garden** opens at 8 AM for its quietest hour, and the **Hallett Sanctuary** and **Wagner Cove** hold their calm through a humid 87° morning. Skip the busy southern lawns and keep to the water's edge.

## Quick recap

**All week, 7–8:30 AM — free lap swim at Gottesman Pool, Harlem Meer.**

**Sat 11 AM–4 PM — Color and Chill at Harlem Meer:** the week's slow, mindful gathering.

**Dawn Wed–Sun** is the contemplative window before the heat: Summit Rock, Conservatory Garden, Hallett Sanctuary.

**Wed & Thu evenings — concerts at mid-park Rumsey:** for quiet, head north of the 79th Street Transverse.

Breathe easy,
The Central Park Guide
