# Central Park Guide for Cyclists — Mon Jun 1 – Sun Jun 7

Hey cyclists — first week in a long stretch with zero charity walks, rides, or races confirmed in the loop's footprint. Three pristine weekday mornings (Mon–Wed) before the season's first 90°F heat dome lands Fri–Sat, and Sunday is the long-ride window before afternoon rain rolls in. (CRCA's Club Series may hold its usual Saturday dawn crit on East Drive — we couldn't confirm a Jun 6 race from CRCA's public calendar, so check crca.net before you roll out if you want to race or avoid the field.)

## Weather this week

Mon–Wed are calm and clear (73–80°F, picture-perfect riding), Thu starts warming (84°F), and Fri–Sat peak at **90°F** with Sun easing back to 84°F with afternoon rain showers possible. For cyclists: Mon–Wed AM is wide-open and ideal, Thu push earlier, **Fri–Sat is dawn-only with hydration and electrolytes**, and Sun a long ride before mid-morning rain is the play.

## Mon Jun 1 — Clean loop, no events on or near East/West Drive

Nothing in the loop's footprint today. East Meadow is closed all day for maintenance, but that doesn't touch the road. This is your best unhurried weekday morning of the week — long laps, paceline work, or a Bear Mountain warm-up. Engineers' Gate and 72nd St Cross Drive both quiet.

## Tue Jun 2 — Quiet midweek, ride early before the warmup

No loop-affecting events. Conditions roughly identical to Monday. If you've been waiting to test FTP or hit hill repeats on Harlem Hill, Tue AM is the window. Pedestrian congestion at Columbus Circle/Merchants' Gate will be normal weekday levels — nothing unusual.

## Wed Jun 3 — Last cool morning before the warming trend

Still calm on the loop. Temperatures climb to 80°F by afternoon, so push your ride to first light if you're going long. No drive closures, no permits crowding the staging areas. Wild West Playground Lawn is closed until 2 PM but that's nowhere near the road.

## Thu Jun 4 — Pivot day: 84°F, dawn rides start mattering

No events on the loop, but this is the day your "I'll just go after work" plan stops working for the week. Get out before 7 AM or accept the heat. East 106 St South Mount closed for maintenance until 2 PM — well off the loop, no impact on riding.

## Fri Jun 5 — Heat wave begins (90°F), dawn-only

No loop events, but the heat is the event. **Pre-7 AM is the only humane long-ride window** — the Reservoir loop and Bridle Path hold shade better than the road if you want to extend. Skip midday entirely. The Final Leg Torch Run runs along 7th Ave / Central Park South sidewalks 10 AM–noon — that's outside the park footprint, not a loop disruption, but expect spectator clusters at Merchants' Gate.

## Sat Jun 6 — Heat-wave peak (90°F): dawn or nothing

With Saturday at 90°F under full sun, **pre-7 AM is the only viable long-ride window** — a 5 AM rollout buys you the coolest air of the day on an empty loop. After 9 AM the heat curve steepens fast; by 10 you're cooking. CRCA's Club Series typically runs a Saturday dawn crit on East Drive during racing season — Jun 6 isn't confirmed on their public calendar, so verify at crca.net if you want to race or steer clear of the field; if there is a crit, the loop is racer-only on East Drive ~4–8:30 AM and clean by 9. Bowling Greens closed all day for maintenance (not loop-relevant, but a heads-up if you usually post-ride coffee on that side).

## Sun Jun 7 — Long ride before the rain

Cooler (84°F) with chance of afternoon showers — **Sunday morning is your long-ride window of the week**. Get the miles in before noon, ideally before 10 AM if the radar tightens up. No NYCC or club rides flagged in the loop calendar; East Meadow stays closed weekend but that's lawn-only. After ~1 PM the day becomes a coin flip for staying dry.

## Quick recap

- **Mon–Wed:** Cleanest weekday riding stretch in weeks. Take advantage.
- **Thu:** Warming trend kicks in — shift to early AM.
- **Fri–Sat:** First 90°F heat wave of 2026. Dawn rides only. Reservoir + Bridle Path for shade.
- **Sat:** Check crca.net for a possible dawn crit; otherwise pre-7 AM long ride is the only sane play at 90°F.
- **Sun AM:** Long ride before the afternoon rain showers move in.
- **No Affects-Loop events all week** — first quiet week in a long run.

Tailwinds,
Central Park Guide
