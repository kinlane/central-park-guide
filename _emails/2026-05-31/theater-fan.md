# Central Park Guide for Theater Fans — Mon Jun 1 – Sun Jun 7

Hey theater fans — this is the **Julius Caesar Part 2 week**. Six consecutive nights of free outdoor Shakespeare at The Pool Lawns, Tue 6/2 through Sun 6/7, 5–10 PM. Layered on top: **Barefoot Shakespeare opens its June show at Summit Rock** Thursday and runs Thu–Sun afternoons, so Thu/Fri/Sat/Sun each give you a Shakespeare double-header (afternoon at Summit Rock, evening at The Pool). Saturday brings a Shakespeare-adjacent bonus — **Tango at the Shakespeare Statue** on the Mall, 6–9 PM. Monday is the only fully dark night of the week.

## Weather this week

Mon–Wed are mild, low-humidity evenings — kind to outdoor curtains and audiences sitting on lawns for five hours. Thu warms; **Fri/Sat hit 90°F**, which makes Barefoot Shakespeare's 3–8 PM Summit Rock slot brutal in the afternoon sun — the 5–10 PM Julius Caesar window at The Pool is the kinder pick those two days, and Sun brings rain chances that could threaten the closing afternoons.

## Mon Jun 1 — Dark night, the only one this week

No theater programming in the park tonight. Use it to plan: Julius Caesar's six-night run starts tomorrow, and you'll want to pick at least two evenings — one early-week (mild) and one late-week. Swedish Cottage Marionette has nothing on the feed this week.

## Tue Jun 2, 5–10 PM — Julius Caesar Part 2 opens at The Pool Lawns

Opening night of the six-night run. Free outdoor Shakespeare on the upper west side around West 100th. 75°F, sunny, calm — the most pleasant evening of the week for a five-hour outdoor sit. Openers and closers carry the most energy; tonight's the opener.

## Wed Jun 3, 5–10 PM — Julius Caesar Part 2, night 2 at The Pool Lawns

Still mild at 80°F. Quieter midweek audience — easier to grab a good lawn spot if you arrive after 6. No competing theater in the park tonight.

## Thu Jun 4 — Double-header day: Barefoot Shakespeare opens + Julius Caesar continues

**3–8 PM — Barefoot Shakespeare Presents Our June 2026 Show @ Summit Rock.** Opening afternoon of Barefoot's four-show June run. Summit Rock is the highest natural point in the park, west side near 83rd — a stiff climb worth making. 84°F and sunny, so bring water and aim for the shaded west face of the rock.

**5–10 PM — Julius Caesar Part 2, night 3 @ The Pool Lawns.** Walks up nicely from Summit Rock for anyone doing both — about a mile north along the west side. First true double-header day of the week.

## Fri Jun 5 — Heat wave begins; evening Caesar is the kinder pick

**3–8 PM — Barefoot Shakespeare @ Summit Rock.** 90°F and mostly sunny. The 3 PM start is the rough part — Summit Rock has limited shade. If you go, drag it out at the back of the run and sit through the cooler 6–8 PM stretch.

**5–10 PM — Julius Caesar Part 2, night 4 @ The Pool Lawns.** This is the better Friday pick. The 5 PM start is warm but the evening cools off; by curtain on Act III the heat has broken. The Pool's surroundings hold cooler air than Summit Rock.

## Sat Jun 6 — Heat-wave peak, two Shakespeares, plus Tango at the Statue

**3–8 PM — Barefoot Shakespeare @ Summit Rock.** 90°F again, peak of the heat wave. Honestly, this is a tough one to recommend in the afternoon sun unless you're committed to the full run.

**5–10 PM — Julius Caesar Part 2, night 5 @ The Pool Lawns.** Same logic as Friday — the evening slot is the humane Shakespeare on a 90° day.

**6–9 PM — Tango at the Shakespeare Statue @ Shakespeare Statue Plaza, the Mall.** Shakespeare-adjacent outdoor performance: free tango social staged at the Bard's statue at the south end of the Mall. Worth a walk-through between Barefoot's curtain and Caesar's middle acts if you're in marathon-Shakespeare mode, or worth making the centerpiece of an evening on its own.

## Sun Jun 7 — Closing afternoon for Barefoot, closing night for Julius Caesar

**3–8 PM — Barefoot Shakespeare closing show @ Summit Rock.** 84°F and a chance of rain showers — watch the radar; outdoor Shakespeare and afternoon storms don't mix. If it holds, this is the last chance to catch Barefoot's June piece.

**5–10 PM — Julius Caesar Part 2 closing night @ The Pool Lawns.** Six-night run wraps. Closing nights usually run hot; go.

## Quick recap

- **Julius Caesar Part 2** — six nights, Tue 6/2 → Sun 6/7, 5–10 PM, The Pool Lawns. Free.
- **Barefoot Shakespeare June show** — four afternoons, Thu 6/4 → Sun 6/7, 3–8 PM, Summit Rock. Free.
- **Tango at the Shakespeare Statue** — Sat 6/6, 6–9 PM, the Mall. Free Shakespeare-adjacent outdoor performance.
- **Mon 6/1** — the week's only dark night.
- **Weather note:** Fri/Sat 90°F afternoons make Summit Rock punishing; The Pool's evening slot is the kinder Shakespeare those days.
- **Delacorte Shakespeare in the Park 2026** — dates not yet announced; you'll see them here first when they land.
- **Swedish Cottage Marionette** — no programming on the feed this week.

Curtain up at sundown,
