# Central Park Guide for Families — Mon Jun 1 – Sun Jun 7

Hi families — this is a Saturday-stacked week. CP Sports Summer Program runs weekday mornings and afternoons at South Dead Road, then Saturday turns into a five-event family pile-up between Conservatory Water, Levin Playground, The Dairy, Hernshead, and Diana Ross. Sunday brings a Sicilian community festival at Cherry Hill. The big caveat: the season's first heat wave lands Friday and Saturday at 90°F, so the smart play is morning-only outdoor time on those two days and the Heckscher Sprayground for cooling off.

## Weather this week

Three pristine days Mon–Wed (73–80°F sunny), warming Thursday, then **90°F Friday and Saturday** as the first heat wave of 2026 hits. Plan park trips for before 11 AM Fri/Sat, keep the Heckscher Playground Sprayground on standby, and Sunday cools back to 84°F with a chance of showers.

## Mon Jun 1 — CP Sports Summer Program kicks off at South Dead Road

The summer youth-sports program starts its weekday run today and goes through Friday: Session A 8:30 AM–noon, Session B 12:30–4 PM at South Dead Road. If your kid is enrolled, this is the rhythm for the next five days. East Meadow is closed all day for maintenance, so steer pickup picnics toward Cedar Hill or Sheep Meadow instead. Otherwise a calm 73°F afternoon — Heckscher and Adventure Playground will be busy in the after-school window.

## Tue Jun 2 — CP Sports continues, classic park afternoon

Same weekday cadence at South Dead Road (A 8:30–noon, B 12:30–4 PM). 75°F and sunny, no closures of note — a good day for Conservatory Water sailboat-renting or a Ramble walk after pickup.

## Wed Jun 3 — Wild West Playground lawn closed until 2 PM

CP Sports continues at South Dead Road. The Wild West Playground Lawn (west side, mid-park around 81st) is closed for maintenance until 2 PM, so route morning playground trips to Diana Ross, Mariners', or Rudin instead. The playground equipment itself should be fine — it's the lawn around it that's roped off. 80°F and sunny, warming trend begins.

## Thu Jun 4 — Last pristine day before the heat

CP Sports A + B at South Dead Road. East 106th Street South Mount closed until 2 PM (north-end maintenance, only matters if you were headed up there). 84°F and sunny — last comfortable afternoon before Friday's heat dome lands.

## Fri Jun 5 — Heat wave begins, morning-only outdoor plans

90°F, mostly sunny — the first 90°F day of 2026. CP Sports still runs at South Dead Road but the kids will be cooking by Session B, so hydrate hard and pack extra water. For everyone else: morning park trips only. The Heckscher Playground Sprayground is your cooling-off play, and shaded picks like the Ramble, the Conservatory Garden, and the Bethesda Terrace arcade hold up better than open lawns. Skip midday Sheep Meadow and the Great Lawn unless you're committed to a tree-line picnic.

## Sat Jun 6 — Five family events stack up (and it's still 90°F)

This is the family headliner. **Get there by 10 AM** — by noon it's brutal.

- **9 AM–3 PM — Model Yacht Racing @ Kerbs Boathouse / Conservatory Water.** Classic Central Park morning. Get there early — the 9–11 AM window is the comfortable one.
- **9 AM–11 AM — Tot Shabbat @ lawn north of Levin Playground.** Outdoor service geared to little kids; finishes before the worst heat.
- **Noon–3 PM — Sing for Hope Choir Fest @ The Dairy.** Free community choir performance in a partly-shaded spot.
- **Noon–6 PM — That Guitar Man From Central Park @ Hernshead.** Lakeside set, west-side shade.
- **Noon–2 PM — South Asian Summer Social @ Diana Ross Playground Lawn.** Cultural community gathering with food and music.

Closures to route around: **East Meadow closed all day** (weekend closure) and **Bowling Greens closed all day** for maintenance. Heat-adapt: Heckscher Sprayground from 11 AM on, frozen-grape-and-water-bottle picnic strategy, and call it by 1 PM unless you're sticking to shade.

## Sun Jun 7 — Sicily. Lands in Hands at Cherry Hill

- **10 AM–1 PM — Sicily. Lands in Hands @ Cherry Hill.** Sicilian-American community festival at one of the park's prettiest picnic spots (just west of Bethesda Terrace). Family-friendly, music + food + culture.
- **1 PM–3 PM — Elope-a-thon "Love in Color" @ Cop Cot.** Worth flagging as a curiosity: it's a public micro-wedding event for queer-affirming couples held at the rustic shelter on the south end. Not a kid event per se, but if you're already wandering the south end, it's a sweet scene to watch from a distance — explain to kids what they're seeing.

84°F, chance of afternoon showers — the heat breaks but bring a rain layer. East Meadow still closed.

## Quick recap

- **Sat 6/6** is the family headliner: **Model Yacht Racing, Tot Shabbat, Sing for Hope, That Guitar Man, South Asian Summer Social** — five free family events stacked. Arrive by 10 AM before the 90°F kicks in.
- **Sun 6/7 10 AM–1 PM:** Sicilian community festival at Cherry Hill.
- **Mon–Fri:** CP Sports Summer Program at South Dead Road (Session A 8:30–noon, B 12:30–4 PM).
- **Wed 6/3:** Wild West Playground Lawn closed until 2 PM — route morning trips to Diana Ross / Mariners' / Rudin instead.
- **Heat-wave call:** Fri + Sat 90°F. Morning park trips only; Heckscher Sprayground is the cooling-off plan.
- **Closed all day Saturday:** East Meadow + Bowling Greens. East Meadow also closed Sun + Mon.

Pack the water bottles,
— Central Park Guide
