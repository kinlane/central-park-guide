# Central Park Picnic Intel — Sat Jun 13 – Fri Jun 19

Picnickers — apologies for the Saturday send; this usually reaches you Friday night. Here's the lawn picture: East Meadow is closed Saturday and Sunday, Cherry Hill is closed through Sunday, and Tuesday brings Cedar Hill and Pilgrim Hill closures. The weekend is hot, but **Monday through Wednesday turns cool and gorgeous (upper 70s)** — the best blanket weather in weeks. Save the big spread for midweek.

## Weather this week

89°F Saturday, 88°F Sunday, 79°F Monday, 79°F Tuesday, 78°F Wednesday, 85°F Thursday (thunderstorms likely), 85°F Friday. Blankets down before 11 AM and find shade this weekend; Monday–Wednesday you can stay out all afternoon in comfort. Thursday's thunderstorms are a wash — pack a backup plan.

## Sat 6/13 — East Meadow and Cherry Hill closed; Sheep Meadow or the Great Lawn

Both East Meadow and Cherry Hill are closed. **Sheep Meadow** (west, 66th–69th) is wide open — claim a shaded edge by 10 AM. The **Great Lawn** is accessible but the east diamonds have Little League traffic. Columbus Circle Food Market is open for provisions. Heads-up: the Martinez Brothers SummerStage show (Rumsey, 6–10 PM) pulls crowds to the east 72nd Street area from 4 PM — keep your blanket away from that stretch.

## Sun 6/14 — Puerto Rican Day Parade on the east side; picnic west

The **Puerto Rican Day Parade** crowds East Drive and the Central Park West sidewalk through midday, so the east-side lawns and approaches are hectic — set up west. **Sheep Meadow** is the clean choice again; East Meadow and Cherry Hill remain closed. Folk Dancers at King Jagiello (11 AM–9 PM) and the BBBC Book Exchange at the Great Lawn SW lawn (5–7 PM) are ambient, not conflicts. Storms arrive evening — plan a lunch picnic, not a sunset one.

## Mon 6/15 — Best day starts (79°F): Cherry Hill and East Meadow reopen

Both Cherry Hill and East Meadow reopen, the heat is gone, and the calendar is clean until evening — the week's best picnic day, and the front of a three-day cool stretch. **Cherry Hill fountain lawn** is one of the park's nicest spreads and it'll be fresh after maintenance. Stake a spot by late morning; no need to chase shade at 79°F.

## Tue 6/16 — Cedar Hill and Pilgrim Hill closed; Mineral Springs open

**Cedar Hill** (79th, east) and **Pilgrim Hill** (72nd) are closed for maintenance. **Mineral Springs Pavilion** (67th, west) is unaffected — north end of Sheep Meadow, food kiosk, good shade. Another 79°F beauty. The 40-in-Focus Opening Reception at the Arsenal (6–8 PM) is a nearby post-picnic stop.

## Wed 6/17 — 78°F, the cool-stretch finale

The coolest day — an all-afternoon picnic is on. Sheep Meadow, the Great Lawn, or a reopened Cherry Hill all work; late-afternoon showers are possible, so keep an eye on the sky after 4 PM. Wellness at the Park (Great Lawn north, 9 AM–1 PM) is a low-key backdrop.

## Thu 6/18 — Thunderstorms; skip the spread

85°F and thunderstorms likely — not a picnic day. Columbus Circle Food Market is open if you want provisions to take home, but the lawns are a gamble.

## Fri 6/19 — Juneteenth near West Drive (1–3 PM)

The West Drive corridor between 82nd and 86th Street is the Juneteenth commemoration zone, 1–3 PM (with a Hunters' Gate gathering on the western edge). Keep a respectful distance if you picnic nearby; the lawns toward the Reservoir in that corridor are open but busy. Quiet alternative: **Dene Lawn** (east, 68th) is underused. Warm at 85°F and breezy.

## Quick recap

- **East Meadow and Cherry Hill:** closed Sat–Sun, both reopen Mon 6/15.
- **Cedar Hill and Pilgrim Hill:** closed Tue 6/16.
- **Mon–Wed is the week** — 78–79°F, key lawns open, clean calendar.
- **Sun 6/14:** Puerto Rican Day Parade crowds the east side — picnic west (Sheep Meadow).
- **Mineral Springs Pavilion:** Tuesday fallback with an on-site food kiosk.
