# Central Park Cycling Heads-Up — Sat Jun 13 – Fri Jun 19

Hey cyclists — apologies, this usually goes out Friday night and I'm a day behind, so here it is Saturday. It still lands in time for the one you really need to know: **Sunday is a write-off on the east side.** CRCA races East Drive at dawn and the Puerto Rican Day Parade takes the east side through midday. Saturday's hot, then Monday–Wednesday turns gorgeous and cool — that's your week.

## Weather this week

89°F Saturday, 88°F Sunday, 79°F Monday, 79°F Tuesday, 78°F Wednesday, 85°F Thursday (thunderstorms likely), 85°F Friday. Hot and sunny through the weekend with Sunday-evening storms, then a beautiful upper-70s stretch Monday–Wednesday before Thursday's thunderstorms. Save your long rides and intervals for the midweek cool-down.

## Sat 6/13 — Clean drives, 89°F; Central Park 5K on the north cross drive 9 AM

No drive closures for the loop, but the **Central Park 5K** stages on the 102nd Street Cross Drive at 9 AM — give the north transverse a wide berth mid-morning. The Martinez Brothers SummerStage show (Rumsey, 6–10 PM) sends a pedestrian surge across East Drive near 72nd around 10:30 PM. Ride before 10 AM in the heat, or after the show clears. East Meadow closed all day.

## Sun 6/14 — CRCA crit 5:30 AM, then Puerto Rican Day Parade on East Drive

The week's big one. **CRCA Central Park Racing Series** owns East Drive from 5:30 AM — if you're not racing it, that's fast club traffic on the loop early; ride West Drive or wait it out. Then the **Puerto Rican Day Parade** stages and disperses across East Drive and the Central Park West sidewalk through midday, so the full loop is unreliable from mid-morning on. Get your laps before 7 AM or skip the loop entirely Sunday. Storms move in by evening.

## Mon 6/15 — Best day starts here (79°F, clean loop)

Heat's gone: 79°F, drying out, nothing on the drives. This is the front edge of the week's good stretch — Monday through Wednesday is open, cool, and quiet. Long ride or intervals, your pick. 40-in-Focus opens at the Arsenal (9 AM); foot traffic at the Fifth Ave entrance stays off the drives.

## Tue 6/16 — 79°F, drives clear

Another clean, cool one — mostly sunny, light wind. Bowling Greens, Cedar Hill, and Pilgrim Hill closed for maintenance, all off the loop. Julius Caesar foot traffic near 100th & West Drive builds after 4:30 PM; the 40-in-Focus opening reception (Arsenal, 6–8 PM) draws pedestrians to Fifth Ave, not the drives.

## Wed 6/17 — 78°F, drives open, last cool day

Coolest day of the week — take the long ride before the weather turns. Showers possible late afternoon, so go morning or midday. Wellness at the Park (9 AM–1 PM, Great Lawn) is off the drives.

## Thu 6/18 — 85°F, thunderstorms likely

Genuinely wet — thunderstorms are the call. Dawn ride before the cells build, or rest day. Roads will be slick if you do get out.

## Fri 6/19 — 85°F, breezy, Juneteenth on West Drive 1–3 PM

**Juneteenth in Seneca Village** puts a permit on West Drive near 82nd–86th Street, 1–3 PM, plus the Hunters' Gate gathering on the western edge — pedestrian presence and possible staging in that stretch. Ride the morning, be off West Drive before noon. West wind 12–15 mph means a headwind on the west side climbs and a push down the east.

## Quick recap

- **Sun 6/14: avoid the loop** — CRCA crit East Drive 5:30 AM, Puerto Rican Day Parade east side through midday. Laps before 7 AM only.
- **Sat 6/13:** Central Park 5K on the 102nd St Cross Drive 9 AM; SummerStage surge near 72nd from 10:30 PM.
- **Mon–Wed: the week** — 78–79°F, full loop, no conflicts. Long rides go here.
- **Thu 6/18:** thunderstorms likely — dawn or skip.
- **Fri 6/19:** Juneteenth on West Drive 1–3 PM — morning ride only.

Tailwinds,
— Central Park Guide
