# Central Park Sports — Sat Jun 13 – Fri Jun 19

Sports fans — apologies for the late drop; this usually lands Friday night and slipped to Saturday. Big competition weekend: the **Central Park 5K** Saturday morning, the **CRCA crit** on East Drive Sunday at dawn, plus the **NY State Golf Croquet Championship** wrapping Saturday and Model Yacht Racing on Conservatory Water. Baseball and softball leagues run all week; the cool midweek air is a gift for field play.

## Weather this week

89°F Saturday, 88°F Sunday, 79°F Monday, 79°F Tuesday, 78°F Wednesday, 85°F Thursday (thunderstorms likely), 85°F Friday. Hot weekend, then a beautiful upper-70s window Monday–Wednesday for extended field sessions; Thursday's thunderstorms will wash out evening games.

## Sat 6/13 — Central Park 5K, Croquet finale, Model Yacht Racing, full league slate

**2026 Central Park 5K** stages on the 102nd Street Cross Drive at 9 AM. **NY State Golf Croquet Championship** final day at Bowling Green Lawn (9 AM–3 PM) is worth watching — croquet at competitive pace — with Primavera Pairs on the same green. **Model Yacht Racing** at Kerbs Boathouse (9 AM–3 PM): fast, compact RC-sailboat racing on Conservatory Water. Kickball, adult softball, and Little League baseball (12U and 13+) run across Heckscher Fields and the Great Lawn diamonds. East Meadow closed all day.

## Sun 6/14 — CRCA crit on East Drive 5:30 AM; leagues continue

**CRCA Central Park Racing Series** races East Drive from 5:30 AM — fast club crit racing on the loop, fun to watch at the 72nd–90th Street stretch before the heat. Note the **Puerto Rican Day Parade** takes the east side later in the morning, so get your spectating in early. Adult softball and Little League baseball run across Heckscher and the Great Lawn complex; East Meadow closure carries over.

## Mon 6/15 — 79°F: full field availability

CP Sports Summer Program opens for the week (two field slots daily, one morning, one afternoon, through Thursday). Adult softball and baseball resume. The best field-play weather in weeks — 79°F and dry — so extended sessions are comfortable all day.

## Tue 6/16 — Bowling Greens closed for maintenance

**Bowling Greens closed all day.** If you had a lawn-bowling or croquet booking, it's out. Adult softball, baseball, and kickball are unaffected on Heckscher and the Great Lawn diamonds. Another 79°F day — ideal.

## Wed 6/17 — 78°F, CP Sports midweek

CP Sports Summer Program at two slots; adult softball, baseball, kickball continuing. Coolest day of the week for play, though late-afternoon showers are possible — get games in by mid-afternoon.

## Thu 6/18 — 85°F, thunderstorms likely

Thunderstorms are the forecast — expect evening leagues to be called. Morning field windows may hold; check before you head out. Columbus Circle Food Market is operating for provisions.

## Fri 6/19 — Leagues continue; Bowling Greens reopen (confirm); Juneteenth on West Drive

Adult softball and baseball across Heckscher and the Great Lawn diamonds. Bowling Greens maintenance should be complete — confirm if you have a booking. **Juneteenth in Seneca Village** (1–3 PM, West Drive / Hunters' Gate) is up on the west side and doesn't touch the eastern and southern fields.
