# Central Park Music — Sat Jun 13 – Fri Jun 19

Music fans — apologies for the Saturday arrival; this normally lands Friday night. The headliner is tonight: **The Martinez Brothers** at SummerStage. A Cappella in the Park, Tango at the Shakespeare Statue, Sunday's Folk Dancers, and a SummerStage festival footprint running through Friday fill out a full week — and after a hot weekend, the evenings turn beautifully cool midweek.

## Weather this week

89°F Saturday, 88°F Sunday, 79°F Monday, 79°F Tuesday, 78°F Wednesday, 85°F Thursday (thunderstorms likely), 85°F Friday. Daytime is hot through Sunday, then comfortable; evening shows are easy all week except Thursday, when thunderstorms threaten the outdoor program.

## Sat 6/13 — Martinez Brothers at SummerStage (ticketed) + A Cappella + Tango

The headliner: **The Martinez Brothers / Wakyin / Luna Mar** at Rumsey Playfield, 6–10 PM, ticketed — Bronx house royalty; if you're holding tickets, arrive by 5:30 PM to beat the 72nd Street entrance crush. Free during the day: **A Cappella in the Park** at King Jagiello Plaza (1–5 PM). At 6 PM, **Tango at the Shakespeare Statue Plaza** (6–9 PM) is a gorgeous outdoor-dance alternative if Rumsey's sold out. NY Classical's *Julius Caesar* at The Pool adds live evening staging — theatrical, but the atmosphere carries.

## Sun 6/14 — International Folk Dancers, King Jagiello Plaza (11 AM–9 PM)

**Central Park International Folk Dancers** take King Jagiello Plaza for a long, free, participatory session (11 AM–9 PM) — Greek circle dances to Balkan sets, reliably eclectic. The BBBC Book Exchange at the Great Lawn SW lawn (5–7 PM) is a quiet ambient backdrop if you're nearby. Evening storms move in, so the afternoon is your window.

## Mon 6/15 — SummerStage Festival operating; 79°F

SummerStage Festival footprint active at Rumsey Playfield and Bandshell Plaza — check the program for confirmed acts (the permit runs into the night). The best evening weather of the week at 79°F: perfect for whatever's on the Rumsey stage.

## Tue 6/16 — 40-in-Focus Opening Reception (Arsenal, free, 6–8 PM); GSB and Friends at the Bandshell

The **Opening Reception for 40 in Focus: SummerStage through the Lens of Photographer Jack Vartoogian** (Arsenal, 6–8 PM, free) is a music-history document as much as a photo show — four decades of SummerStage on the walls. Earlier, **GSB and Friends** plays Bandshell Plaza (4 PM). SummerStage Festival continues at Rumsey; *Julius Caesar* at The Pool from evening.

## Wed 6/17 — SummerStage Festival; 78°F

SummerStage Festival running again, with the coolest evening of the week for an outdoor show. Wellness at the Park (9 AM–1 PM) is off the concert circuit. Watch for late-afternoon showers, but evening should clear.

## Thu 6/18 — SummerStage Festival, thunderstorms likely

SummerStage Festival is on the calendar, but thunderstorms are likely — check for weather holds before heading out, and bring a backup if the outdoor show is paused.

## Fri 6/19 — SummerStage Festival, Juneteenth

SummerStage Festival closes out the week. **Juneteenth in Seneca Village** (1–3 PM, West Drive / Hunters' Gate) often carries live cultural performance — check the listing for programming. NY Classical's *Julius Caesar* at The Pool in the evening, likely the run's final night.

## Quick recap

- **Sat 6/13:** Martinez Brothers at Rumsey, 6–10 PM — TICKETED. Plus free A Cappella (1–5 PM) and Tango (6–9 PM).
- **Sun 6/14:** International Folk Dancers, King Jagiello Plaza, 11 AM–9 PM — free.
- **Tue 6/16:** 40-in-Focus Opening Reception (Arsenal, 6–8 PM, free); GSB and Friends at the Bandshell (4 PM).
