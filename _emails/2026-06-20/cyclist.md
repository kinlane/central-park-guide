# Central Park Cycling Heads-Up — Sat Jun 20 – Fri Jun 26

*Quick note: this edition usually lands Friday night — I let it slip a day again, so here it is Saturday morning, covering Sat Jun 20 – Fri Jun 26.*

Hey cyclists — the drives are open all week. No races on the loop, no charity rides, no closures on East, West, or Center Drive. Your only real variables are the weather and a couple of pedestrian-congestion spots at the southwest corner.

## Weather this week

A sunny, dry weekend (Sat 84°, Sun 83° with a late-day storm risk) gives way to a wet Mon–Tue (showers and thunderstorms, 77°/80°), Wednesday clears to a flawless 84° and sunny, and Thu–Fri stay warm (80°/83°) with scattered shower chances. No heat to fight — Wednesday is the standout ride day; Mon–Tue, watch the radar and the slick pavement.

## Sat 6/20 — Clean drives, sunny dawn-loop day

84° and sunny, nothing on the drives. Get your early laps in before the pedestrian and tourist load builds — by mid-morning the loop fills with a clear, warm Saturday's worth of foot traffic.

## Sun 6/21 — Pedestrian cluster at the 72nd St Cross Drive, 9–11 AM

The **Mile Square Schlep Run stages on the 72nd Street Cross Drive and Bandshell Plaza, 9–11 AM**. The drives stay open, but expect a knot of runners and spectators crossing mid-park — ease off through the 72nd St transverse and Bandshell stretch during that window, or ride early before it sets up. 83° with an afternoon storm risk, so morning is the move.

## Mon 6/22 — Wet drives, congestion at Columbus Circle

Showers and thunderstorms, 77° — slick descents on the West Drive sweep and into the bottom of the loop, so brake early. **FIFA World Cup bus operations stage at Columbus Circle from 6 AM**, pushing extra vehicles and pedestrians around Merchants' Gate. Give the southwest entrance room.

## Tue 6/23 — Wet drives, calm otherwise

Rain showers likely, 80°. Open loop, no events touching the drives. The Naumburg concert draws an evening crowd to the Bandshell, but spectators stay off the roadway. A quiet, wet spin if you want it.

## Wed 6/24 — The week's best ride: clean and sunny

84° and sunny, drives wide open. **This is the day to grab a long ride** or your interval session — the one dry, clean window all week. Only an evening "Media Challenge" on the Lower Loop at 7 PM, well after a morning or midday ride.

## Thu 6/25 — Open loop, FIFA buses return

80° with a storm chance. Drives clear. **FIFA World Cup buses are back at Columbus Circle at 6 AM** — same Merchants' Gate congestion as Monday. Plan your entrance accordingly.

## Fri 6/26 — Open loop, normal Friday

83° with a chance of showers and no loop impact. Ride the dry windows; nothing structural between you and a clean lap.

## Quick recap

- **Clean drives all week** — no races, rides, or closures on East/West/Center.
- **Wed 6/24 is your ride** — 84°, sunny, the only dry-and-clean day in a wet stretch.
- **Sun 6/21, 9–11 AM:** runner cluster on the 72nd St Cross Drive (Mile Square Schlep Run) — ease through mid-park or ride early.
- **Mon & Thu, 6 AM:** FIFA World Cup buses at Columbus Circle — congestion around Merchants' Gate.
- **Mon–Tue wet** (slick descents); **Thu–Fri unsettled** — radar before you roll.

Tailwinds,
