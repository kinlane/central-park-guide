# Central Park Sports — Sat Jun 20 – Fri Jun 26

*Quick note: this edition usually lands Friday night — I let it slip a day again, so here it is Saturday morning, covering Sat Jun 20 – Fri Jun 26.*

Hey sports fans — a full week of league play with the season's usual three-field rhythm (North Meadow, Great Lawn, Heckscher), one fun run on Sunday, and model-yacht racing on Saturday. Thursday is the busiest day on the fields; the only thing that shifts schedules is the Mon–Tue rain.

## Weather this week

Sunny and dry for the weekend (Sat 84°, Sun 83° with a small afternoon storm risk), then showers and thunderstorms Mon–Tue (77°/80°) that could soften fields and trigger rainouts, a clear 84° Wednesday, and warm, unsettled Thu–Fri (80°/83°). Expect Mon–Tue games to be the ones at risk; check with your league before heading out those two days.

## Sat 6/20 — Busy fields, plus model-yacht racing

49 permits across the fields — **North Meadow (22), Great Lawn softball (13), and Heckscher (13)** all packed under a sunny 84°. For something different, **Model Yacht Racing runs on Conservatory Water at Kerbs Boathouse from 9 AM** — a great watch. The East Meadow is closed all weekend, so nothing's staged there.

**The Mile Square Schlep Run goes off at the 72nd Street Cross Drive, 9–11 AM** (Runs & Races) — the week's one road event. On the fields, another full slate: **North Meadow (23) and Heckscher (18)** carry the day, Great Lawn lighter at 6. 83°, get the morning games in before the afternoon storm chance.

## Mon 6/22 — Rain threatens a 60-permit slate

60 permits booked — **North Meadow (21), Heckscher (19), Great Lawn (18)** — but showers and thunderstorms (77°) put Monday's games most at risk of the week. Confirm before you go; wet-field cancellations are likely.

## Tue 6/23 — Still wet, heavy bookings

63 permits — **North Meadow (23), Great Lawn (20), Heckscher (18)** — under rain showers (80°). Same story as Monday: the schedule is loaded but the sky may have the final say.

## Wed 6/24 — Clear skies, clean slate

84° and sunny — the best playing day of the week. 61 permits (**North Meadow 22, Great Lawn 21, Heckscher 16**) on dry fields. If your league got rained out Mon–Tue, this is the makeup day everyone will want.

## Thu 6/25 — The week's busiest day

**71 permits — the peak — led by North Meadow (30) and Great Lawn (23)**, with Heckscher at 16. 80° with a chance of storms; if it stays dry, the fields are at maximum capacity. Get there early for any walk-on space.

## Fri 6/26 — Lighter close
