# Central Park Sports This Week — Sat Jun 6 – Fri Jun 12

Hey sports fans — Mastercard Mini 10K shuts East Drive Saturday morning, softball and baseball are at full summer volume across all meadow complexes, and the NY State Golf Croquet Championship takes Bowling Green on Friday. Monday at 79°F is the week's most comfortable day for any athletic activity.

## Weather this week

93°F Saturday, 87°F Sunday, 79°F Monday, 85°F Tuesday, 90°F Wednesday, 92°F Thursday and Friday. Heat management is the story for every outdoor sport this week — early starts are essential.

## Sat 6/6 — Mastercard Mini 10K: East Drive closed 8–10:30 AM (93°F)

Hard-include: the **Mastercard Mini 10K** races East Drive 8–10:30 AM (affects_loop: true). East Drive is closed to all other activity during the race. Field access via north entrances and the mid-park transverses is unaffected.

**Saturday's sports footprint:**

## Sun 6/7 — Clean field day, 87°F

East Drive and West Drive clear. Baseball and softball continue across all meadow complexes. East Meadow closure carries over. 87°F — games starting at 8–9 AM are far more comfortable than those at noon.

## Mon 6/8 — Lightest day, best conditions (79°F)

79°F and sunny — the week's most comfortable day for athletic activity. CP Sports Summer Program runs weekday activations near E. 97th and W. 96th Streets. Otherwise lighter on adult league permits — good day for tennis or pickup.

## Tue 6/9 — Full weekday schedule (85°F)

Full softball adult and baseball schedule for the midweek. Tennis courts at full summer booking. Kickball rosters active. 85°F — manageable with water and early starts.
