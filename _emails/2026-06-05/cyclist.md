# Central Park Cycling Heads-Up — Sat Jun 6 – Fri Jun 12

Hey cyclists — Mini 10K closes East Drive Saturday morning, 93°F follows it up the same day, and Monday at 79°F is the week's open window. Plan around those three facts.

## Weather this week

93°F Saturday, 87°F Sunday, 79°F Monday, 85°F Tuesday, 90°F Wednesday, 92°F Thursday and Friday. Ride early in the week; every day after Monday requires heat management.

## Sat 6/6 — Mastercard Mini 10K: East Drive closed 8–10:30 AM (93°F)

Hard-include: **Mini 10K** races East Drive from 8 AM through roughly 10:30 AM. East Drive is inaccessible for cycling during the race. Your window is before 7:30 AM — get on and be done before setup intensifies. After 10:30 AM the course reopens but 93°F and post-race pedestrian crowds near 72nd Street make riding unpleasant. Bowling Green Lawn and East Meadow both closed all day.

## Sun 6/7 — Clean loop, 87°F

No race, no drive closures. Dawn riding (before 7 AM) gives clean pavement and manageable air. West Drive tends to stay cooler than East Drive on hot mornings. East Meadow closure carries over.

## Mon 6/8 — Best day of the week (79°F, clear loop)

79°F, sunny, no events on the drives. Long ride or intervals — do it Monday. DJ Ali Bandshell Rave 3–7 PM has zero loop impact.

## Tue 6/9 — Clean loop, 85°F

Morning ride. Naumburg evening crowd arrives by 7 PM on central paths — no drive impact.

## Wed 6/10 — 90°F, NY Phil pre-event congestion near Great Lawn

NY Phil Great Lawn concert at 8 PM draws 60,000+. People start arriving from early afternoon; the area between Rumsey Playfield and the Great Lawn fills from ~4 PM. Drives remain open, but expect higher pedestrian spillover than a normal Wednesday. Ride in the morning.

## Thu 6/11 — 92°F, SummerStage at Rumsey 6–10 PM

Rumsey area occupied all evening, drives clear. Pre-dawn ride only in 92°F heat.

## Fri 6/12 — 92°F, Carlos Konig Salsa at Bandshell 5–8 PM

Salsa at the Bandshell is off the drives. Same 92°F — early morning only. **40-in-Focus exhibit opens at the Arsenal** (9 AM–5 PM) if you want a reason to stop after a dawn ride.

## Quick recap

- **Sat 6/6: East Drive closed 8–10:30 AM** for Mini 10K — ride before 7:30 AM or skip.
- **Mon 6/8 is the week** — 79°F, open drives, no conflicts.
- **Wed 6/10:** NY Phil crowd builds in the afternoon — drives stay open, pedestrian spillover increases.
- **Fri 6/12:** 40-in-Focus opens at Arsenal — a post-ride stop worth noting.

Tailwinds,
