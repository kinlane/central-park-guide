# Central Park Running Heads-Up — Sat Jun 6 – Fri Jun 12

Hey runners — the Mastercard Mini 10K shuts East Drive Saturday morning and 93°F arrives the same day. Monday at 79°F is the one clean, comfortable window of the week. Every other day is heat management.

## Weather this week

93°F Saturday, 87°F Sunday, 79°F Monday, 85°F Tuesday, 90°F Wednesday, 92°F Thursday, 92°F Friday. The heat doesn't let up after Monday — front-load any quality work early in the week.

## Sat 6/6 — Mastercard Mini 10K: East Drive closed 8–10:30 AM (93°F)

Hard-include: the **Mastercard Mini 10K** races East Drive starting 8 AM through roughly 10:30 AM. East Drive is closed for training during that window. With 93°F arriving by midday, the only viable option is pre-dawn — get on the loop before 6 AM, finish by 7:30, beat both the race setup and the heat. The course clears after 11 AM but the temperature makes afternoon running genuinely unsafe. East Meadow closed all day.

## Sun 6/7 — Clean loop, 87°F

No loop events. Dawn start (before 7 AM) for the cooler air and shade on the Bridle Path and Reservoir track. East Meadow closure carries over.

## Mon 6/8 — Best day of the week (79°F, clean loop)

This is the day. 79°F, sunny, nothing on the drives. Long run, quality session, or anything that requires full effort — do it Monday. The DJ Ali Bandshell Rave runs 3–7 PM but has no loop impact.

## Tue 6/9 — Clean loop, 85°F

Still manageable in the morning. Baroklyn / Naumburg concert at 7:30 PM brings an evening crowd to the Bandshell area but doesn't touch the drives. Run in the morning.

## Wed 6/10 — 90°F, NY Phil crowd near Great Lawn from afternoon

90°F. The NY Philharmonic plays the Great Lawn at 8 PM — crowd assembles from around 3–4 PM, filling the central-park paths between Rumsey Playfield and the Great Lawn. Drives stay clear; run in the morning before the crowd builds.

## Thu 6/11 — 92°F, SummerStage at Rumsey 6–10 PM

Yellowcard at SummerStage doesn't touch the loop drives. 92°F demands pre-dawn running only — or skip to Friday morning.

## Fri 6/12 — 92°F, Carlos Konig Salsa at Bandshell 5–8 PM

Same 92°F ceiling with a slight shower chance. The Salsa at Bandshell (5–8 PM) and Julius Caesar at The Pool Lawns (5–10 PM) are both off the loop. Pre-dawn or early-morning only. The **40-in-Focus exhibit opens at the Arsenal** today if you want a post-run reason to stop in.

## Quick recap

- **Sat 6/6: Mini 10K shuts East Drive 8–10:30 AM** — run before 6 AM or skip Saturday.
- **Sat 6/6 also hits 93°F** — first heat wave; pre-dawn or don't.
- **Mon 6/8 is the week** — 79°F, clean loop, no conflicts. Use it.
- **Wed 6/10:** NY Phil crowd builds near Great Lawn from 3 PM — drives clear, paths fill.
- **Fri 6/12:** 92°F again; 40-in-Focus opens at Arsenal for a cool post-run stop.

Have a strong week,
