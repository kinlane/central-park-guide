# Central Park Sports This Week: Fri May 15 – Fri May 22

Hey sports fans,

New Friday-evening edition heading into the weekend. One bike race, model yachts, and the usual league baseball action. Here's the week.

## Sat May 16, 4–8:30 AM — CRCA Racing

**CRCA Central Park Racing Series** crit on **East Drive**. Saturday-morning bike racing is back as a weekly fixture. Posts up east of 72nd are best — riders coming through fast. Get coffee around 6, watch 4–5 laps, you're done by breakfast. The most accessible elite-amateur cycling in NYC.

## Sat May 16 — multiple sports + spectacle

- **Model Yacht Racing** at Kerbs Boathouse, 9 AM–3 PM. Central Park Model Yacht Club's regular Saturday race day. Conservatory Water with remote-controlled sailboats — old-school NYC park ritual.
- **Heckscher Fields adult softball** (-01, -02, -05) running through the day
- **North Meadow adult & Little League baseball** — fields 1–6 active 8 AM to evening

## Sat–Sun — Little League weekend

Standard spring weekend pattern across **Great Lawn Softball fields 1–8** and **North Meadow Baseball/Softball**. Most fields active 8 AM through late afternoon both days. If you like wandering between games, this is the best Saturday format — multiple games running in parallel.

## Sun May 17 — Heckscher softball + tennis

Heckscher Fields running adult softball through the morning. No big race or special sports event Sunday.

## Mon–Fri weekday sports

- **Daily youth soccer training** at Lawn North of Levin Playground (James Christie sessions, mid-morning and afternoon)
- **Adult/youth softball and baseball** at North Meadow evenings
- **Heckscher youth soccer/football** weekday evenings

## Bowling Green note

The lawn bowling green is closed today (Fri 5/15) until 2 PM for maintenance. Open after that and the rest of the week.
