## Pattern observations

The early-spring rhythm of the park has settled into a clear weekly shape:

- **Sundays = community events** (Folkdancers, sometimes Skate Circle, sometimes races)
- **Saturday mornings = CRCA crit racing** on East Drive
- **Saturday daytime = stacked recreational events**, often three or four at once across the park
- **Weekdays = the calm**, when the park belongs to walkers, runners, and birds
- **The Pool Lawns** are now a working outdoor theater venue through end of June

This week's Saturday is dense even by recent standards — six concurrent events between 10 AM and 3 PM. Worth noting if you're tracking when "busy" tips into "saturated."
