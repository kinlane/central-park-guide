# Central Park Walk Notes: Mon Jun 29 – Sun Jul 5

Hi walkers,

This is a shade-and-water week. A heat wave builds through the holiday, so the play is early mornings, the leafy interior, and a couple of free things worth pausing for along the way.

## Weather this week

Pleasant Monday (85°), then the heat climbs hard — 96° Wed, 100° Thu, 101° Fri, and a stormy 97° on the Fourth. Walk before 10 AM or after the sun drops, and stick to the canopy: the Ramble, North Woods, and the Mall's elms hold the shade when the open lawns turn into a griddle.

## Mon Jun 29 & Tue Jun 30 — your cool windows

The most comfortable mornings of the week. Take the scenic loop while you can — **Bow Bridge, Bethesda Terrace, the Ramble** — and linger by the water. The **Arsenal Gallery's free "40 in Focus" photo exhibit** (9 AM–5 PM, near the 64th & 5th entrance) is a perfectly air-conditioned mid-walk stop celebrating 40 years of SummerStage. Note the Arsenal corner gets busy Tuesday morning with FIFA World Cup bus staging at nearby Columbus Circle.

## Wed Jul 1 — free music at the Bandshell, and a SummerStage night

A good evening to be in the park once the worst heat eases: **SummerStage presents Shabaka, Kokoroko, Omar and Lovie at Rumsey Playfield, free, from 5 PM** — you can hear it drifting across the Mall even if you don't go in. If you want a quiet wander instead, the heat will push the wedding-and-photo crowds onto the shaded spots, so **Bethesda, Cherry Hill, and the Cop Cot** may be booked for ceremonies midweek — beautiful to pass, not to sit.

## Thu Jul 2 & Fri Jul 3 — 100°/101°, dawn strolls only

Walk at first light or skip the open ground entirely. **Acoustic Songwriters Day at Bandshell Plaza, 2 PM Thursday** is free, but it's the hottest part of a 100° day — go only if you can take the shade and bring water. Otherwise these are mornings-in-the-Ramble days.

## Sat Jul 4 & Sun Jul 5 — holiday crowds, then a folk dance

The Fourth is 97° with afternoon storms and the lawns packed with picnics — an early shaded walk before the crowds is the move. Sunday brings the lovely **Central Park International Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza, 11 AM** (free, by Turtle Pond) — a gentle, shady spot to pause and watch.

## Quick recap

**Mon–Tue mornings:** the week's coolest walking — take the scenic loop early.

**Free indoor stop:** Arsenal Gallery "40 in Focus" photo exhibit, 9–5 daily.

**Wed 5 PM — SummerStage (Shabaka) at Rumsey:** free, drifts across the Mall.

**Sun 11 AM — Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza:** free, shaded, by Turtle Pond.

**Wed–Sat — heat wave (96–101°):** walk at dawn, stay under the canopy, carry water.

Walk in the shade,
The Central Park Guide
