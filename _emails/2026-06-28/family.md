# Central Park Family Plans: Mon Jun 29 – Sun Jul 5

Hi families,

It's a July 4th week with a serious heat wave attached, so this one is all about water, shade, and early starts. A few free, kid-friendly things land midweek and over the holiday — here's how to time them.

## Weather this week

Warm Monday (85°), then dangerously hot — 96° Wed, 100° Thu, 101° Fri, and a stormy 97° on the Fourth. Plan park time for before 11 AM or late afternoon, hit the playground sprinklers and the splash spots, and keep the middle of the day for somewhere with air conditioning.

## Mon Jun 29 & Tue Jun 30 — sprinklers and boats before the heat

The two most comfortable mornings. Great time for the **playground sprinklers** (Heckscher, Ancient Playground, and the Wild West are reliable) and for **model sailboats on Conservatory Water** by Kerbs Boathouse. Tuesday morning, give Columbus Circle a wide berth — FIFA World Cup buses are staging there from early.

## Wed Jul 1 — free music you can picnic to

**SummerStage at Rumsey Playfield is free** (Shabaka and friends, from 5 PM) — an easy early-evening outing once the day cools, with room to spread a blanket near the Mall and hear the show. Bring water and a fan; it'll still be warm.

## Thu Jul 2 — Acoustic Songwriters Day at the Bandshell

Free live music at **Bandshell Plaza at 2 PM** — but it's the hottest stretch of a 100° day, so this is a quick-stop-with-shade-and-water plan, not a sit-all-afternoon one. Honestly, Thursday is a sprinkler-and-AC day for the little ones.

## Sat Jul 4 & Sun Jul 5 — the holiday

The Fourth is 97° with afternoon thunderstorms and big picnic crowds (Sheep Meadow, the Great Lawn, and Cherry Hill fill up by late morning) — stake out a shady spot early or save the park for the cooler evening. **Note: the Macy's fireworks are on the East River, not in the park.** Sunday eases a touch, with the gentle, free **Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza at 11 AM** (by Turtle Pond) — fun to watch with kids.

## Quick recap

**Mon–Tue mornings:** coolest windows — sprinklers and model boats before the heat.

**Wed 5 PM — free SummerStage at Rumsey:** picnic-and-music evening once it cools.

**Thu 2 PM — Acoustic Songwriters Day (Bandshell):** free, but a 100° day — short visit, shade, water.

**Sat 7/4:** 97° and crowded — early or evening only; fireworks are on the East River, not the park.

**Sun 11 AM — Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza:** free and kid-friendly.

**All week — heat wave:** water, shade, sprinklers, and AC at midday.

Have a fun (and cool) week,
The Central Park Guide
