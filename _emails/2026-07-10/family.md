# Central Park Family Plans: Fri Jul 10 – Thu Jul 16

Hi families,

A big free-fun weekend — Movie Night on the Great Lawn, model sailboats, storytelling, and a Ranger nature "World Cup" for kids — then a heat wave that makes the free Gottesman Pool your best friend by midweek. Plan the outdoor stuff for the weekend mornings and the water for the hot days.

## Weather this week

Storms Friday and showers Saturday in the low-80s, then a hot, sunny stretch Sunday through Thursday climbing to 95° by Wednesday. Do the lawns and fields early in the day all week, and lean on the pool, shade, and water fountains once the real heat arrives Sunday.

## Fri Jul 10 — Movie Night on the Great Lawn

Free **Movie Night** on the Great Lawn at 8 PM — bring a blanket and a picnic (weather permitting; afternoon thunderstorms are possible, so check the sky). Earlier in the day, **Gottesman Pool** up at the Harlem Meer has free 7 AM lap swim for the grown-ups.

## Sat Jul 11 — model sailboats and storytelling

A classic Central Park kids' Saturday: **Model Yacht Racing** on Conservatory Water by Kerbs Boathouse at 9 AM (the little sailboats — always a hit), and **Storytelling in Central Park** at the Hans Christian Andersen statue at 10 AM. Both are morning events, which is perfect — you'll be home before the afternoon showers.

## Sun Jul 12, 1 PM — Ranger Games: Nature World Cup

The Urban Park Rangers host **Ranger Games: Nature World Cup** near the West 106th Street entrance (Strangers' Gate) at 1 PM — a free, kid-focused nature-and-field-day event, and a great excuse to explore the wilder north end. Also up north, **Summer on the Harlem Meer** runs family programming at the Dana Discovery Center from 9 AM. First hot day at 85°, so hats, water, and sunscreen.

## Mon Jul 13 — 86°, a pool-and-playground morning

No special programming, but it's a good day for an early playground run and a cool-off. The **Clay Oval** youth ballfields (East 102nd Street) are active if you have a young player.

## Tue Jul 14 — Gottesman Pool learn-to-swim sessions begin

Session 1 of the free **Gottesman Pool** parent-and-tot and children's learn-to-swim classes runs across the late morning and early afternoon (10 AM onward). It's 91° and sunny — a pool day if there ever was one. Register/check-in details are at the pool; spots fill fast.

## Wed Jul 15 — 95°, water and shade only

The hottest day of the week. Keep it to the pool, sprinklers, and shade — the Ramble and North Woods are the coolest wanders. Save the open fields for another day.

## Thu Jul 16 — 92°, more pool time

Still hot. Another round of free Gottesman Pool learn-to-swim sessions runs midday, and the Columbus Circle Food Market (Merchants' Gate, 9:30 AM) is an easy shaded-edge stop on the way in.

## Quick recap

- **Fri 8 PM — Movie Night on the Great Lawn** (weather permitting).
- **Sat — Model Yacht Racing (9 AM) and Storytelling at the Andersen statue (10 AM).**
- **Sun 1 PM — Ranger Games: Nature World Cup** at Strangers' Gate (W 106th).
- **Tue & Thu — free Gottesman Pool learn-to-swim sessions begin** — your midweek heat plan.
- **Heat wave Sun–Thu, 95° Wednesday** — mornings, shade, and water all week.

See you in the park,
The Central Park Guide
