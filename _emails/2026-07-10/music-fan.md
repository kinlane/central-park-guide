# Central Park Music & Performance: Fri Jul 10 – Thu Jul 16

Hi music fans,

This is the loaded week of the summer so far: **three SummerStage nights in a row** at Rumsey Playfield — Nora En Pure's PURIFIED Friday, Brazilian star Pedro Sampaio Saturday, and a free Bastille Day bill Sunday — plus a Sunday festival at the Bandshell, a midweek Moroccan SummerStage, and a free Great Lawn concert to close. Most of it free.

## Weather this week

Storms Friday and showers Saturday in the low-80s, then clear and hot Sunday through Thursday, peaking at 95° Wednesday. The outdoor shows should play through — carry water for the evening sets, and know that Friday's storms are the only real threat to a lineup this week.

## Fri Jul 10, 4 PM — Nora En Pure presents PURIFIED (SummerStage benefit)

Swiss producer **Nora En Pure** brings her melodic deep-house **PURIFIED** series to Rumsey Playfield as a SummerStage benefit show, running from 4 PM into the evening. It's a ticketed benefit rather than a free night, but it's the marquee opener. Watch the radar — afternoon thunderstorms are the wild card.

## Sat Jul 11, 4 PM — Pedro Sampaio (free SummerStage)

A high-energy Brazilian afternoon: **Pedro Sampaio**, one of the biggest names in **funk carioca**, headlines a free SummerStage show at Rumsey Playfield from 4 PM. Free means arrive early — the popular ones reach capacity. Earlier, catch **That Guitar Man from Central Park** at Hernshead (noon) and **tango at the Shakespeare statue** on Literary Walk (6 PM) for an all-day music crawl.

## Sun Jul 12 — Bastille Day at SummerStage + folk dancing + a festival

The day's centerpiece is a free **Bastille Day Concert** at Rumsey Playfield (6 PM), headlined by **Black M** and **Laurent Voulzy** — a celebration of French and Francophone music. Before that, the **Central Park International Folkdancers** meet at King Jagiello Plaza at 11 AM, and the **Disability Unite Music and Arts Festival** fills Bandshell Plaza from 11 AM for Disability Pride Month. A full, free day.

## Mon Jul 13 — quiet on the music calendar

No shows on the books. A good evening to walk the empty Bandshell or Bethesda area (Terrace itself is closed this week) and let the ears rest before Wednesday.

## Tue Jul 14, 6 PM — The Magic Raven Theater Company at the Bandshell

A theatrical-musical evening at Bandshell Plaza from 6 PM — the Magic Raven company's staged program is the one midweek performance to catch. It's 91° and clear, so a warm but playable night.

## Wed Jul 15, 6 PM — Morocco: From Dakhla to Casablanca (free SummerStage)

A free SummerStage world-music night at Rumsey Playfield — **Hoba Hoba Spirit, Hamid El Kasri**, and more, spanning Moroccan traditions from Dakhla to Casablanca. It's the hottest day of the week at 95°, so hydrate through the set; the music's worth the swelter.

## Thu Jul 16, 7 PM — Free Concerts on the Great Lawn

The week closes with a free evening concert on the Great Lawn at 7 PM — bring a blanket. Still 92° and clear, an easy warm-night listen.

Looking ahead: **Naumburg Orchestral Concerts** resume their Tuesday season at the Bandshell on **July 21** (Orchestra of St. Luke's) — mark it.

## Quick recap

- **Fri 4 PM — Nora En Pure / PURIFIED** at Rumsey (SummerStage benefit, ticketed).
- **Sat 4 PM — Pedro Sampaio** at Rumsey (free; funk carioca).
- **Sun 6 PM — Bastille Day Concert** at Rumsey (free; Black M, Laurent Voulzy) + Folkdancers 11 AM + Disability Unite festival at the Bandshell.
- **Tue 6 PM — Magic Raven Theater Company** at the Bandshell.
- **Wed 6 PM — Morocco SummerStage** (free); **Thu 7 PM — Free Concerts, Great Lawn.**
- **Next Naumburg: Tue Jul 21** (Orchestra of St. Luke's).

Enjoy the music,
