# Central Park Running Heads-Up: Fri Jul 10 – Thu Jul 16

Hey runners,

Two things own your week: Saturday morning the loop belongs to NYRR's **9/11 Memorial & Museum 4-Mile** — about 5,000 runners on the lower loop from 7:30 AM — and then a heat wave that builds all week and peaks at 95° Wednesday. Front-load in the cooler, storm-flecked Friday–Saturday air, then go dawn-only from Sunday on.

## Weather this week

Showers and thunderstorms Friday and scattered rain Saturday in the low-to-mid 80s, then a clear, relentless heat wave Sunday through Thursday climbing 85° → 91° → 95° at Wednesday's peak. Run the dry windows Friday–Saturday, then treat every day after as first-light-only — out by 6, short, and carry water.

## Fri Jul 10 — 88° and thundery, open loop between storms

No closures on any drive, so the loop, Reservoir, and Bridle Path are all yours — the only obstacle is the radar. Storms fire up in the afternoon and evening, so bank your run early or catch a dry gap and bail when it lights up.

## Sat Jul 11, 7:30 AM — 9/11 Memorial & Museum 4-Mile takes the lower loop

This is the day to plan around. About 5,000 runners stage on **West Drive in the 60s–70s** and race four miles on the lower loop starting 7:30 AM; the field clears by roughly 9:30–10 AM. The loop is a race course, not your training loop, until then — this is **not** a "clean Saturday." Run before 7 AM, after 10, or take the Bridle Path or the Reservoir track while it's busy. Scattered showers, low 80s, so it won't be a hot one.

## Sun Jul 12, 5:30–7:30 AM — CRCA crit racing on East Drive

The CRCA Central Park Racing Series runs its early-morning laps on **East Drive from 5:30 to 7:30 AM** — a fast peloton sharing the road with you. Not a closure, but if you're out at dawn, expect race bikes at speed on the east side; run counterclockwise-aware or start after 7:30. First clear day of the stretch at 85°, so otherwise it's a good — if warm — morning.

## Mon Jul 13 — 86°, open loop, go early

Nothing on the drives. Warm and sunny, so get your miles in before 8 and keep them honest. The Reservoir and Bridle Path stay shaded longest.

## Tue Jul 14 — 91°, dawn only

Hot and fully open. First-light run or nothing; this is a day to shorten up and slow down. Hydrate before, during, and after.

## Wed Jul 15 — 95°, hottest day of the week

The peak. Dawn or treadmill — there's no smart way to run the loop in mid-afternoon at 95°. If you must, go absolute first light, keep it very short, and stay near a water fountain.

## Thu Jul 16 — 92°, still brutal, early or bust

Marginally cooler but still dangerous by midday. Same playbook: out at dawn, short, hydrated. Open loop all day.

## Quick recap

- **Sat 7:30 AM — 9/11 Memorial & Museum 4-Mile.** Lower loop is a race course until ~10 AM; run before 7, after 10, or use the Bridle Path / Reservoir.
- **Sun 5:30–7:30 AM — CRCA crit racing on East Drive.** Fast peloton sharing the road; start after 7:30 if you'd rather not.
- **Heat wave Sun–Thu, peaking 95° Wednesday.** Every day is dawn-only from Sunday on.
- **Fri–Sat are your cool windows** — storm-dodging Friday, wet-but-mild Saturday between race hours.

Have a strong week,
The Central Park Guide
