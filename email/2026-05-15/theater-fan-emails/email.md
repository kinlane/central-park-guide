# Julius Caesar continues + week ahead: Fri May 15 – Fri May 22

Hey theater fans,

New Friday-evening edition. Julius Caesar at The Pool is running through May 30 — and the next show in the same space is already on the calendar after it. Here's the week.

**Daily 10 AM–9 PM at The Pool Lawns** (100th–103rd, just inside CPW). The production extends through Sat May 30. Check the company's listing for actual showtimes within that daily window.

If you've been putting it off, this weekend is a strong choice — weeknights are calmer than the upcoming Memorial Day weekend will be.

- **Golden hour**: ~7:30–8 PM is when the staging is most striking
