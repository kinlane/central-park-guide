# Central Park Walking Plans: Fri May 15 – Fri May 22

Hi walkers,

New Friday-evening edition heading into your weekend. The park is at one of its loveliest moments of the year — late spring color, comfortable temps, long evenings. Here's what to plan around and where to wander.

## Sat May 16 — busy day, walk with intention

Six concurrent things going on Saturday between 10 AM and 3 PM:

- **CRCA bike racing** on East Drive, 4–8:30 AM (crit racing — fun to walk past at 7 AM)
- **Columbus Circle Food Market** at Merchants' Gate, 9:30 AM–9 PM
- **Model Yacht Racing** at Kerbs Boathouse, 9 AM–3 PM
- **Sacred Sites Open House Day 1** at Mineral Springs, 10 AM–3 PM
- **That Guitar Man** at Hernshead, 1–5 PM
- **PS 77 Spring Festival** at Levin Playground lawn, 2–5 PM

Stitched together, you can make a great walking loop: **start at Conservatory Water (model boats) → Sheep Meadow → Mineral Springs (Sacred Sites) → Hernshead (That Guitar Man) → Bow Bridge → Bethesda.** That's a 2-hour walk with three free events along the way.

Or, for solitude Saturday: **head north** to Conservatory Garden and North Woods — fully calm.

## Sun May 17 — Sacred Sites + Folkdancers

- **Sacred Sites Open House Day 2** at Mineral Springs, 10 AM–3 PM
- **Folkdancers** at King Jagiallo Plaza, 11 AM–7 PM

Pair both into a Sunday afternoon walk across the park.

## Scenic-spot celebrations Sat & Sun

**Bow Bridge, Bethesda Terrace, Belvedere Castle Terrace, Cherry Hill, Wagner Cove, Cop Cot, Ladies' Pavilion** all have private ceremonies sprinkled throughout the weekend. Beautiful to encounter; expect crowds. **Walk those spots Mon–Thu instead for solitude.**

## Mon–Thu (May 18–21) — best walking week in a while

No major events all week. Empty paths, peak rhododendron bloom, long light. If you've been meaning to do a full park walk — north to south, or the perimeter — **this is the week**.

## Best walks this week

- **Conservatory Garden → Harlem Meer → North Woods** — peaceful any day
- **Mall → Bethesda → Bow Bridge** — beautiful **Mon, Tue, or Wed** when ceremonies thin out
- **Reservoir at sunset** — clean every evening this week
- **Saturday's "event circuit"** — Conservatory Water → Mineral Springs → Hernshead → Bethesda

## Quick recap

**Sat 5/16 — busy across the south/west park**
Either do the event circuit or head north for solitude.

**Sun 5/17 — Sacred Sites Day 2 + Folkdancers**

**Mon–Thu — wide open for walking**
Best weekday stretch this month.

**Sat–Sun avoid scenic-spot privacy**
Bow Bridge, Bethesda, Cherry Hill all booked.

Enjoy the late spring,
