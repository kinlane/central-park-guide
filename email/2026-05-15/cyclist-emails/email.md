# Central Park Cycling Heads-Up: Fri May 15 – Fri May 22

Hey cyclists,

New Friday-evening edition, heading into your weekend. After the back-to-back weekend cycling events of the past two weeks, this one's much quieter on the road side. Here's the week.

## Sat May 16, 4 AM–8:30 AM — CRCA Racing Series

The Saturday-morning CRCA crit racing series is back on **East Drive**. If you race, you know the drill. If you don't:

- **Before 4 AM** — loop is yours
- **4–8:30 AM** — race traffic on East Drive: marshals, rolling closures, fast paceline. Ride West Drive or watch from the curb
- **After 8:30 AM** — clean

Pro tip: 6 AM coffee + lap-watching is a great Saturday tradition.

## Sat May 16 daytime — south-end congestion

**Columbus Circle Food Market** at Merchants' Gate, 9:30 AM–9 PM (Fri/Sat/Sun). Lots of foot traffic at the SW entrance — slow your line through Columbus Circle on weekend laps.

## Sun May 17 — open road

No road events. Folkdancers at King Jagiallo and Sacred Sites at Mineral Springs are both interior — your loop is unaffected. **Sunday is the day to grab a long ride.**

## Mon–Fri (May 18–22) — best lap week in a while

No scheduled cycling events all week. Julius Caesar continues at The Pool but stays off the road. Weekday car-free hours apply as usual.

A few small interior items, none affecting roads:
- **Bowling Green Lawn** closed til 2 PM today (Fri 5/15), maintenance
- **East Meadow** closed all day Sat 5/16 and Sun 5/17 — interior, no impact on cycling
- **Columbus Circle Food Market** runs Fri/Sat/Sun (5/15, 5/16, 5/17, and again 5/22) — slow through the SW entrance on those days

## Quick recap

West Drive or watch the racing.

**Fri/Sat/Sun (and Fri 5/22) — Food Market at Columbus Circle**
Slow line through Merchants' Gate.
