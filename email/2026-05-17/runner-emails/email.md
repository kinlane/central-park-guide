# Central Park Running Heads-Up: Mon May 18 – Sun May 24

Hey runners,

Sunday-evening edition, looking ahead to the week starting tomorrow. **No races or organized rides on the loop all week** — but Memorial Day weekend brings heavy south-end foot traffic, and Tuesday's 93°F is going to be the first real heat day of the year.

## Weather this week

Hot and sunny through Tuesday — **Mon 86°F, Tue peaking at 93°F** — then Wednesday-evening thunderstorms, cool damp Thu–Sat, and **rain showers chance Sun**. Front-load long runs Monday morning before the Tuesday heat peak; Thursday and Friday are the cool-weather makeup window; Saturday will likely be wet.

## Mon May 18 — early morning, then back off

86°F. **Run before 8 AM** for cleanest air. Small note: **Bethesda Fountain Terrace party 6–9 PM** — minor pedestrian spill onto 72nd Cross Drive at the Bethesda underpass that evening, easy to navigate.

## Tue May 19 — 93°F, no events, but heat is the obstacle

The hottest day of the year so far. **Run before 7 AM or skip.** No events on the loop, so the only thing you're fighting is the heat. Reservoir at dawn — cherry tree shade on the east side — is the move. Bring water.

## Wed May 20 — storms incoming, morning is the window

Hot morning (high 80s), thunderstorms by late afternoon. **Morning run, then home.** Avoid exposed Reservoir/loop sections in the evening when lightning fires.

## Thu May 21 – Fri May 22 — the makeup window, no events

70°F then 66°F. **Best running stretch of the week.** No races, no path closures. One small thing: **Columbus Circle Food Market opens Thu and runs through Sun** at Merchants' Gate (9:30 AM–9 PM, holiday weekend extension). **Enter the park at 72nd or above** to skip the SW entrance bottleneck on those days.

Also Thu: small event at Mineral Springs, 5–7 PM. Not blocking paths.

## Sat May 23 — Memorial Day weekend opens, south end stacks up

Rain showers likely all day, ~65°F. **No CRCA crit racing this Saturday** (Memorial Day skip). But six events fill the south end regardless of weather:

- **Skate Circle** at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM — West Drive at 72nd jammed (cancels in heavy rain)
- **That Guitar Man at Hernshead**, noon–6 PM — foot traffic on West Drive 75th–78th
- **Salsa Dura Social** at Bandshell, 5–7:30 PM — Bandshell sits on West Drive, audience spills onto the path
- **Columbus Circle Food Market**, Merchants' Gate, 9:30 AM–9 PM
- **Mineral Springs party**, 3–6 PM
- **Model Yacht Racing** at Kerbs, 9 AM–3 PM (rain-dependent)
- East Meadow closed

Plan a **morning run** before these fire up, or stay above 86th in the afternoon. Reservoir and upper park stay clean all day.

## Sun May 24 — Memorial Day Sunday, similar pattern

71°F, chance of rain showers.

- **Folkdancers** at King Jagiallo, 11 AM–7 PM (Turtle Pond east — not a loop issue)
- **Skate Circle** at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM
- **Mineral Springs picnic**, noon–5 PM
- **Food Market** at Merchants' Gate, 9:30 AM–9 PM
- East Meadow closed

Morning is clean. Afternoon south end is busy. Same playbook as Saturday.

## Quick recap

**Tue 5/19 — 93°F, no events, dawn-only running**

**Thu 5/21 + Fri 5/22 — best running stretch of the week**
Cool weather, no events.

**Sat 5/23 + Sun 5/24 mornings — clean before Memorial Day crowds**
Afternoon south end busy both days.

**Thu–Sun — Merchants' Gate congested with Food Market**
Enter at 72nd or above.

Have a strong week — and respect Tuesday's heat,
The Central Park Guide
