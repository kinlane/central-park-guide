# Central Park Walking Plans: Mon May 18 – Sun May 24

Hi walkers,

Sunday-evening edition, looking at the week starting tomorrow. **Thursday and Friday will be the best walking days of the month** — cool, post-storm, no events. Memorial Day Saturday brings a wet south-end stack; Monday/Tuesday's heat means early or evening walks only.

## Weather this week

Hot and sunny through Tuesday with **Tuesday peaking at 93°F**, Wednesday-evening thunderstorms, then cool and damp Thursday onward with **rain showers likely Saturday** and chance of rain Sunday. Plan early-morning or post-7-PM walks Mon–Tue; **Thursday and Friday will be the best walking weather of the week**.

## Mon May 18 — Bethesda party 6–9 PM, otherwise calm

Hot day (86°F) so walk early or after dinner. **Bethesda Fountain Terrace** has a private party 6–9 PM — beautiful from the upper terrace overlook, busy below.

## Tue May 19 — 93°F, no events, heat is the obstacle

Pre-9 AM or post-7 PM. The Ramble, North Woods, and the Mall's elm-shaded stretch stay cooler than open paths.

## Wed May 20 — morning walks, storms after 4 PM

The storms over the Reservoir are beautiful from a covered Engineers' Gate vantage — Wednesday evening is the year's first real lightning weather. Don't get caught on the loop when they fire.

## Thu May 21 – Fri May 22 — the magic days

70°F then 66°F. **No events. Empty paths. Cool, possibly misty.** Best walking weather of the month.

**One recommended walk:** Conservatory Garden → Harlem Meer → North Woods → Ravine → Loch → exit at North Meadow. About 90 minutes, fully calm, post-storm waterfalls running. Pick Thursday or Friday morning.

Friday brings the **Columbus Circle Food Market** at Merchants' Gate (9:30 AM–9 PM) — easy lunch stop on a south-end walk.

## Sat May 23 — south end stacked + rain

Rain showers likely all day, 65°F. The south end still fills with weekend events despite rain — though rain will thin most:

- **Skate Circle** at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM
- **That Guitar Man at Hernshead**, noon–6 PM (Lake-side guitar)
- **Salsa Dura Social** at Bandshell, 5–7:30 PM — first Bandshell concert of the season
- **Columbus Circle Food Market** at Merchants' Gate, 9:30 AM–9 PM
- **Mineral Springs party**, 3–6 PM
- **Model Yacht Racing** at Kerbs, 9 AM–3 PM (rain-dependent)
- East Meadow closed

**The Ramble in light rain is unmatched** — birdsong subdued, the woods feel bigger, the Lake reflects nothing. With rain gear, Saturday is a walking day worth keeping.

## Sun May 24 — Folkdancers, Skate Circle, Mineral Springs picnic

71°F, chance of rain.

- **Folkdancers** at King Jagiallo, 11 AM–7 PM
- **Skate Circle** at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM
- **Mineral Springs** picnic, noon–5 PM
- **Food Market** continues at Merchants' Gate
- East Meadow closed

Morning is calm; afternoon south end is busy.

## Scenic-spot ceremonies this week

Bow Bridge, Bethesda Terrace, Belvedere Castle Terrace, Cherry Hill, Wagner Cove, Cop Cot, Ladies' Pavilion — small private ceremonies sprinkled through the week, mostly midday. **For solitude at these spots, walk Thu or Fri morning.**
