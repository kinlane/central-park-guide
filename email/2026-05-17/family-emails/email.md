# Central Park Family Plans: Mon May 18 – Sun May 24

Hi families,

Sunday-evening edition, looking at the week starting tomorrow. **Memorial Day Saturday is the big family day** — five kid-friendly events stacked in one afternoon. The weekday week is quiet except for Tuesday's heat.

## Weather this week

Hot and sunny through Tuesday — **Mon 86°F, Tue peaking at 93°F** — then thunderstorms Wednesday evening, cool damp Thu–Sun with **rain likely Saturday**. Hydrate the kids hard Mon–Tue; have an indoor Plan B ready for Saturday.

## Mon May 18 — quiet day, heat-aware

86°F. Mornings or after dinner outside. **Heat-day kid picks:**
- **Zoo** — opens 10 AM, mostly shaded
- **Marionette Theatre** at Swedish Cottage — indoor, air-conditioned (check their schedule)
- **Conservatory Water** model boat pond — shaded by trees

## Tue May 19 — 93°F, indoor or evening day

The heat day. The Zoo is your friend (shaded paths, indoor exhibits). Marionette Theatre is air-conditioned. If you go to a playground, **mornings before 10 AM or evenings after 6 PM only**. Heckscher Playground sprinklers may help if they're running.

## Wed May 20 — morning's still hot, storms in the evening

Aim for early activities. Get home before late afternoon thunderstorms.

## Thu May 21 – Fri May 22 — best park weather of the week

70°F then 66°F. No scheduled family events, so this is unstructured kid time: playgrounds, the zoo, picnics, walking the bridges. **Best family-walking weather all week.**

Friday: **Columbus Circle Food Market** opens at Merchants' Gate (9:30 AM–9 PM) — easy lunch or snack stop on a Friday park visit.

## Sat May 23 — the big family day (weather pending)

Five kid-friendly events scheduled, though rain may shift things:

- **Model Yacht Racing** at Kerbs Boathouse, 9 AM–3 PM — kids love it; cancels in steady rain
- **Columbus Circle Food Market** at Merchants' Gate, 9:30 AM–9 PM — covered stalls, runs in any weather
- **That Guitar Man at Hernshead**, noon–6 PM — Lake-side guitar, sing-alongs (rain-dependent)
- **Mineral Springs picnic + party** (10 AM–6 PM) — community gathering
- **Skate Circle** at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM — fun to watch, music, energy (cancels in rain)
- **Salsa Dura Social** at Bandshell Plaza, 5–7:30 PM — kids welcome, first Bandshell concert of the season

**East Meadow is closed all day** — picnic elsewhere.

If the rain holds off the morning: **Model boats at Kerbs at 9 AM** is the play. Indoor Plan B: Marionette Theatre, the Zoo, or a Met-side day.

## Sun May 24 — Memorial Day Sunday

71°F, chance of rain showers.

- **Folkdancers** at King Jagiallo, 11 AM–7 PM — costumed dancers, live music, kid-friendly
- **Skate Circle** at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM
- **Mineral Springs picnic**, noon–5 PM
- **Food Market** continues at Merchants' Gate
- East Meadow closed

Could be the better outdoor day of the weekend.

## Quick recap

**Mon–Tue — heat advisory, indoor + shaded picks**
Zoo, Marionette Theatre, Conservatory Water.

**Thu–Fri — perfect family park weather, no scheduled events**

**Sat 5/23 — big family Saturday (weather pending)**
Model boats, That Guitar Man, Skate Circle, Salsa Dura.

**Sun 5/24 — Folkdancers + Skate Circle + Mineral Springs picnic**

**East Meadow closed Sat + Sun**

Stay cool, stay dry,
The Central Park Guide
