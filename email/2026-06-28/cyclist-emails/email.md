# Central Park Cycling Heads-Up: Mon Jun 29 – Sun Jul 5

Hey cyclists,

Open drives all week — no races, no closures, no loop-occupying events. The two things to ride around this week are a building heat wave and a FIFA World Cup staging footprint parked at Columbus Circle.

## Weather this week

Rideable mornings Monday and Tuesday (mid-80s), then a brutal climb — 96° Wed, 100° Thu, 101° Fri, 97° on the Fourth with afternoon storms. Roll early all week and carry more water than you think you need; from Wednesday on, anything after 9 AM is a sufferfest, and the afternoons are genuinely unsafe.

## Mon Jun 29 — clean, open loop

Nothing on the drives. A normal early-morning loop before the heat settles in. Good day for repeats if you want them before the week closes in.

## Tue Jun 30 & Sun Jul 5 — FIFA bus ops at Columbus Circle

**FIFA World Cup bus operations stage at Columbus Circle from 6 AM** both days. The loop itself stays open, but expect buses and pedestrian congestion choking the **Merchants' Gate / Columbus Circle** entrance — the SW corner you'd normally roll in and out of. Enter and exit at **W 72nd** or **Engineers' Gate (90th & 5th)** to skip the snarl. Storms Tuesday night.

## Wed Jul 1 – Fri Jul 3 — the heat, not the calendar

The drives are wide open midweek, but 96°/100°/101° is the story. Be clipped in by 6:30 and off the loop before the sun gets high. **SummerStage spectator crowds spill toward East Drive near 72nd after 5 PM Wednesday (Shabaka) and Friday (Odd Mob)** — only relevant if you're chasing a rare cooler-evening ride; ride the west side or go before the gates open.

## Sat Jul 4 & Sun Jul 5 — holiday crowds, early rolls

The Fourth is 97° with afternoon thunderstorms and holiday foot traffic building mid-morning — dawn is your only clean, cool window. Sunday backs off the peak, but mind the FIFA congestion at Columbus Circle noted above.

## Quick recap

**No closures or races all week** — drives open every day.

**Tue & Sun, from 6 AM — FIFA bus ops at Columbus Circle:** SW-corner congestion; enter at W 72nd or Engineers' Gate.

**Wed–Sat — heat wave (96–101°):** clip in by 6:30, hydrate, no afternoon rides.

**Wed & Fri evenings — SummerStage:** spectator foot traffic near East Drive at 72nd after 5 PM.

Tailwinds and stay cool,
The Central Park Guide
