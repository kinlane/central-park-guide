# Central Park Stage: Romeo & Juliet continues at the Delacorte

Hi theater fans,

Free Shakespeare in the Park's *Romeo & Juliet* opened at the Delacorte this past weekend and continues its run into the week — the headline for anyone who wants Shakespeare under the stars. A heat wave is the thing to plan around for an open-air night.

## Weather this week

A serious heat wave — 96° Wed, 100° Thu, 101° Fri — easing to a stormy 97° on the Fourth, with thunderstorm risk Friday into Saturday evening. For a Delacorte night, the early-week and Sunday evenings will be the more bearable ones; keep an eye on the radar for any Friday/Saturday show.

## All week — Free Shakespeare in the Park: Romeo & Juliet (Delacorte Theater)

*Romeo & Juliet* continues at the **Delacorte Theater** at Turtle Pond — the quintessential Central Park theater night, free under the stars. A heads-up on the details: our permit feed only surfaces the Delacorte's dates sporadically, so **check the Public Theater's calendar for exact performances and the free-ticket lottery** before you go. The Delacorte is conventionally **dark Mondays**, with evening curtains the rest of the week. If you've never done a Delacorte night, this run is the one — just bring water for a hot evening.

## Also onstage — the Swedish Cottage marionettes

The **Swedish Cottage Marionette Theatre** runs its summer family show on its own standing schedule (reserve ahead) — a hand-built puppet stage that's a Central Park institution. Like the Delacorte, its dates don't flow cleanly into our permit data, so confirm showtimes with the Conservancy before making the trip.

## Live performance to fill in around the play

If you want more than the one stage, SummerStage and the Bandshell carry the week:

- **Wed Jul 1, 5 PM — Shabaka / Kokoroko / Omar / Lovie (free, Rumsey Playfield):** spiritual jazz and Afrobeat with real theatrical presence.
- **Thu Jul 2, 2 PM — Acoustic Songwriters Day (Bandshell Plaza):** storytelling set to music, free (it's 100° — sit in the shade).
- **Fri Jul 3, 5:30 PM — Odd Mob (Rumsey Playfield):** high-energy electronic; watch the storm risk.
