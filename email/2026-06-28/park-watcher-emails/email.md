# Central Park Weekly Digest: Mon Jun 29 – Sun Jul 5

Hi park watchers,

A July 4th holiday week colliding with the season's first 100°+ heat wave — and you can read both in the permit data. Here's the pattern view.

## Weather this week

The first true heat wave of the summer: 96° Wed, 100° Thu, 101° Fri, then a stormy 97° Fourth. It's the dominant force on the week — and the calendar bends around it, with permits visibly thinning at the peak.

## The heat is rewriting the calendar

Field-sport permits run heavy early in the week — **67 sport bookings Tuesday**, mostly North Meadow, the Great Lawn, and Heckscher — then drop by more than half (**21 on Friday**) as the heat peaks, before the holiday weekend brings them back up. Private bookings show the same shape: a midweek cluster (**17 ceremonies and parties Wednesday**) that collapses to near-zero across the Fourth and Fifth. Holiday weekends always quiet the private calendar; the heat is amplifying it.

## FIFA World Cup footprint arrives

A new operational signature in the data: **FIFA World Cup bus operations staging at Columbus Circle**, Tuesday and Sunday from 6 AM. It's the first sign of the 2026 tournament's presence pressing on the park's SW corner — worth tracking as the World Cup ramps and these footprints likely grow.

## SummerStage rhythm and a continuing exhibit

Rumsey keeps its two-night cadence: **Shabaka / Kokoroko / Omar / Lovie (free) Wednesday**, **Odd Mob Friday**. The Arsenal Gallery's **"40 in Focus: SummerStage through the Lens of Photography"** runs all week (9–5) — a fitting companion piece as the concert series hits its summer stride. Naumburg's free classical season continues just past this window, resuming **Tuesday Jul 7** with Delirium Musicum.

## Data note

A new venue string — **"Graywacke Arch Lawn"** (near the Met, by the Greywacke Arch) — surfaced in this week's permit feed and isn't yet in the places vocabulary. Flagging it for the gazetteer.

## Quick recap

**Heat wave (96–101°):** first 100°+ stretch of summer; permit volume dips at the peak.

**Sports permits:** 67 Tue → 21 Fri → back up over the holiday weekend.

**Private bookings:** midweek cluster (17 Wed) collapsing to ~zero across 7/4–7/5.

**FIFA World Cup:** bus ops staging at Columbus Circle, Tue & Sun — new footprint to watch.

**Programming:** SummerStage Wed (free) + Fri; Arsenal "40 in Focus" exhibit all week; Naumburg resumes 7/7.

**Gazetteer:** "Graywacke Arch Lawn" — new unmatched venue.
