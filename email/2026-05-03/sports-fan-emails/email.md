# Central Park Sports This Week: May 3–9

Hey sports fans,

Spring league season is fully underway. Here's what to catch this week if you like watching games — or finding open fields.

## Today (Sun May 3) — busy day on the diamonds

**Little League baseball is everywhere.** Games running 8 AM through evening across:
- **Great Lawn Softball fields 1–6** (multiple games, most start 8 AM)
- **North Meadow Softball field 9** (games through 6 PM)
- **Heckscher Fields Softball field 4** (afternoon games)

If you want to catch competitive 12U baseball, the **Great Lawn around midday** is the place. Multiple games running side by side — easy to wander.

Other today: **Football – Youth at Heckscher Soccer-01** in the early evening.

## TD Five Boro Bike Tour finish (Sun 5/3, 7 AM–noon)

If you like watching endurance events, **Center Drive and East Drive between 6 PM Saturday setup and noon Sunday finish** is the place. 32,000 cyclists rolling through, big finish festival energy.

## Mon–Fri — weekday games

- **Soccer training (James Christie group)** at Lawn North of Levin and South Dead Road A, midday into afternoon — drop in to watch developmental sessions
- **Youth football & soccer at Heckscher-01**, late afternoons
- **Little League baseball** continues evenings on most fields

## Thu May 7 — Lawn Bowling night

**University Club Lawn Bowling** at Bowling Green Lawn (yes, in Central Park) **6–8 PM**. Strange and wonderful. If you've never watched lawn bowling, go.

## Sat May 9 — model boats and skating

- **Model Yacht Racing** at Kerbs Boathouse, 9 AM–3 PM. Conservatory Water gets remote-controlled sailboats racing on the pond. Free and oddly mesmerizing.
- **Central Park Skate Circle** at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM. Skaters of every level, music, crowds — closer to spectacle than sport, but unmissable when it returns.

## Quick recap

**Sun 5/3 all day — Little League at Great Lawn & North Meadow**

**Sun 5/3, 7 AM–noon — Bike Tour finish at Center & East Drive**

**Mon–Sat 5/4–5/9 — youth soccer/football, evenings**

**Thu 5/7, 6–8 PM — University Club Lawn Bowling at Bowling Green Lawn**

**Sat 5/9, 9 AM–3 PM — Model Yacht Racing at Kerbs Boathouse**

**Sat 5/9, 2–7 PM — Skate Circle at Dead Road North**
