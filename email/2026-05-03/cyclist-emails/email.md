# Central Park Cycling Heads-Up: Week of May 3–9

Hey cyclists,

Whether you're riding the loop daily or just clipping in on weekends, here's what's happening in the park this week.

## Today (Sun May 3) — Five Boro Bike Tour finish

If you're in the Tour, congrats — Central Park is your finish line this morning. If you're not, the **finish festival on Center Drive and East Drive runs 7 AM to noon**. The lower and east loop will be jammed with riders, support vehicles, and crowds. Don't try to ride through it.

- Want to ride this morning? Stick to the **upper loop above the 102nd Transverse** until things clear
- Easier option: **wait until 1 PM**, the loop should be reopening
- The Bandshell area also gets busy after noon (Arise New York, 12–6 PM) — give West Drive between 66th and 72nd a wider line

## Mon–Fri (May 4–8) — normal car-free hours, watch Engineers' Gate area

Standard weekday car-free riding all week. One small thing: there's a daily setup at the **5th Ave sidewalk at 97th / Duke Ellington Circle (midnight–noon)** for the Olmsted Luncheon. Doesn't affect the road, but pedestrian flow at that corner will be heavier than usual — slow your line through there.

## Wed May 6 afternoon — Reservoir north end

An event setup at North Reservoir Landscape A from 2–5 PM. Won't close the road, but the East Drive section between 96th and 102nd may have foot traffic crossing.

## Sat May 9 afternoon — Skate Circle returns near 72nd

**Central Park Skate Circle takes over Dead Road North from 2–7 PM.** West Drive at 72nd gets congested with skaters, spectators, and music. Plan a morning ride or route through the upper park in the afternoon.

## Quick recap

**Sun 5/3, 7 AM–noon — Center & East Drive**
Stay above 102nd, or wait until 1 PM.

**Mon–Fri — Engineers' Gate area**
Slow line through 90th/5th, no road closures.

**Wed 5/6, 2–5 PM — East Drive at the Reservoir**
Watch for pedestrians crossing.

**Sat 5/9, 2–7 PM — West Drive at 72nd**
Ride morning, or stick to upper-park loops.

Best riding windows this week: **Sun afternoon (after 1 PM), weekday mornings, and Sat morning**. Tailwinds.
