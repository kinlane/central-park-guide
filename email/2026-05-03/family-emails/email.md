# Central Park Family Plans: Week of May 3–9

Hi families,

Spring is rolling and the park has plenty going on for kids this week. Here's a heads-up on what to plan for, what playgrounds are closed, and a few free things worth swinging by.

## Today (Sun May 3)

Lots of birthday parties and picnics happening today, but nothing that closes a playground. A few things kids might enjoy:

- **Copper Hills HS Dance Flash Mob** at Bethesda Fountain Terrace, 12:15–1 PM — a quick fun stop
- **Central Park International Folkdancers** at King Jagiallo Plaza (around 76th/CPE), 11 AM–7 PM — costumes, live music
- **Arise New York** at the Bandshell, noon–6 PM — outdoor festival vibe

A heads-up: the **TD Five Boro Bike Tour finish closes Center Drive and East Drive 7 AM to noon**. If you're walking south end with a stroller this morning, expect crowds and detours.

## Playgrounds closed this week

Two playground areas are closed for maintenance — worth knowing before you trek over:

- **East 72nd Street Playground Lawns**: closed all day **Mon 5/4, Tue 5/5, and Thu 5/7**
- **Levin Playground**: closed all day **Thu 5/7 and Fri 5/8**

Other playgrounds (Heckscher, Diana Ross, Mariners', Wild West, Ancient, Adventure, Heckscher) are open as usual.

## Best family pick of the week — Sat May 9

- **Model Yacht Racing at Kerbs Boathouse, 9 AM–3 PM.** Kids love watching the little remote-controlled sailboats on Conservatory Water. Free, drop-in.
- **Black Fae Day NYC** at Heckscher Picnic Area, 1–5 PM — community gathering, great atmosphere
- **Central Park Skate Circle** at Dead Road North, 2–7 PM — fun to watch even if you're not skating (older kids may want to bring blades)

## Mother's Day weekend (Sun May 10)

Just outside this week, but worth flagging:
- **Bondd Pilates Mother's Day Pop-up Class** at the Great Lawn (Sat 5/9, 11 AM–1 PM) — for moms
- The park is generally peaceful Mother's Day morning if you want a quiet picnic

## Quick recap

**Sun 5/3 morning — south end of park crowded**
Bike Tour finish. Avoid Center & East Drive til noon.

**Mon 5/4–Fri 5/8 — two playgrounds closed**
East 72nd Lawns (M/T/Th), Levin (Th/F).

**Sat 5/9 — best family day**
Model boats at Kerbs, Skate Circle at 72nd, picnics at Heckscher.

Have a great week with the kids,
The Central Park Guide
