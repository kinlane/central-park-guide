# Central Park Music & Performance: Mon Jul 6 – Sun Jul 12

Hi music fans,

A loaded midweek: free classical at Naumburg Tuesday, then two big free SummerStage nights back-to-back — **Spoon** Wednesday and **Shaggy** Thursday. The weekend adds a lakeside guitar legend and Sunday folk dancing, all of it free.

## Weather this week

Thunderstorms Monday and Tuesday in the mid-70s, then clearing and warming through the week — comfortable low 80s Wednesday, up to the low 90s by Friday and storm-flecked in the upper 80s over the weekend. Tuesday's Naumburg show carries the only real rain question — these Bandshell concerts rarely scrub and storms tend to clear by curtain, but check the radar before you head over; Wednesday onward is fine outdoor-listening weather.

## Tue Jul 7, 7:30 PM — Delirium Musicum at the Naumburg Bandshell (free)

The week's centerpiece for the classically inclined: **Delirium Musicum** plays the free **Naumburg Orchestral Concerts** series at the Bandshell on the Mall, 7:30 PM — the oldest free outdoor classical series in the country, and this LA-based string collective brings real fire to it. Bring a blanket and get there early for a spot near the shell.

## Wed Jul 8, 6 PM — Spoon / Ratboys / Bodega at Rumsey Playfield (free)

The big free SummerStage night: **Spoon headlines Rumsey at 6 PM, with Ratboys, Bodega, and host Alisa Ali** — indie-rock heavyweights on a free bill, on the week's most comfortable evening. Free lines for a name like Spoon form well before doors, so arrive early.

## Thu Jul 9, 7 PM — Shaggy's Yaad at Rumsey Playfield (free)

Turned up loud: **"Shaggy's Yaad" — Shaggy with a deep guest list** (Robin Thicke, Tanto Metro & Devonte, Rayvon, and more) at Rumsey, 7 PM, free — a full dancehall-and-reggae bill on a warm 89° evening. Another early-arrival night.

## Sat Jul 11, noon–6 PM — That Guitar Man From Central Park at Hernshead

A gentler weekend note: **"That Guitar Man From Central Park"** plays his long-running set at **Hernshead on the Lake, noon–6 PM** — one of the park's most scenic spots to catch live guitar, right on the water off the West Drive.

## Sun Jul 12, 11 AM — Central Park International Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza (free)

Close the week in motion: the **International Folkdancers** hold their weekly free gathering at **King Jagiello Plaza** by Turtle Pond from 11 AM — live folk music and open circles you can join or just listen to.

## Quick recap

**Tue 7:30 PM — Delirium Musicum, Naumburg Bandshell (free classical).**

**Wed 6 PM — Spoon / Ratboys / Bodega, Rumsey (free).**

**Thu 7 PM — Shaggy's Yaad, Rumsey (free).**

**Sat noon–6 PM — That Guitar Man at Hernshead.**

**Sun 11 AM — Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza (free).**

See you at the Bandshell,
The Central Park Guide
