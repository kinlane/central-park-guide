# Central Park Nature Notes: Fri Jun 26 – Thu Jul 2

Hi nature lovers,

A quiet, green week for the park's wild corners — bird walks, fishing at the Meer, and a heat wave that makes the dawn hours the only ones worth having. Here's where the calm is.

## Weather this week

Damp and mild this weekend (mid-70s Sat with showers, mid-80s Sun) — good for greenery and birds — then a heat wave Tuesday onward, peaking near 94°F Thursday. The rain freshens the Ramble and the Loch; midweek, the **dawn chorus and early hours** are the only comfortable (and best) time to be out.

## Fri Jun 26 & through the week — Birding Bob at the Boathouse

**Bird Watching with Birding Bob** runs its near-daily walks from the **Loeb Boathouse** (Fri 8:30 AM; Sat/Sun 7:30 & 9:30 AM). Late June is past peak migration, but the Ramble is full of breeding residents — warblers, the resident red-tails, herons at the Lake. A reliable, well-led way into the park's quietest woods.

## Sat Jun 27 — Fishing at the Harlem Meer + quiet north end

**Catch-and-release fishing at the Harlem Meer** runs from the **Dana Discovery Center (10 AM)** — poles and bait provided, a gentle way to spend a morning at the water's edge. With Pride events drawing crowds to the south end this weekend, the **north end is yours**: the **Loch and the Ravine** (listen for the waterfalls after the rain), **North Woods**, and the **Conservatory Garden**.

## Sun Jun 28 — head north while the south celebrates

The south end is busy with the SummerStage Pride celebration. Perfect excuse to spend the day in the park's wildest quarter: **Hallett Nature Sanctuary** (if open hours align), the **Ramble** mid-morning, and **Turtle Pond** below Belvedere, where the turtles bask and the dragonflies are out in force in this warmth.

## Tue Jul 1 – Thu Jul 2 — dawn is everything

The heat builds to **94° Thursday**. Wildlife goes quiet and shaded by midday, so do you: be in the **Ramble or North Woods by 6:30–7 AM** for the dawn chorus and the coolest air, and retreat to the deep shade (the Loch, the Pinetum) before the heat tops out. Carry water.

## Quick recap

**Fri–Sun — Birding Bob walks (Loeb Boathouse):** Ramble residents; Fri 8:30, Sat/Sun 7:30 & 9:30.

**Sat 6/27, 10 AM — Fishing at the Harlem Meer (Dana Center):** catch-and-release, gear provided.

**Sat–Sun — north end is the quiet one:** Loch, Ravine, North Woods, Conservatory Garden while the south celebrates Pride.

**Tue–Thu — heat (up to 94°):** dawn in the Ramble, deep shade by midday, hydrate.
