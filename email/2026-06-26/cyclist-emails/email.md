# Central Park Cycling Heads-Up: Fri Jun 26 – Thu Jul 2

Hi cyclists,

Two weekend morning runs share the loop, the FIFA World Cup machinery is humming at Columbus Circle, and a heat wave lands midweek. Here's the riding picture.

## Weather this week

Damp and mild this weekend (mid-70s Sat with showers, mid-80s Sun), then a building heat wave — 89°F Tue, 91°F Wed, 94°F Thu, with afternoon thunderstorm risk Tue–Thu. The weekend mornings are your best loops; midweek, ride at dawn and watch the western sky for late-day storms.

## Sat Jun 27, ~8 AM — Front Runners NY Pride Run 4M

A 4-mile running race stages at **Bandshell Plaza** and occupies the mid-loop **7:30–10 AM**. Runners will be on the roadway through the 60s–72nd; ease your pace through there or ride before 7:30 / after 10:30. Counter-clockwise full laps aren't worth fighting in that window.

## Sun Jun 28 — Achilles 4M + Dreamland Pride spectators

**Achilles Hope & Possibility 4M** stages at **Bandshell Plaza**, ~8:15 AM, with a large handcycle and adaptive field on the course until ~11 AM — slow down and give wide berth. Then **Dreamland: Pride in Central Park at SummerStage (Rumsey Playfield) runs 3–10 PM**: heavy pedestrian flow crossing **East Drive near 72nd** all afternoon and evening. Ride the loop in the morning; the PM is for spectators, not pace.

## All week — Columbus Circle / Merchants' Gate congestion

**FIFA World Cup bus operations** are running at **Columbus Circle** (early mornings Sat & Tue onward). Expect extra pedestrian and vehicle churn at **Merchants' Gate** on your way in and out — take the entrance wide and slow.

## Tue Jul 1 – Thu Jul 2 — heat, clear roads

No loop closures midweek, but **89°/91°/94°** makes afternoon riding rough. Dawn laps are the move; carry two bottles. SummerStage has a Wed-evening show (5 PM, Rumsey) — minor spectator crossings near 72nd after work.

## Quick recap

**Sat 6/27, 7:30–10 AM — Front Runners Pride Run (Bandshell):** runners on the mid-loop; ride early or late.

**Sun 6/28, 8–11 AM — Achilles 4M (Bandshell):** handcycles on course, give room; Dreamland Pride crowds East Drive at 72nd 3–10 PM.

**All week — Columbus Circle:** FIFA bus ops; slow through Merchants' Gate.

**Tue–Thu — heat (89–94°):** dawn laps, hydrate, watch for afternoon storms.

Best rides: **weekday sunrise** and **Sat before 7:30**.

Tailwinds,
The Central Park Guide
