# Central Park Sports: Fri Jun 26 – Thu Jul 2

Hey sports fans,

Two weekend 4-milers, World Cup soccer popping up around the park, the model-boat regatta, and the usual busy field slate — then a heat wave that'll thin the diamonds by midweek. Here's the rundown.

## Weather this week

Showery and mild this weekend (mid-70s to mid-80s) — playable — then a heat wave Tuesday onward, **89° Tue, 91° Wed, 94° Thu**, with afternoon storm chances. Expect field play to push to mornings midweek, and watch for heat-related game cancellations Wed–Thu.

## Sat Jun 27 — Pride Run 4M + a full field slate

The **Front Runners NY LGBT Pride Run 4M** stages at **Bandshell Plaza** (~8 AM) and runs the loop through ~10 AM. On the fields: the usual Saturday load of **adult and youth softball, baseball, and little league at North Meadow and Heckscher**, plus **CP Sports summer programs**. The **Model Yacht Club races** run at **Conservatory Water (10 AM)**, and **pickleball at Wollman Rink** continues (the "Wimbledon Court" pop-up is also up at Wollman).

## Sun Jun 28 — Achilles Hope & Possibility 4M

**Achilles Hope & Possibility 4M** stages at **Bandshell Plaza** (~8:15 AM) — a large adaptive-athlete and handcycle field on the loop until ~11 AM. A great one to come watch. Sunday fields run their usual youth and adult schedules; the south end is otherwise given over to the Dreamland Pride concert in the afternoon.

## All week — World Cup in the park

The FIFA World Cup is showing up around Central Park: **FIFA Arena free drop-in soccer at Tavern on the Green** (daily), **bus operations at Columbus Circle**, and a **World Cup watch party at SummerStage/Rumsey** (Sat 5 PM, Panama v. England). Good pickup-and-spectate energy if you want it.

## Tue Jul 1 – Thu Jul 2 — heat takes the fields

Diamonds and courts stay booked, but **89°/91°/94°** will hammer afternoon play. Morning games and early court bookings are the move; expect some heat cancellations Wednesday and especially **Thursday (94°)**. Hydrate hard if you're on the field.

## Quick recap

**Sat 6/27, ~8 AM — Front Runners Pride Run 4M (Bandshell):** loop race; busy fields at North Meadow/Heckscher.

**Sat 6/27, 10 AM — Model Yacht Club races (Conservatory Water):** the regatta; pickleball at Wollman.

**Sun 6/28, ~8:15 AM — Achilles Hope & Possibility 4M (Bandshell):** big adaptive field — worth watching.

**All week — World Cup:** free soccer at Tavern, watch party at Rumsey (Sat), bus ops at Columbus Circle.

**Tue–Thu — heat (89–94°):** morning play; expect afternoon cancellations Wed–Thu.

Play hard, stay cool,
The Central Park Guide
