# Central Park Running Heads-Up: Fri Jun 26 – Thu Jul 2

Hey runners,

Two morning races stage off the loop this weekend, then the week turns into a heat-management problem. Here's how to time your runs.

## Weather this week

Showery and mild through the weekend (mid-70s to mid-80s), then a heat wave builds Tuesday onward — 89°F Tue, 91°F Wed, 94°F Thu with afternoon thunderstorm chances. Get your long runs in over the cool, damp weekend mornings; from Tuesday on, run before 8 AM or skip the loop in the afternoon.

## Sat Jun 27, ~8 AM — Front Runners NY LGBT Pride Run 4M

The Front Runners NY Pride Run stages at **Bandshell Plaza** and runs a 4-mile loop. Expect the lower-to-mid loop and the Mall to fill with runners and staging from roughly **7:30 to 10 AM**. This is a loop event — don't plan a clean tempo through the 60s–72nd St stretch in that window. Run before 7:30, after 10:30, or use the **Reservoir** / **Bridle Path**.

## Sun Jun 28, ~8:15 AM — Achilles Hope & Possibility 4M

Achilles Hope & Possibility also stages at **Bandshell Plaza** — a 4-miler with a large adaptive-athlete field and handcycles on the course. The loop is busy and slower-moving **8 AM – 11 AM**; give the field room. Run early, or head to the Reservoir track. Also note **Dreamland Pride at SummerStage (Rumsey Playfield) runs 3–10 PM** — spectator crowds spill toward East Drive near 72nd in the afternoon and evening.

## Sat–Sun afternoons — Skate Circle at Dead Road

Central Park Skate Circle sets up at **Dead Road North, 2–7 PM** both days; the crowd edges toward **West Drive near 72nd**. Minor, but expect foot traffic if you're doing an afternoon West Drive rep.

## Tue Jul 1 – Thu Jul 2 — the heat, not the calendar

The loop is otherwise clear midweek, but the temperature is the obstacle. **Tue 89°, Wed 91°, Thu 94°.** Front-load before 8 AM, carry water (the Reservoir fountains are reliable), and treat Thursday afternoon as off-limits. SummerStage has shows Wed evening (SHABAKA et al., 5 PM at Rumsey) — minor East Drive spectator traffic near 72nd after work.

## Quick recap

**Sat 6/27, 7:30–10 AM — Front Runners Pride Run 4M (Bandshell Plaza):** loop busy mid-park; run early or take the Reservoir.

**Sun 6/28, 8–11 AM — Achilles Hope & Possibility 4M (Bandshell Plaza):** large adaptive field, give room; Dreamland Pride crowds East Drive PM.

**Sat–Sun, 2–7 PM — Skate Circle (Dead Road):** light foot traffic near West Drive at 72nd.

**Tue–Thu — heat wave (89–94°):** run before 8 AM, hydrate, skip the afternoon loop.

Cleanest windows: **weekday early mornings** and **Sat before 7:30**. Stay cool out there.

See you on the loop,
The Central Park Guide
