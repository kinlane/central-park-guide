# Central Park Wellness Week: Fri Jul 3 – Thu Jul 9

Hi wellness seekers,

This is a week that rewards the early riser and the rain-walker. A dangerous heat wave makes the front half a dawn-only proposition, then a cool, wet, quiet stretch opens up the kind of solitude that's hard to find in July. Plus a genuinely restorative new option: **free morning lap swim at the Gottesman Pool.**

## Weather this week

100° today and 96° on the Fourth confine any outdoor practice to the first hour of daylight, before Sunday breaks into a cool, showery, storm-fed stretch (upper 70s–low 80s) that runs through Wednesday. Claim the dawn windows Friday–Saturday for stillness before the heat; from Sunday on, the rain empties the park and hands you the quiet contemplative hours you usually have to hunt for.

## Fri Jul 3 & Sat Jul 4 — dawn practice, then retreat

Be settled somewhere shaded and cool by sunrise: **Summit Rock, the Conservatory Garden before it opens up, Hallett Nature Sanctuary, or the north end of the Reservoir.** By 9 AM the heat is unforgiving — carry water, keep it gentle, and don't push a midday practice this weekend. The Conservatory Garden's shade and fountains are the calmest cool-down.

## New: free morning lap swim at Gottesman Pool

The **Gottesman Pool at the Davis Center (Harlem Meer)** is running free lap swim from 7 AM most mornings this week — a quiet, meditative, low-impact way to move in a heat wave, and a lovely reason to be at the peaceful north end at dawn. First-come, first-served.

## Sun Jul 5 – Wed Jul 8 — the quiet, rainy reset

The heart of the week for solitude. As the storms roll through (wettest Monday and Tuesday), the park empties out and the contemplative spaces open up: **the Ramble, North Woods, Hallett, and the Conservatory Garden** are nearly deserted between cells, the air washed and cool. Bring rain gear and treat a slow walk through the Ravine — with the Loch waterfalls running full after the rain — as a moving meditation. One heads-up for solitude-seekers: SummerStage brings crowds to Rumsey Wednesday evening, so keep to the north and west sides if you want quiet.

## Thu Jul 9 — warm, bright mornings return

Back to 88° and sunny — reclaim the dawn window before the heat rebuilds. The Gottesman Pool adds gentle learn-to-swim sessions this day if you want structured water time.

## Quick recap

**Fri & Sat — heat wave:** sunrise practice only, in shade (Summit Rock, Conservatory Garden, Hallett).

**All week — free lap swim at Gottesman Pool**, 7 AM, Davis Center (Harlem Meer).

**Sun–Wed — cool, rainy, and empty:** the week's real gift — quiet Ramble, North Woods, and Conservatory Garden between storms.

**Wed evening — SummerStage crowds at Rumsey:** stay north/west for quiet.

Breathe easy,
The Central Park Guide
