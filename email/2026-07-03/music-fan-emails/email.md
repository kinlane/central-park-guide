# Central Park Music & Performance: Fri Jul 3 – Thu Jul 9

Hi music fans,

A rich back half of the week: free classical at Naumburg, then two nights of free SummerStage at Rumsey. The front half is quieter — the heat saw to that, taking one show off the board entirely.

## Weather this week

A dangerous 100° today and 96° on the Fourth already forced one cancellation, before the week cools into a showery, storm-prone stretch (upper 70s–low 80s) from Sunday on. The outdoor shows that matter are Tuesday through Thursday, when it's cooler — keep an eye on the radar for the Tue/Wed storm chance, but these venues rarely scrub for a passing cell.

## Fri Jul 3 — Odd Mob at Rumsey is OFF (heat)

Heads-up if you had a ticket: **SummerStage cancelled tonight's Odd Mob benefit show at Rumsey Playfield**, rescheduling it to 2027 because of the extreme-heat threat. Nothing else on the music calendar tonight — sit this scorcher out.

## Sun Jul 5 — Central Park International Folkdancers, King Jagiello, 11 AM (free)

A gentler note: the **International Folkdancers** hold their weekly free gathering at King Jagiello Plaza (by Turtle Pond) at 11 AM — live folk music and open circles, worth a listen if you're near Belvedere on a cooler 85° morning.

## Tue Jul 7 — Delirium Musicum at Naumburg Bandshell, 7:30 PM (free)

The week's centerpiece for the classically inclined: **Delirium Musicum plays the Naumburg Orchestral Concerts series at the Bandshell on the Mall, 7:30 PM, free** — the oldest free outdoor classical series in the country. Bring a blanket; storms tend to clear by curtain, but check before you head over.

## Wed Jul 8 — Spoon / Ratboys / Bodega at Rumsey, 6 PM (free)

The big free SummerStage night: **Spoon headlines Rumsey Playfield at 6 PM, with Ratboys, Bodega, and host Alisa Ali** — indie-rock heavyweights on a free bill, cooler at 84°. Get there early; free SummerStage lines for a name like Spoon form well before doors.

## Thu Jul 9 — Shaggy's Yaad at Rumsey, 7 PM (free)

Closing the week loud: **"Shaggy's Yaad" — Shaggy with a deep guest list** (Robin Thicke, Tanto Metro & Devonte, Rayvon, and more) at Rumsey, 7 PM, free. A full dancehall-and-reggae bill on a warm 88° evening; arrive early for this one too.

## Quick recap

**Fri — Odd Mob cancelled** (heat, moved to 2027); nothing else tonight.

**Sun 11 AM — Folkdancers at King Jagiello Plaza (free).**

**Tue 7:30 PM — Delirium Musicum, Naumburg Bandshell (free classical).**

**Wed 6 PM — Spoon / Ratboys / Bodega, Rumsey (free).**

**Thu 7 PM — Shaggy's Yaad, Rumsey (free).**

See you at the Bandshell,
The Central Park Guide
