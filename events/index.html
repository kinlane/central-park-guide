Seasonal staples

Some things happen every year. These are some park traditions worth knowing.

Spring

  • Cherry Blossoms bloom
  • Shakespeare in the Park rehearsals
  • Central Park Film Festival (spring series)
  • Turtle Pond turtle sightings (peak basking season)
  • Migratory bird watching in the Ramble

Summer

  • SummerStage concerts
  • Shakespeare in the Park main run
  • Free yoga on the Great Lawn
  • Roller disco/skating parties near Bandshell
  • Outdoor movie nights on the Lawn

Fall

  • New York City Marathon
  • Central Park Conservancy's "Fall Foliage Walks"
  • Great Pumpkin Flotilla
  • Harvest Festival
  • Peak leaf-peeping around the Mall and Literary Walk

Winter

  • Ice skating at Wollman Rink
  • Holiday markets near Columbus Circle
  • Snow sculpture gatherings on the Great Lawn
  • Winter birding in the Ramble (owls, hawks, sparrows)
  • Horse carriage rides with seasonal lights
