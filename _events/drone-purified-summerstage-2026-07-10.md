Swiss producer and DJ **Nora En Pure** brings her **PURIFIED** series to Central Park's SummerStage on **Friday, July 10, 2026** at Rumsey Playfield — a benefit concert of melodic, deep-house sets that runs into the evening.

## Event Details

- **Event:** Nora En Pure presents PURIFIED
- **Date:** Friday, July 10, 2026
- **Time:** 4:00 PM (doors) into the evening
- **Location:** Rumsey Playfield, Central Park
- **Place:** Rumsey Playfield
- **Type:** SummerStage benefit concert
- **Cost:** Benefit (ticketed)

## About This Event

PURIFIED is Nora En Pure's touring concert brand, built around long-form melodic house sets. As a SummerStage benefit show at Rumsey Playfield, proceeds support City Parks Foundation's free programming across the city. See [cityparksfoundation.org/summerstage](https://cityparksfoundation.org/summerstage/) for tickets and set times.
