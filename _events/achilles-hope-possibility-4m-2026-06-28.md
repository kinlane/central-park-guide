Achilles International's flagship Hope & Possibility 4-miler, one of the largest inclusive races in the U.S., operated by NYRR in Central Park.

## Event Details

- **Event:** Achilles Hope & Possibility 4M
- **Date:** Sunday, June 28, 2026
- **Time:** 08:15 – 10:30
- **Location:** Naumburg Bandshell, Central Park
- **Organizer:** Achilles International / New York Road Runners (presented by TD Bank)
- **Official site:** https://www.nyrr.org/races/achilleshopepossibility4mpresentedbytdbank

## Route Impact

NYRR / Achilles International 4-mile inclusive race, start 8:15 AM Sunday, with handcyclists, wounded veterans, and athletes of all abilities on the drives. The loop carries the field roughly 7:30–10:30 AM; run before 7:30 or after 11, or use the Bridle Path / Reservoir.
