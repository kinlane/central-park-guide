## 29th Annual Central Park Holiday Lighting

Annual holiday celebration featuring meeting Santa and seasonal characters, group carol singing on the plaza, warm beverages including hot cocoa, and various seasonal activities. Free coloring pages, carol songbooks, and holiday recipes available for download.
