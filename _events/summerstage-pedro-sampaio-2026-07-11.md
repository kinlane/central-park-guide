Brazilian DJ, singer, and producer **Pedro Sampaio** — one of the biggest names in **funk carioca** — headlines a free SummerStage concert at Rumsey Playfield on **Saturday, July 11, 2026**, a high-energy Brazilian festival afternoon in Central Park.

## Event Details

- **Event:** Pedro Sampaio
- **Date:** Saturday, July 11, 2026
- **Time:** 4:00 PM
- **Location:** Rumsey Playfield, Central Park
- **Place:** Rumsey Playfield
- **Type:** SummerStage concert (free)
- **Cost:** Free

## About This Event

Part of City Parks Foundation's free SummerStage series, this Brazilian festival concert spotlights funk carioca — the Rio-born dance-music movement — with Pedro Sampaio leading the bill. Free and open to the public; arrive early, as popular SummerStage shows reach capacity. See [cityparksfoundation.org/summerstage](https://cityparksfoundation.org/summerstage/) for set times.
