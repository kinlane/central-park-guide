## RBC Race for the Kids 4M and Health Walk

This annual charity race features a 4-mile run through Central Park to raise money to help change the lives of young people. Includes a complimentary 1-mile health walk accessible to all registrants. Proceeds benefit MSK Kids, the pediatric program at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center.
