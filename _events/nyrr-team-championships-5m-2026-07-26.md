New York Road Runners' annual club Team Championships, a five-mile race in Central Park worth double points in the NYRR Club Points Series.

## Event Details

- **Event:** NYRR Team Championships 5M
- **Date:** Sunday, July 26, 2026
- **Time:** 07:30 – 10:00
- **Location:** 102nd Street Cross Drive, Central Park
- **Organizer:** New York Road Runners
- **Official site:** https://events.nyrr.org/nyrr-team-championships-5m

## Route Impact

NYRR club-team 5-mile championship on the park drives, first wave 7:30 AM Sunday with staggered men's, women's, and non-binary starts through mid-morning. Start and finish are on the 102nd Street Cross Drive, heading west onto West Drive and running the lower loop. Expect the full loop to carry race traffic roughly 7–10 AM, with staging and breakdown on the north end from 6 AM. Run or ride before 6:30 or after 10:30, or use the Bridle Path / Reservoir.
