Annual fundraiser for GMHC (Gay Men's Health Crisis). Opening ceremony at the Naumburg Bandshell on the Mall, followed by a 6.2-mile route through Central Park. Finish-line festival on the Mall through 12:30 PM.

## Event Details

- **Event:** AIDS Walk New York
- **Date:** Sunday, May 17, 2026
- **Opening Ceremony:** 09:15
- **Time:** 10:00 – 12:30
- **Location:** Naumburg Bandshell, Central Park
- **Organizer:** GMHC
- **Official site:** https://aidswalkny.org/day-of-event/

## Route Impact

Opening ceremony at Naumburg Bandshell 9:15 AM, 6.2-mile walk through Central Park starting 10 AM. Crowds enter from CPW, Fifth Ave, and both transverses. East Drive in the 60s–70s is unusable for running roughly 9 AM – 12:30 PM.
