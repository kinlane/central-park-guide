## Before, there was Seneca Village! (2025)

Commemorative performance bringing 1845 Seneca Village to life through interpretive music, dance, and spoken word. Explored how Andrew Williams and others' 1825 land purchases developed into a thriving community where Black New Yorkers built their own homes, churches, and a school.
