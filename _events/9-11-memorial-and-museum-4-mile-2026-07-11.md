New York Road Runners produces the **9/11 Memorial & Museum 4-Mile**, presented by RXR, on **Saturday, July 11, 2026 at 7:30 AM** in Central Park — two months before the 25th anniversary of the September 11 attacks. About **5,000 runners** are registered (the field is sold out). Proceeds support the 9/11 Memorial & Museum, helping keep the Memorial free and expanding the Museum's educational programs.

## Event Details

- **Event:** 9/11 Memorial & Museum 4-Mile (presented by RXR)
- **Date:** Saturday, July 11, 2026
- **Time:** 7:30 AM start (field clears by roughly 9:30–10:00 AM)
- **Distance:** 4 miles on the Central Park loop
- **Staging:** West Drive, West 60s–70s
- **Participants:** ~5,000 (sold out)
- **Beneficiary:** 9/11 Memorial & Museum
- **Register / info:** [nyrr.org/911memorialandmuseum](https://www.nyrr.org/911memorialandmuseum)

## Loop impact

The lower loop is given over to the race and its staging from around 7:30 AM until the field clears. West Drive and the lower East Drive are effectively unusable for other runners and for cyclists during that window, with the heaviest runner and spectator crowding at the West 60s–70s staging area before and after the start. Plan cool-downs, rides, and walks for the north end, the Bridle Path, or the Reservoir until mid-morning.
