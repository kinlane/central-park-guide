## CityParks Seniors Fitness: Free Tennis Lessons

City Parks Foundation invites all senior citizens to join their spring fitness program, which aims to promote wellness and fitness among New Yorkers. The program offers complimentary tennis instruction in Central Park as part of a broader initiative providing Yoga, fitness walking, pickleball, and Tai Chi classes across NYC parks.
