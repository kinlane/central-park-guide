## Free Shakespeare in the Park 2026

Free Shakespeare in the Park returns to the newly renovated Delacorte Theater in 2026 with two productions: Romeo and Juliet (directed by Saheem Ali, May 22 - June 28, starring Ra'Mya Latiah Aikens and Daniel Bravo Hernandez) and The Winter's Tale (directed by Tony winner Daniel Sullivan, dates TBA). A Public Works production and Public Forum series revival round out the lineup.
