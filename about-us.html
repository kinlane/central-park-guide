About us

The path we're on

CentralPark.Guide is the go-to source for what's happening in Central Park. From the big things you plan around to the little surprises you'd otherwise miss.

See what's happening

Born from the patrol

It all started with Poppy. Every day, she patrols the park from her sidecar. Nose up, ears back, spotting squirrels, shortcuts, and surprises.

Now, the stories and sightings from her patrol feed into something bigger: a guide that helps everyone enjoy the park.

Meet Poppy & the patrol squad

A community guide, not just a calendar

This isn't an official bulletin board. It's powered by the people who use the park every day.

  • Runners logging detours or major races
  • Families sharing festivals
  • Musicians posting gigs
  • Neighbors tipping each other off

When you add to it, the whole community wins.

The movement we're starting
